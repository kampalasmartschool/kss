WEATHER IN OUR DISTRICT.

a) Weather.

This is the condition of the atmosphere of a given place at a given time.

Or: 

Weather is the atmospheric condition of a place at a given time.

b) Weather forecasting:

This is telling of future weather changes.

c) Meteorology:

This is the study of weather.

Or: 

The studying of weather changes and conditions.

d) Meteorologists:
These are people that study weather conditions.

e) Meteorological station:

A meteorological station is a place where weather conditions are studied.

Uganda’s meteorological station is located at Entebbe of Wakiso District.
