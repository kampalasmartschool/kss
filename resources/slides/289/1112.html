THE RAIN CYCLE.

This is the continuous process through which rain is formed.  The cycle is different when it comes to the different types of rain (fall).

Types of rainfall.

Convectional rainfall.

This is the type of rainfall received in areas with lakes, water bodies and forests. 

Convectional rains usually fall in afternoons. 

It’s received in districts like Kampala, Wakiso, Kalangala and others.
