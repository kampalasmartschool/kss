Air Pressure.

It is also called atmospheric pressure. Air pressure is measured in Millibars (mb).

Areas with high air pressure experience dry conditions while those with low pressure with rainfall. 

The instrument used to measure air pressure is called a barometer.

Humidity.

This refers to the amount of water vapour in the atmosphere.

Humidity is measured by a hygrometer.
