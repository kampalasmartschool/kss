Cyclonic rainfall

This type of rainfall is experienced when warm moist air masses meet cold dry masses rise, condense and form rain.

Warm air rises because it is lighter than cold air. 

It’s mainly received in flat areas like Karamoja and Teso.

Cyclonic rainfall is also called Frontal rainfall.

 

  • A place where the two wind masses meet is the front/ a doldrum.
  • Hence it being called the frontal rainfall.
  • It’s received in districts like Abiro, Kotido, Kaabong and Bukedea.
