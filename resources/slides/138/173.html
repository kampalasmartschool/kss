Forming plurals of nouns by adding - ’s’

Examples

chair                 pencil                rubber               broom

school               table                 basin                seed

book                 chart                 chalkboard        umbrellas

 

Practice

  1. Read the words above.
  2. Spelling the words above

 

Activity

Write the plurals of the words in the brackets:

  1. Janet has two _________ (rubber)
  2. There are many __________ in our classroom. (table)
  3. The ____________ are on the shelves. (book)
  4. The teacher made many _________ (chart) for our class.
  5. The children brought many ________ at school on a rainy day. (umbrella)
