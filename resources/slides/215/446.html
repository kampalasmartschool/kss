Topic: Measures

Weight (mass)

  1. What is weight?

a) Weight is how heavy or light something is

b) We can tell how heavy or light something is after weighing it

  1. We can weigh some objects using nonstandard tools eg. tins, baskets, pots etc
  2. We measure mass (weight) in kilograms (kg) and grams (g).
  3. Examples of things we weigh. 

 

  • Sugar
  • Peas
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Millet
  • Maize flour
  • Bread
  • Beans
  • Rice
  • Cassava flour
