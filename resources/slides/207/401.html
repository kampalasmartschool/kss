History of money

Long ago, people used to exchange goods for goods and services for services (barter trade). Later, they introduced cowrie shells.

When the Indians came, they introduced rupees. The rupees also got expired and now we have the present currency called shillings.

Features on money

  1. a coin of 50 shillings has a head of a cob and the coat of arms
  2. a coin of 100 shillings – a cow and a coat of arms
  3. a coin of 200 shillings – a fish
  4. a coin of 500 shillings – a head of a crested crane
  5. a coin of 1000 shillings – a crested crane
