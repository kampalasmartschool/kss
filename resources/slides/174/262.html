Activity

Fill in the gap with a correct word from the bracket.

  1. A _____________________ of thieves broke into our house. (gang, collection)
  2. A band of ________________________ was marching along the street. (singers, musicians)
  3. Children like playing where there is a _______________________ of sand. (set, heap)
  4. A ______________________ of wolves chased an antelope. (pack, pride)
  5. We have a large herd of _________________________. (cows, cattle)
