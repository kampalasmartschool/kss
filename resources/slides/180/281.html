Verbs that double the last letter before adding ‘ed’

Example

Mop   –        mopped

Scrub          –        scrubbed

Nod    –       nodded

Verbs that end with ‘y’ we remove ‘y’ and add ‘ied’

Examples

Bury – buried

Carry – carried

Hurry – hurried
