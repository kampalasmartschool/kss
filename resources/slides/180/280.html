Past simple tense

Verbs that add letter ‘d’

Example

Move  –       moved

Wave –       waved

Dance –       danced

Agree -        agreed

Care   -        cared

Arrive          -        arrived

Behave         -        behaved

Verbs that add ‘ed’

Example

Cook   –        cooked

Kick   –        kicked

Play    –        played

Add    -        added
