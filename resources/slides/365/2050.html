POEM

Study the poem below and answer questions about it correctly.

 

Languages in Uganda and in the world,

From East to West,

North to South,

People speak different languages

Because a language is a gift from God.

 

In the East,

The Bagishu speak Lumasaba

In the West,

People speak Runyakitara,

In the North,

People speak Luo,

In South Africa,

People speak Zulu,

People indeed like their languages,

Because a language is a gift from God.

 

In England, people speak English,

Come to France, people speak French,

In Germany, people speak German,

Go to China, people speak Chinese

Visit Congo, people speak Lingala,

What about in Rwanda, people speak Kinyarwanda.

Fly to Rome, people speak Latin.

Indeed people love their languages

Because a language is a gift from God.
