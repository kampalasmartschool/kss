Plants.

Plants  are  anything growing on earth’s  surface.

Examples of plants.

  • Maize plants.
  • Bean plant.
  • Cow pea plant.

Reasons why they are called plants.

  • They make their own food.
  • They have chlorophyll.

GROUPS  OF  PLANTS.

  1.  Flowering plants

Are plants which bear flowers e.g maize, beans, grass, tomatoes, peas, etc.

  1. Non-flowering plants.

Are  plants  which  do not  bear  flowers  e.g ferns,  conifers, mosses, liverworts.

Characteristics  of  plants.

They  grow, reproduce,  feed,  breathe, excrete.
