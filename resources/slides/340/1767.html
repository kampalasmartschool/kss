PESTS  AND  DISEASES.

A pests is an  animal  which  destroys  farmers’  crops.

Examples

  • Monkey.

  • Rats.

​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

  • Weevils.
  • Goat.
  • Birds.
  • Cows.

Pests  control  measures.

  • Weeding.
  • Crop rotation.
  • Using  scare  crows.
  • Spraying  using  pesticides.
  • Row  planting.
  • Early  planting.

Note: Crop rotation , early planting and proper spacing are natural methods of controlling pests.

  • Removing  infected   parts  from the  plant.
  • Uprooting  the  plants  which   are severely   attacked.

Crop  diseases.

  • Mosaic.
  • potato  blight.
  • rust 
  • blast 
  • panama smut   and  rot
