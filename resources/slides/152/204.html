Adjectives:

Adjectives are words which tell us more about nouns.

Examples:

fat                    thin                   ugly                  big

happy               clean                good                 early

sad                   small                easy                 rich

weak                dry                    short                 tall

strong               lazy                  kind                  quick

dull                   dirty                  slow

Practice

  • Read the adjectives
  • Construct oral sentences using the adjectives above
  • Spelling the adjectives
