Completing using the correct adjectives

  1. Who is the ________________ boy in this school? (dirty)
  2. Lucky is ___________________than Adella. (ugly)
  3. Is the ground _____________________? (dry)
  4. You are _____________________ today than yesterday. (happy)
  5. I was very ________________________ to school today. (early)
