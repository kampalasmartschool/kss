Articles ‘a” and ‘an’

We use an with words that begin with a vowel sound

We use a with words that start with a consonant sound

Examples

An elephant

An owl

An apple

A bag

A book

A broom

Activity

Fill in the gaps with a or an to complete the sentences correctly.

  1. Rose is eating ______________ yam.
  2. Our hen has laid ____________ egg.
  3. _____________ fish lives in water.
  4. I have _____________ mango in my bag.
  5. ______________ orange is sour.
