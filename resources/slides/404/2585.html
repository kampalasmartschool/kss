                         Games and sports

Children play many times when they are at school. They sometimes play the same games during break time, lunch and on their way to school back home.

Children run short distances. They jump and skip ropes. They throw and kick balls in the field. They also enjoy hide and seek on the school compound.

For all the games, there are losers and winners. At times, there is clapping and shouting for the winners. There can be crying and fighting for losers but this is not good at all.

Some games are more interesting at school where there is a referee for football, an umpire for netball and coaches to train players. In school where there is good support, the players are usually strong and happy. They always beat other teams.
