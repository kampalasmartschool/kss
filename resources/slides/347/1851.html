POSTURE.

Posture means the position of the body for everything we do.

There is correct posture for sitting, standing, walking, running and sleeping.

IMPORTANCE OF GOOD POSTURE:

  1. We look smart.
  2. Muscles of the abdomen and diaphragm become strong.
  3. Good posture keeps the organs of the intestines in proper position.
  4. Good posture helps the skeleton to develop in the right way.
  5. Bad posture leads to indigestion and deformities of the skeleton.
