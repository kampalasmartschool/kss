            Inter House Music, Dance and Drama Festival in my School

Have you ever attended a Music, Dance and Drama festival? Have you ever taken part in one? Well, I have.

In my school, we had a music festival in second term. We spent a lot of time practising. We learnt and rehearsed poems, dances and plays. A few days before the final concert, we had a dress rehearsal. This is when performances were done exactly as they will be done on the big day.

 

On that day, the whole school assembled in the new main hall. Official called adjudicators arrived from the district. There were four houses in the competition. Each house recited a poem and performed traditional dance, a folk song and acted a play. Everybody did their best. At the end of the day, the adjudicators announced the results. They gave out three trophies to the best house for reciting a poem, a dance and acting the best play. We all enjoyed the festival.
