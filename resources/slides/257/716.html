CHILDHOOD IMMUNISABLE DISEASES.

What is immunization?

Immunization is the giving of vaccine to a person to protect or prevent a certain disease

Examples of immunisable diseases.

Measles

Tetanus

Tuberculosis

Diphtheria

Polio

Whooping cough

 

Other immunisable diseases are:-

  • Typhoid
  • Pneumonia
  • Hepatitis  B
  • Hepatitis
  • influenza
