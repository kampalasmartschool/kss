SIGNS AND SYMPTOMS OF IMMUNISABLE DISEASES.

Tetanus   

  • Stiff muscles all over the body.
  • Running nose and cough.

Whooping cough

  • Running nose
  • Cough

Measles

  • Red eyes
  • Sore mouth
  • Running nose

Tuberculosis                         

  • Cough that lasts long
  • Running nose
  • Hard to breath

Polio

  • Lame legs or hands become thin.

Diphtheria

  • Swollen neck
  • Sore throat
  • Finds it hard to breath.

Importance of immunization.

 

  • It reduces on infant mortality rate
  • It promotes good health
  • It controls childhood diseases
