SENSES AND SENSE ORGANS.

We have five senses and these are:-

  1. Seeing.
  2. Hearing.
  3. Smelling.
  4. Tasting.
  5. Feeling.

We use eyes to see.

We use ears to hear.

We use the nose to smell.

We use the tongue to taste.

We use the skin to feel.
