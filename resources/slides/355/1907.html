Experiment to find out drainage of water through the types of soil.

Equal volume of measured water was poured in each funnel.  After sometime, water was collected in each jar.

Observation:

More water was collected in a jar containing sand soil followed by the jar containing loam soil and least water in the jar containing clay soil.

Conclusion:

Sand soil is more permeable than the loam and clay soil.

 

SOIL CONSERVATION.

It is the practice of keeping soil in one place and fertile.

If soil is not looked after property it can lead to soil exhaustion

Soil exhaustion is the loss of soil fertility.

Causes of soil exhaustion.

  1. Soil erosion
  2. Leaching

Leaching is the washing away of soil nutrients from the top layer to the lower layer.

Causes of leaching.

      a) Soil erosion.

      b) Deep ploughing.
