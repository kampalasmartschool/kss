SOIL PROFILE.

Soil profile is the vertical arrangement of soil layers. It is made up of three layers namely;

  • Top soil.
  • Sub soil.
  • Parent rocks.

Top soil: This is the most important one because it contains most of the nutrients needed for plant growth.

Which soil layer is suitable for plant growth?

The top layer is the best for plant growth. This is because it contains humus.
