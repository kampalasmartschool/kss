SOIL EROSION.

Soil erosion the washing away of top soil.

Agents of soil erosion.

An agent of soil erosion is the factor that helps soil erosion to take place.

There are many agents of soil erosion. They include:

  1. Wind
  2. Running water.
  3. Man.
  4. Animals.

CAUSES OF SOIL EROSION.

i. Deforestation.

This is the massive cutting down of trees.

ii. Overgrazing.

It is the keeping of animals on the small piece of land for a long time.

iii. Over cultivation.

This is the growing of crops on a piece of land year after year without leaving the land to rest.

iv. Bush burning

It is the removing of plant cover using fire and leave the land bare.

v. Mono cropping

This is the growing of one type of crop repeatedly on the same piece of land

vi. Poor farming methods.

These include:

  • Ploughing along the slopes.
  • Planting crops along the slopes.
