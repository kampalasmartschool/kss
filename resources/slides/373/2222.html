Analogies are words that show similarity in comparison to the given set of words

Lower class work; Review occupation, opposite, homes, young ones and sounds of creatures

Examples

Little is to big as dwarf is to giant.

A spider is to fly as cat is to rats.

Sheep is to mutton as pig is pork.

Bray is to donkey as neigh is to horse.

Finger is to hand as toe is to foot.

North is to south as East is to West.

Tears is to sorrow as smile is joy

Cat is to kitten as dog is to puppy.

Feathers are to birds as scales are to fish.

Flock is to sheep as herd is to cattle.

Eat is to ate as go is to went.

Food is to hunger as drink is to thirst.

Knives are to cut as guns are to shoot.
