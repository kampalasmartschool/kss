QUESTION TAGS- FUTURE CONTINUOUS TENSE

Examples

  1.  We shall be singing nicely, shan’t we?
  2. They won’t be listen to you, will they?
  3. We shan’t be missing lunch, shall we?
  4. I shall be fishing from the pond, shan’t I?
