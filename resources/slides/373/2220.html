AFFIRMATIVE, NEGATIVE AND INTERROGATIVE

(Future continuous tense)

Examples

1. A – They will be sowing the beans.

N – They won’t be sowing the beans.

I – Will they be sowing the beans?

2. A - We shall be coming early next week.

N – We shan’t be coming early next week.

I -Shall we be coming early next week?
