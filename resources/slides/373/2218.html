ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE (FUTURE SIMPLE TENSE)

1. Jane will take the ball (active)

The ball will be taken by Jane.

2. Stella will a letter write to me.

A letter will be written to me by Stella.

3. The timekeeper will ring the bell

The bell will be rung by the time keeper.
