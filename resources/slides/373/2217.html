QUESTIONS TAGS (FUTURE SIMPLE TENSE)

Examples

  1. They won’t visit us today, will they?
  2. I shall manage that work, shan’t I?
  3. Paul won’t see the doctor, will he?
  4. Mother will take care of the baby, won’t she?
