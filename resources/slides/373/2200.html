PAST CONTINUOUS TENSE

Lower class work: Review present continuous tense.

This tense is used to show that two events are or were taking place at the same time.

Examples

  1. I was eating my food when the bell rang. (to ring)
  2. The boy was crying when we entered the house. (cry)
  3. We were singing when the guest arrived. (sing)
