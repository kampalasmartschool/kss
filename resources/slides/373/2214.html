ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE OF PAST PERFECT TENSE

The main verb of the past perfect tense in the passive voice is written in the past participle.

Examples

1.  A – Sheila had stolen the money.

P – The money had been stolen by Sheila.

2. A - Onyango had drawn the map.

P - The map had been drawn by Onyango.
