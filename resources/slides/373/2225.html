DIALOGUE

Read the conversation below and answer the questions in full sentences.

Tim:      Dora, what is that you are going to bake?

Dora:    I am going to bake bread.

Tim:      Bread! Can someone bake bread at home?

Dora:    Yes, you can make your own bread at hoe.

Tim:      Sure! How?

Dora:    Just get all the necessary ingredients such as yeast, sugar, salt, butter, flour, food colour and eggs.

Tim:      How do you know which ingredient to put first?

Dora:    there is a recipe to follow. A recipe is a guide to follow on how to bake something.

            It shows you the right amount of each ingredient to put.

Tim:      Now, what is the recipe for making bread?

Dora:    Get three teaspoonfuls of yeast, lukewarm water, three spoonfuls of sugar and one teaspoonful of salt.

Tim:      That sounds very difficult. I need to do it practically.

Dora:    Alright! Here are the ingredients. Let’s do it practically.

Tim:      Thank you Dora.
