QUESTION TAGS (PAST CONTINUOUS TENSE)

Examples

  1. She was peeling matooke, wasn’t she?
  2. They were not sleeping, were they?
  3. Sarah and Ann were dancing, weren’t they?
  4. The dog was sleeping, wasn’t it?
