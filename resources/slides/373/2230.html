MY FIRST TIME AT THE GROCERY

Buying and selling

My first time at the grocery

Mother sent me to the shop

She gave me some money to spend

In the shop I asked

How much does tea and coffee cost?

 

The grocer asked whether

I had the money to pay

I looked for the coins in my pocket

I put the money on the counter

 

The grocer asked for the money again

I gave her the money I had

She waved bye to me

Come back another time.
