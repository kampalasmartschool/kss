Study the dialogue and answer questions that follow in full sentences.

 

Maria:   I am tired of reading. Shana let’s go for a walk.

Shana:  I am tired of writing too. Where shall we go then?

Maria:   Let’s go and look at McDonald’s fence.

Shana:  Why is he making the fence?

Maria:   He wants to protect his young trees from cows and goats.

Shana:  Before we go, what do you want to be after your studies, Maria?

Maria:   I hope to be a nurse because I am good at sciences.

Shana:  Who teaches you science in Primary Four?

Maria:   We have two teachers. Mr. Katana and Mr. Yuma.

Shana:  Well, I intend to be a reverend because the Bible is my best book.

Maria:   No wonder! You are so kind like Jesus.

Shana:  We are just three students in class who want to be reverends.
