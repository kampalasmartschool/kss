Comprehension – road signs

Road signs guide us on the road.

We cross the road at the zebra crossing.

Traffic lights also help us on the road.

The red light tells the driver to stop.

The orange light tells the driver to get ready and the green light tells the driver to go.

People who walk along the road are called Pedestrians.
