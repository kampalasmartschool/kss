Importances of pollination.

  • It allows fertilization to take place in crops.
  • Leads to high yields in farmers harvest.

Uses of flowers.

  • For decoration  on various functions.
  • Making of insecticide.
  • Perfume making.
  • Used to get dyes.
  • Sources of income by growing / selling.
  • Sign of love.
  • Sign of respect for the dead.

FERTILISATION.

The union of a male and female gamates to form a zygote.

After pollination, pollen tubes develop reaching doen the ovule.

After fertlisation in the ovary, ovules become seeds and an ovary develops into a fruit.
