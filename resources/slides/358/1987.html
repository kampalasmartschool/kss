ROOT SYSTEMS.

These are two main root systems.

  1. Fibrous root system.
  2. Tap root system.

Types of roots.

i. Prop roots.

These give extra support to a plant such plants include;- maze, sorghum, etc.

ii. Buttress roots.

iii. Clasping roots.

iv. Stilt roots.

v. Breathing roots.

vi. Storage roots e.g. carrots, cassava, sweet potatoes etc.
