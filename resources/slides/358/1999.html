PLANT PROPAGATION.

Plant propagation refers methods used in growing crops.

There are two basic methods of propagation.

i) Seed propagation.

ii) Vegetative propagation.

SEED PROPAGATION.

Most flowering plants are propagated by means of seeds e.g. beans, maize, coffee, mango etc.

i. Vegetative propagation.

In this method a part of a parent plant is planted to give rise to a new plant.

Parts if plants used in vegetative propagation are:-

  • Stems
  • Leaves
  • Buds

Types of vegetative propagation.

  • Natural vegetative propagation.
  • Artificial vegetative propagation.
  • Natural vegetative propagation.
