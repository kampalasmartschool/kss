REPRODUCTION IN FLOWERING PLANTS.

There are two types of reproduction in plants.

  • A sexual reproduction (Vegetative propagation)
  • Sexual reproduction.

THE FLOWER.

The structure of a flower.

POLLINATION.

The transfer of pollen grains from anthers to the stigma.

Types of pollination.

  • Cross pollination.
  • Self-pollination.

SELF POLLINATION .

The transfer of pollen grains from anthers to the stigma of the same flower.

Self pollination occurs when the anthers are taller than the stigma.

Agents of pollination.

  • Animals.
  • Wind.
  • Water.
