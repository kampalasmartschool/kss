STEMS.

Functions of the stem to plants.

  • They hold leaves and branches in position to get sunlight.
  • Hold the flower for pollination.
  • Help in transpiration.
  • Some stems make food for the plant.

Functions to man.

  • Some are sources of food.
  • Some are sources of income.
  • Some provide herbal medicine.
  • Some are used for propagation.
  • Some provide timber.

Types of stems.

  • Upright stems.
  • Climbing / creeping stems.
  • Underground stems.

Climbing stems.

Reasons why pants climb others.

  • To get support.
  • To obtain sunlight.

Ways how plants climb others.

  • Use of tendrils.
  • Use of hooks.
  • By twinning or clasping.

Tendrils.

Plant stem propagations.

This is the way plants can be grown using stems.

Examples.

  • Bulbs.
  • Rhizomes.
  • Corns.
  • Stem tubers.
  • Suckers.
