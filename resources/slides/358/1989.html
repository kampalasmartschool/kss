Functions of leaves to both plants and man (animals).

To plants.

  • Leaves help plants to make their food through photosynthesis.
  • Leaves help plants in respiration through the stomata.
  • Leaves help plants in transpiration.
  • Some plants store their food in leaves.

To man and animals.

  • Leaves produce oxygen for breathing during their repiration.
  • Leaves can be used as herbal medicine.
  • Some leaves are eaten by both man and animals.
