FRUITS.

A fruit is a developed ovary containing seeds.

Fruits have two scars.

  • Style scar.
  • Stalk scar.

Fruits protect seeds and assist in dispersal.

Types of fruits.

  • Succulent fruits.
  • Dry fruits.

SUCCULENT FRUITS / JUICY FRUITS.

These are fruits whose pericarp and mesocarp become juicy and fleshy and can be eaten.

They are divided into three groups.

  • Berries.
  • Drupes.
  • Pomes.

i. Berries.

These are fruits with many seeds. The soft pericarp is divided into three layers.

  • Epicarp
  • Mesocarp
  • Endocarp

Examples of berries.

  • Oranges.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Pawpaw.

Diagram of an orange fruit.

ii. Drupes.

These are fruits with one seed inside a hard endocarp. Drupes have three layers;

  • Epicarp
  • Mesocarp
  • Endocarp

Examples of drupes.

  • Mangoes.
  • Ovacadoes.
  • Coconuts.
  • Cashew nuts.
