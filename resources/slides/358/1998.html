TROPISM.

Tropism is growth movement of plants in response to a stimulus.

A stimulus is any change in the environment which the plant is sensitive to.

KINDS OF TROPISM.

i. Phototropism.

This is growth movement of plants towards light eg when a plant is placed in the dark box with a small opening towards the plant tends to grow.

ii. Geotropism.

This is a growth movement of a plant towards the direct of force of gravity plant root grow wards due to force of gravity.

iii. Hydrotropism.

This is a growth movement of some parts of certain plant in response to touch one side. This stimulus helps twinning plants such as beans, passion fruits and yams climbs by use of tendrils , hooks.

iv. Chemotropism.

This is the growth movement of plant parts towards the source of chemical eg pollen tubes grow the style to reach the ovules.
