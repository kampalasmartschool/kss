SEEDS.

A seed is a fertilized ovule that develops into a new plant.

Classes of seeds.

  • Monocotyledonous seeds.
  • Dicotyledonous seeds.

Monocotyledonous seeds.

These have one cotyledon.

 Examples monocotyledonous seeds.

  • Rice.
  • Maize.
  • Sorghum.
  • Millet.

Dicotyledonous seeds.

These are seeds with two cotyledons.

Examples Dicotyledonous seeds.

  • Beans.
  • Peas.
