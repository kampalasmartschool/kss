Using: Hardly, barely/ scarcely

These words are negative and they go with ‘when’

Examples

  1. The boss paid for his lunch. The driver came for him.

Hardly had the boss paid for his lunch when the driver came for him.

Barely had the boss paid for his lunch when the driver came for him.

Scarcely had the boss paid for his lunch when the driver came for him.
