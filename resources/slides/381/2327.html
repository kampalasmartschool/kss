ACTIVITY

Complete the sentences with the correct form of the word in the brackets.

  1. The waiter ________________ the cups every morning. (wash)

  4. The waitress is __________________ the table for lunch. (lay)
  5. The cashier gave me a __________________ after receiving the money. (receive)
  6. Who ____________________ the customers tomorrow. (to serve)
  7. Has anybody _________________ your order, please? (take)
  8. The old man ___________________ for chips and chicken yesterday? (order)
  9. Having ___________________ for the meals, I was served by the waiter. (pay)
