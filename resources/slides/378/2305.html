Use of…….either….or…….

Either …or… is used in a sentence in the affirmative sense when referring to a choice between two possibilities.

Example

1. We can eat now. We can eat after the show.

We can either eat now or after the show.

2. Peter will bring my books. Paul will bring my books.

Either Peter or Paul will bring my books.

3. I shall drink coca cola. I shall drink juice.

I shall drink either coca cola or juice.
