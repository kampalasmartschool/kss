……….prefer ………to………

We use prefer to to say we like one thing or activity more than another. We can use a prepositional phrase with to when we compare two things or actions.

Examples

1. Robert likes knitting. He likes sewing more.

      Robert prefers sewing to knitting

2. Elijah likes designing. He likes hemming more.

      Elijah prefers hemming to designing
