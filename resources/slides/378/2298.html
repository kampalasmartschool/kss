At Kineme shopping centre, there works a seamstress called Nampa. She is such a skilled seamstress that she designs and sews ladies’ and men’s clothes in all fashions.

Sometimes, she sews wedding and graduation gowns with attractive designs. All her garments are purchased at affordable prices despite the fact that they are nice looking.

 

Her eldest daughter, Nvanungi is a designer. She knits school sweaters and weaves nice table cloths as well as carpets. She has a big stall in the African Heritage Village in Kampala city. Nvanungi also curves beautiful sculptures. Her sculptures are mostly bought by foreign tourists from USA, Sweden, China and France because they are decorated with attractive designs.

 

Nampa and her daughter earn a lot of money daily from both local and foreign customers. This has enabled them to expand their business and train their siblings as well. In addition, Nampa and Nvanungi usually exhibit their products at the Uganda Manufacturers Association ground at Lugogo. This has not only helped them to advertise their products but also to widen their market.
