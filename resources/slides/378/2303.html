Using….so as……

Examples

1. He wanted to buy a new book. He went to the bookshop.

He went to the bookshop so as to buy a new book.

2. Mother saved some money. Her intension was to buy a car.

Her mother saved some money so as to buy a car.

3. He revises very hard. His aim is to pass well.

He revises very hard so as to pass well.
