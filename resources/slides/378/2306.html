Use of …...neither……nor…

Neither ….nor… is used in a sentence in the negative sense when you want to say that two or more things are not true.

Examples

1. Peter won’t bring my books. Simon won’t bring my books.

Neither Peter nor Simon will bring my books.

2. I shall not drink beer. I shall not drink cola.

I shall drink neither beer nor cola
