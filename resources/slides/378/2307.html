Use of……..and neither……….

Examples

1. I cannot g back there. You cannot o back there.

    I cannot go back there and neither can you.

2. Janet does not eat pork. Janet doesn’t eat mutton.

    Janet does not eat pork and neither does she eat mutton

3. Musa is not short. Mate is not short.

   Musa is not short and neither is Matt.
