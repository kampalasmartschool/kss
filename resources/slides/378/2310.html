The use of …….as soon as……../ ………..immediately……… Immediately ……../ As soon as………

Examples

1. The seamstress finished sewing the dress. She ironed it.

  • The seamstress ironed the dress as soon as she finished sewing it.
  • As soon as the seamstress finished sewing the dress, she ironed it.
  • Immediately the seamstress finished sewing the dress, she ironed it.
  • The seamstress ironed the dress as soon as she finished sewing it.
