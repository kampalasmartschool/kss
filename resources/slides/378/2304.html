Use of……..so………..that……

Examples

a) He washes cars. He wants to get school fees.

    He washes cars so that he can get school fees.

b) He went to town. He wanted to buy a radio.

     He went to town so that he could buy a radio

 

Use of ………..in order to………

Examples

a) He works hard. He wants to live a happy life in future.

    He works hard in order to live a happy life in future.

b)I work up very early. I wanted to catch the morning bus.

    I woke up early in order to catch the morning bus.
