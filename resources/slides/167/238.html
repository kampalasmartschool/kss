Interrogative pronouns / questioning words

Examples:

  1. What is your name?
  2. How old are you?
  3. Where do you live?
  4. Whose book is that?

Every asking sentence must end with a question mark (?)

Words used to begin questions. What, How, Where, Who, Whose, Which, Do, Can, When, Is, Are
