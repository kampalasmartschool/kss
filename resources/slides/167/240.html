Prepositions of verbs

Examples

at, after, over,   from,    against,   in,    next to,  of,  to

Example

Fill in the gaps to complete the sentences correctly.

  1. Kato is running after the ball.
  2. He was leaning against the table.
  3. Grace is suffering from headache.
  4. The children are interested in the story.
