Prepositions:

Prepositions are words used to show place or position of something.

Preposition of place.

in,         on,        under,   over,     between,           near,     across,    along,   behind,    infront of,        at

Example

  1. The pot is in the house.
  2. The ball is under the chair.

Activity

Fill in the correct preposition given in the box.

  1. The ball is ______________ the table.
  2. The house is _____________ the trees.
  3. The bottle is _______________ the pot.
  4. The tree is _______________ the house.
  5. The plane is flying _____________ the hills.
