.Word problem involving division of numbers by 2.

Example 1

Share 6 mangoes between 2 girls. How many does each get?

          6 ÷ 2 = 3 mangoes

Activity

a)      Ten divided by 2 equals.

b)     Share 16 sweets equally between 2 boys.

c)     Daddy had 8 bananas. He shared them between 2 children. How many bananas did each child              get?

d)   Mary had 14 oranges. She shared them among her two friends. How many oranges did each                 friend  get?

e)   Mother bought 6 pencils. She shared them among her two boys. How many pencils did each boy           get?
