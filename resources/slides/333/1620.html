2. Wild animals.

What are wild animals?

Wild animals are animals that live in the bush, forest or water.  (wilderness).

 Examples.

Zebra,  rat, snake, fish, lion, rhino, crocodile, elephant, giraffe, monkey, fox, leopard, tiger          

Most wild animals are dangerous to us.

Snake

Leopard

Uses of wild animals.

  • They give us food e.g.  meat
  • They give us skin e.g. snake, leopard
  • They give us ivory (elephant)
  • They attract the tourists and they give us money.
  • They give us horns.
