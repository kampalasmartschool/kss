Dangers of wild animals.

  • Wild animals are dangerous to us and to domestic animals. 
  • They destroy our crops. 
  • Some of them kill people.  
  • Some wild animals spread diseases.

From wild animals, we get the following.

Elephant                -           ivory

Rhino                     -           horns

Kob                        -           meat

Buffalo                  -           meat

Leopard                 -           skin

Snake                    -           skin
