Animal movements.

Animals move from one place to another for reasons like:-

(a)        to look for food.

(b)        to look for water.

(c)        to look for shelter.

(d)        to look for protection.

(e)        to look for their young ones.

(f)        to hide from danger.

(g)        to look for new homes

Different animals have different ways of moving.

        Animal                                      Movement

            Bird                                         flies

            Monkey                                   jumps

            Cow                                         walks

            Snake                                      glides

            Fish                                         swims

            Caterpillar                              wriggles

            Man                                         walks.

There are many animals that use more than one type of movement e.g.

  1. A bird can walk and fly.
  2. A cat can walk and run.
  3. Man can crawl, walk and run.
  4. A duck can walk and fly.
  5. A dog can walk and run.
