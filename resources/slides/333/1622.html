From horns we can get:– glue, earrings, buttons, bungles, necklaces and many others.

Importances of animal products.

  • From horns, hooves and bones of some animals, we make glue which is used in schools and offices.
  • We can sell these products and get money.
  • In some parts of the world, elephants have been trained to transport people.
