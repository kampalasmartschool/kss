Harmful/bad insects.

Harmful insects are insects that are dangerous or cause injury to people or plants.

Examples:

Houseflies

Mosquitoes

Cockroaches

 

Houseflies,  bedbugs,  termites,  mosquitoes,  cockroaches,  caterpillars,  tsetse flies,  locusts,  bean weevils,  cotton stainer,  bean weevils, 

A spider is not an insect because it has eight legs and two main body parts.

How these insects are harmful.

  • Houseflies carry germs which cause diseases like cholera, dysentery, diarrhoea, typhoid, trachoma, conjunctivitis.

  • Tsetse flies spread sleeping sickness to people and animals

  • Mosquitoes spread malaria.

  • Bean weevils eat bean seeds

  • Cotton stainer stains cotton.

  • Caterpillars eat leaves of plants.

  • Banana weevils eat banana stems.

  • Termites eat stems, roots of plants and spoil our homes.
