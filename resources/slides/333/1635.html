Life stages of a cockroach.

 

- A cockroach lays its eggs in dark places or corners e.g. cupboards.

- Cockroaches feed on papers, clothes, left overs and dirty things in the toilet.

- Other insects which grow through three stages are grasshoppers, locusts.
