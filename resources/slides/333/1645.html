SEEDS.

What is a seed?

A seed is a living part of a plant that grows into a new plant.

-           Seeds are found in the fruit.

-           The fruit protects the seeds.

Some of the seeds can not be eaten because they are poisonous, sour and hard.

Germination of seeds.

-           Germination is the growing of a seed into a new plant or a seedling.

A seedling is a young plant.

Seeds need the following to germinate.

(i)         Moisture (water)

(ii)       Oxygen / air.
