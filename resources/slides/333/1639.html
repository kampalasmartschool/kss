Uses of plants.

(a)        Plants provide us with shade.

(b)        They act as wind breakers.  (trees protect our homes from strong wind)

(c)        They give us flowers for decoration.

(d)        They provide us with medicine (Medicinal plants)

(e)        They provide us with timber.

(f)        They give us firewood.

(g)        They fence our school.

(h)        They provide us with charcoal.

(i)         Forests are homes of some wild animals plus bushes.

(j)         We get fruits from plants.

(k)        Plants provide us with food.
