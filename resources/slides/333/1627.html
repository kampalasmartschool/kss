Animal weapons.

A weapon is something an animal uses to defend itself from danger or enemy.

          Animal                                     Weapon

            Snake                                      fangs

            Crocodile                                 tail

            Bedbug                                    bad smell

            Cat/dog/lion                             claws/teeth

            Elephant                                  trunk

            Cow                                         horns

            Dog/rat/lion/pigs                      teeth

            Chameleon                             change colour

            Birds                                        beaks

            Bee                                          sting

            Snail/tortoise                           hard-shell

            Man/monkey                             hands
