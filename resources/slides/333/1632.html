Parts of an insect.

An insect has 3 main body parts.  (Abdomen, thorax, head)

Parts of an insect.

The head;

  • On the head there is a pair of eyes and a pair of feelers.
  • An insect uses the feelers to feel as it moves.
  • The thorax has four wings which help the insect to fly and the legs.
  • These wings are found on the thorax.  There are some insects that do not have wings.
  • On the thorax again, legs are found.

The abdomen.

  • On the abdomen there are spiracles.
  • Insects use spiracles to breathe.
