Animal sounds.

All animals make sounds. They make sounds for different reasons for example:-

(a)        When they are hungry or thirsty.

(b)        When they are angry/annoyed.

(c)        When they are sick/in pain.

(d)        When they are looking for their young ones.

(e)        When they are in danger.

(f)        When they want company.
