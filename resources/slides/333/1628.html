  • Domestic birds
  • Wild birds

Domestic birds

These are birds that we keep in our homes. 

Examples; Turkey, ducks, pigeon, hens, cocks.

Stages of a bird.

            Egg      -            Nestling      -      Bird

    

Uses of domestic birds.

  • For eggs for example from hens.
  • For meat for example chicken.
  • As pets e.g. parrots.
  • Cocks tell the time.

Wild birds.

These are birds that are found in forests and bushes.  Some of them can be eaten and their eggs can also be eaten. 

Examples.

An owl, an eagle, Ostrich,  Swan,  Penguin,  weaver birds.
