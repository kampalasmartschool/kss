Changes in insects.

Insects grow from eggs.

           Stages of a house fly.

- A housefly grows through four stages.

- It is common in dirty homes.

- The larva stage of a housefly is called a Maggot.

-Houseflies are common in dirty places like rubbish pits, pit latrines or toilets.  This is where they lay the eggs from and grow.
