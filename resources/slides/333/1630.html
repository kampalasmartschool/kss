Useful/good insects.

These are insects that are good/useful to us.

Butterfly

Bee

Grasshopper

Examples:

      Grasshoppers,    bees,   white ants,   bees,   moth,   butterflies.

These insects help us in different ways.

  1. We use some of them as food e.g. white ants, grasshoppers.
  2. Some make food for us.  E.g. bees make honey.
  3. Some insects help to pollinate flowers and we get fruits and seeds.  E.g. butterflies, moth and bees.
