Dangers of plants.

- Some plants have thorns and they prick us.

- Some plants are poisonous.

- Some plants have bad smell.

- Some plants keep dangerous animals and insects like snakes, bees, wasps, rats

- Some plants have leaves which make the compound dirty.

- Some plants keep dangerous animals e.g. snakes.

Caring for plants.

We care for plants by:

-           Watering them.       

-           Weeding.       

-           Spraying

-           Pruning

-           mulching         -           mulching is the covering of  top soil with dry plant  materials.

-           Fencing.

Plants need the following conditions to grow well.

-           Sunlight

-           Oxygen (air)

-           Moisture (water)
