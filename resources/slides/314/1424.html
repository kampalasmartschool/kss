Wind instruments  (Aerophones).

These produce sound by vibration of air blown inside them.

Their pitch is determined (increasing ) by reducing the size. The smaller the instrument the higher the pitch.

Examples of Wind instruments.

  • Records.
  • Whistles.
  • Trumpet.
  • Panpipes.
  • Flute etc
