MUSICAL INSTRUMENTS.

They are classified into three:

  • String instruments.
  • Wind instruments.
  • Percussion instruments.

String instruments (chordophones).

These produce sound by vibration of their strings when plucked.

The pitch of sound in string instruments can be increased by loosening or tightening the strings

Examples of string instruments.

  1. Cello.
  2. Viola.
  3. Violin.
  4. Bow harp (Adungu)
  5. Guitar.
  6. Lyre.
  7. Tube fiddle.
  8. Harp.
