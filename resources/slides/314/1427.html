THE MAMMALIAN EAR.

The structure of the mammalian ear.

The ear is divided into three:-

  1. Outer ear.
  2. Middle ear.
  3. Inner ear.

The middle ear.

It consists of:-

  1. Ear drum.
  2. Ossicles.
  3. Eustachian tube.

The inner ear.

It consists of:-

  • Semi circular canal
  • The cochels
  • Auditory nerves
  • It is fluid filled.

Common diseases of the ear.

  1. Otitis externus.
  2. Otitis internus.

N.B   Disorder may be foreign bodies and any harm caused by an accident.

Uses of wax found in the ear.

  • It traps dust and some foreign bodies in the ear.
  • It cleans the ear canal.
  • It protects the ear against infections , wax contains chemicals that kill germs.
