Volume.

It is the loudness or softness of sound.

OR: Volume is the magnitude or intensity of a certain sound.

ECHO.

Is the reflected sound (the bouncing back of sound waves).

Echo is formed when sound is reflected by an object.

Uses of echo in nature.

  • It helps bats to find their way and locate food.
  • It helps sailors to detect the depth of the sea or ocean.
  • Fisherman use echo to locate shoals of fish before letting down their nets.
  • Pilots use echo surrounding from thick clouds, mountains etc to avoid accidents.

Disadvantages of echoes in Nature.

  • Echoes are troublesome especially in empty halls.
  • They cause repeated sounds and noise.
  • Soft,porous surfaces absorb sound.

How do we prevent echoes.

In cinema halls,broadcasting studios and conference or theatre halls the walls are covered using soft boards,thick and heavy curtains that absorb sound.
