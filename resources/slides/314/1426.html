Devices used in storing sound.

Sound can stored:-

a) by writing music (Solfa notation).

This is a form of writing music using syllables on staves

b) Recording.

Sound can be stored on record players dicks , compact disks , computers etc

Devices used to store sound.

  • Memory cards.
  • VCDs.
  • DVDs.
  • Magnetic tapes.
  • Projectors.
  • Tape recorders.
  • Mobile phones.
  • Flash card.
  • Computer discs.
  • Ipods.
