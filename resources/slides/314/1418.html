FORMS OF ENERGY.

What is energy?

Energy is the ability of the body to do work.

Energy exists in the following forms (types).

  1. Sound energy.
  2. Heat energy.
  3. Light energy.
  4. Mechanical energy.
  5. Nuclear (Atomic energy)
  6. Solar energy.
  7. Chemical energy.
  8. Electrical energy.

N.B  Kinetic and potential energy are forms of mechanical energy.

SOUND.

Sound is a form of energy that stimulates the sense of hearing (that enables us to hear).

Use of sound.

  • For communication.
  • Entertainment.
  • For protection.
  • For evidence in courts of law.
  • Helps to promote culture i.e. traditional folk sing and folk tales.

TYPES OF SOUND.

  1. Loud sound.
  2. Soft sound.
  3. High sound.
  4. Low sound.
