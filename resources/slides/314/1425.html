PERCUSSION INSTRUMENTS.

These produce sound by vibration of movable parts on the instrument or column of air inside them.        

Their pitch can be increased by reducing the size of the area struck or increasing the tightness.

Examples of Percussion Instruments.

  • Drums
  • Thumb piano
  • Shakers
  • Xylophone
  • Cymbals
  • Shakers
  • Rattles etc
