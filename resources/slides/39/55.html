Second letter

Alphabetical order considering the second letter.

 

 

 

Examples:

1. boy,        bag,        best,       bird

      bag,        best,       boy,        build

2. most,      meat,      mat,        milk

      mat,        meat,      milk,     most

Exercise:

Arrange the following words in alphabetical order.

  1. bench,    box,        basket,      big
  2. desk,      dog,        dig,           duck
  3. mug,       man,       meat,        millet
  4. fight,       fly,          fought,      fall
