FLOWERS.

A flower is a reproductive part of a flowering plant.

Uses of flowers to plants.

  • For reproduction.

Uses of flowers to people.

  1. For decoration.
  2. Showing love.
  3. For sale.
  4. For making perfume.
  5. They are used as wreaths.
  6. Some flowers are eaten.
  7. Some are used as a sign of welcome (bouquet).

Uses of flowers to other animals.

  1. Bees collect nectar and pollen from flowers.
  2. Humming birds, sun birds collect nectar from flowers.
