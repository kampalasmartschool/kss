POLLINATION.

It is the transfer of pollen grains from the anthers to the stigma.

Types of pollination.

  • Self-pollination.
  • Cross pollination.

Self-pollination.

It is the transfer of pollen grains from the anthers to the stigma on the same flower.

Movement of pollen grains.

Plants which carry out self-pollination.

  • Tomatoes
  • Wild marigold.

Cross pollination.

It is the transfer of pollen grains from the anthers to the stigma of different flowers but of the same kind.

Movement of pollen grains.

Plants which carry out cross pollination.

  1. Maize
  2. Coconut
  3. Pawpaw
  4. Cow peas
  5. Passion fruits
