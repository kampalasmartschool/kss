New Stone Age

  

  • It is also called Neolithic stage.  The most important discovery in this period was farming.
  • Farming marked the end of the middle stone and beginning of the new Stone Age.
  • Farming enabled to live a settled life
  • Man started living in communities and law were made to maintain order.
  • The discovery of iron led to the end of the Stone Age and beginning of the Iron Age.
