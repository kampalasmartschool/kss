UPTHRUST FORCE.

Is the force that liquids exert on to objects put in them.

NOTE: Upthrust makes objects weigh less when put in liquids.

The stone weighs more when it is in air and the weight reduces when its lowered in water.

The weight of the stone can be read from the spring balance.

Conclusion.

Objects weigh less in liquids. This is due to the up thrust force in liquids.
