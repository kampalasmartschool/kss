MEASUREMENT.

LENGTH.

Length is the distance between two points.

Length is the measured using the following units;

  • Millimetre (mm)
  • Centimeter (cm)
  • Metre (m)
  • Decametre (Dm)
  • Hectometre (Hm)
  • Kilometer (km)

The most commonly used units for measuring length are:- centimetre, metre and kilometre. The basic unit for measuring length is metre.
