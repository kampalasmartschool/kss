Finding density.

Example: Find the density of an object of mass 150g and volume of 30cc.

Solution:

D = M÷V

D = 150÷30

D = 5g/cc.

Example.

If the mass of the cuboid below is 480g, find its density.

Volume = L x W x H

                = 3 x 4 x 5

                = 60cm3

Density =   Mass ÷ Volume

=480 ÷ 60

=8g/cm3
