Types of shaped objects.

There are two types of shaped objects namely;

  1. Regular shaped objects
  2. Irregular shaped objects

Regular shaped objects.

These are objects which have a proper shape e.g. a cube, cuboid, blocks, tins, piece of chalk etc.

Cuboid

Cube

Cylinder

 

Irregular shaped objects.

These are objects which don’t have proper or well defined shape e.g. stones, keys, needles, shoes, sweet potato tuber, cassava tuber etc.
