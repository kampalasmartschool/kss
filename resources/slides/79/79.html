Activity 1

Discussing the verbs which end with   “e” orally

Activity 2

Change the verbs in brackets to present simple tense to fill the gaps

  1. Scott is ________________a car. (drive)
  2. Is our teacher ______________on the chalkboard? (write)
  3. They are_______________ to visit us. (come)
  4. Amanda is _______________cakes. (bake)
  5. She is _____________very well. (dance)
  6. We are __________________very fast. (move)
