MACHINES.

What is a machine?

  • A device or tool used to simplify man’s work.

How do machines simplify work?

  • By reducing the force used to do a piece of work.
  • Changing the direction of forces.
  • Increasing the speed of doing work.

Types of machines.

The two types of machines are:-

    Complex machines – are those made of many component parts and need training to use them.Eg tractor, sewing machine.

Simple machines – these are with few parts and do not need special training to use them.Egknife , panga , hoe.
