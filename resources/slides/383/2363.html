SCREWS.

What is a screw?

  • An inclined plane wound round a rod.

Examples of machines that use screws.

  • Bolts and nuts.
  • Bottles lids.
  • Motor car jack.
  • Spiral stair cases.

Uses of screws.

  • Car screw jack is used to lift vehicles.
  • To hold two or more things together.
  • To drill holes in wood or metal.
  • To tighten bottle tops.
