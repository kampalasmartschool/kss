WEDGES.

What is a wedge?

  • An inclined plane with two sloping surfaces i.e. double inclined plane.

Give the examples of wedges.

Bullet

Chisel

Panga

Other examples of wedges.

  • An axe
  • A panga
  • Knife
  • Scissors.

Uses of wedges:

  • For splitting logs of wood.
  • For cutting objects.
  • For sewing.
  • For digging.
