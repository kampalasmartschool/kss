WHEELS AND AXLE.

What are wheels and axle?

These are machines composed of two rotating wheels fixed together.

The wheel is fixed on a small wheel called axle or shaft onto which it rotates.

Examples of wheel and axle machines.

Door knobs

Windlass.
  • Car steering wheel.
  • Handle of bicycles.
  • Pedal and chains of bicycles
  • Sewing machine.
  • Windmill.

Application of wheels and axle in daily life.

  • Used in windlasses to draw water.
  • Sprocket wheels and chains used driving bicycles.
  • Car steering wheels.
  • Door knobs used to open doors.
