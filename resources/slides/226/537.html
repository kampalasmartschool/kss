Word statements in addition of 2 digit numbers with regrouping

a) There are 13 boys and 17 girls in P.2 class. How many children are there altogether?

 

b) Joan had 26 sweets.  Her mother gave her 9 more sweets.  How many sweets did she have altogether?

 

c) Peter had 30 oranges. His friend added him more 40, for him to sell. How many oranges does he have now?

 

d) Mother had 58 eggs, father brought in more 32 eggs. How many eggs does mother have?
