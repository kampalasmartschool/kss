Verbs

Verbs are doing words.

Examples of verbs.

Sweep, mop, arrange, write, cook, collect, eat, run, go, etc

Structures

The children are sweeping the classroom.

The teacher is writing on the chalkboard.

Activity

Complete the sentences with the words in brackets

  1. The cleaner is mopping the ________________________ (chalkboard, classroom)
  2. The children are arranging______________________(classroom, roof)
  3. The teacher is writing on the _______________________(desk, book)
  4. John is driving a ____________________ (bicycle, car)
  5. Mummy is going to the ________________________ (market, classroom)
