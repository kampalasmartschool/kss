CALCULATING TIME.

  • Time is calculated from the Greenwich meridian.
  • Every 15º make an hour, therefore 1º = 4 minutes.
  • The world is divided into 24 divisions called Time Zone and each time zone is 15º apart.

NB:  A time zone is a region on the globe that experiences the same standard time.

  • East of the Greenwich meridian, we add time while West of the Greenwich, we subtract time.
  • East Africa’s time zone is called East African Standard Time (EAST).

 

Worked Examples On Calculating Time.

Example 1.

The time in Ghana is 10:00am.  What time will it be in Uganda which is 45º East?

15º     =        1hour

45º    =        45º÷15º

          =        3 hours

Since its East, we add 3 hours to 10:00am.

 Hrs.            Min.                                13:00

 10               00                                   -12:00

+ 3               00                                    1:00pm

 13               00hrs

It will be 1:00pm in Uganda.

 

Example 2.

The time in Accra is 1:00pm. What is the time in Gabon which is 30º East?

15        =          1hour

30º      =          30º÷15º

            =          2 hours.

Since its East we add 2hours to 1:00am.

Hrs.                 Min.                                       

   1                   00                               

+ 2                   00       

   3                   00 pm.

It will be 3:00pm in Gabon.

Example 3.

It is 6:00am in Accra.  What time will it be in Liberia 30º West?

15º      =          1hour

30º      =          30º÷15º

            =          2 hours.

Since its  West we subtract 6:00 – 2 hours

 Hrs.                Min.                                       

   6                   00                               

- 2                   00       

  4                    00 a.m

It will be 4:00am in Liberia.

Example 4.

Its 10:00 am at the GMT. What time will it be if one moves 30° E.

15º      =          1hour

30º      =          30º÷15º

            =          2 hours.

Since movement is to the East. We add 2 hours to the original time.

  10:00am

+ 02:00        

  12:00 noon.

It will be 12:00noon.
