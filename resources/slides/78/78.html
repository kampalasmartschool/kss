The present Continuous Tense (Now tense)

We add “ing” to some verbs to change them to present continuous tense

Words   ”is” and  ”are” are used in the present continuous tense

 

Examples of present Continuous tense

Verb                             present continuous tense

reach                            reaching

eat                                eating

point                             ______________________

look                              ______________________

play                              ______________________
