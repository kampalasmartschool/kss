Difference between a hen and a cock.

  • A hen has a small comb and wattle on its  head while a cock has a big comb and wattle.
  • A cock has a long spur than that of a hen.
  • A cock has a long tail feathers than those of a hen.
  • A cock has brightly hackle feathers than those of a hen.

Types of Chicken.

A type of chicken means a class of birds kept for a special purpose.

Type

Purpose

Layers.

-kept for egg production (laying eggs) e.g. the white leg horn, the brown egger, Ancona, Minorca , Sykes.

Broilers (Heavy breeds) table birds.

-kept for meat production e.g. Light Sussex, the Ply mouth rock.

Dual Purpose.

-kept for both meat and egg production e.g. New Hampshire, Rhode Island red Black Australlup , kroilers.

Breeds of Chicken.

1. Local breeds.

Characteristics of Local breeds

  • They are more resistant to diseases and parasites.
  • They can withstand harsh climatic conditions than the exotic.
  • They have a slow growth rate.
  • They lay small and only a few eggs during each laying season.

NB: They can be improved by cross breeding.

Cross breeding improves their quality of performance.

Definition of Cross Breeding.

Mating animals of different breeds but of the same species.

1. Dual purpose.
