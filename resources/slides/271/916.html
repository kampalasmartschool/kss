PARASITES.

A  Parasite is a living organism which depend on other living organism for food without necessarily killing them but causing harm and inconveniences.

Types of Parasites.

a) Ecto Parasites.

These are parasites which attack fowls from outside their bodies e.g lice, redmites and depluming mite.

Prevention.

-Dust birds with ecto parasite pesticides.

b) Indo parasites.

These are parasites which attack the bird from inside its body especially the alimentary canal. E.g. Tape worms, round worms, hook worms.

Prevention.

  • Deworm the birds regularly.
  • Observe good hygiene in the poultry house.
  • Regular cleaning of troughs.

Effects of diseases and parasites to poultry.

  • They affect growth of the fowl.
  • They affect production in layers.
  • They affect profit and income from poultry farming.
