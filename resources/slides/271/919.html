2. Artificial Incubation.

This is a method where an incubator machine is used to hatch the eggs.

Some incubators use a lamp to supply heat while others use electricity.

Advantages of Artificial incubation.

  • Very many eggs can be hatched at the same time.
  • Artificial incubation can be used for commercial purpose.

Disadvantages of Artificial incubation.

  • It is an expensive method to manage.
  • It needs special attention and care.
  • There must be someone to always supervise it.

NB. The separating of chicks that is cocks from hens is called chick sexing.
