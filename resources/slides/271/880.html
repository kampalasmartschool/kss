External features of a bird.

Spur – For protection or defense.

Beak – Picking food and protection / support the bird during mating.

Feathers:-

  • Provide warmth to the bird.
  • Cover the body of a bird to protect it from mechanical injury.
  • Enable birds to fly.
  • Provide shape and colour for easy identification.
