Structures

Is ……….(mother, father) …………………..(cooking food)?

Yes, ………………is or No, ………………not.

Examples

1.             Is sister dancing?

                                       Yes, she is dancing.

2.                 Is mother sleeping?

No, she is not. She is cooking.
