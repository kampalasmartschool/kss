APICULTURE.

Apiculture is the rearing of bees for honey and wax.

Reasons why people keep bees.

  • Bees provide honey to man.
  • Honey can be sold to get money.
  • Honey is used to sweeten tea and bread.
  • Bees provide man with pollen which when eaten contain proteins.
  • Bees pollinate crops or plans for easy fertilization.
  • Honey is a source of carbohydrates.
  • Bees provide man with wax.

 Fig 2. Bee wax.

Products from bee wax.

  • Crayons.
  • Candle wax.
  • Cosmetics.
  • Polish for shoes and floors.
