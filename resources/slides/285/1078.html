SELF DESCRIPTION 

I look in the mirror

I look in the mirror

And what do I see?

A pair of eyes

Looks back at me

A nose, two ears,

Two eyebrow, too

Two lips, and teeth, to say

“I love you”

I look in the mirror

And what do I see?

I look in the mirror

And see myself

                        By Helen Moore
