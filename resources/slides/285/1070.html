Reflexive and Adjective pronouns

Adjectives pronouns

These are pronouns used as adjectives.

Example

my, yours, his, her, their, our.

Reflexive pronouns

These are pronouns formed by adding “self” or “ selves” on most of the adjective pronouns.

Example

Adjective pronouns             Reflexive pronouns

My                                                        myself

Our                                                       ourselves

You’re                                                   your selves

Her                                                       herself

His (him)                                               himself

Its                                                         it self

Their ( them)                                         themselves.

One’s                                                    oneself.
