Read the story and answer the questions about it in full sentences.

Christian drove to Lake Tahoe to take pictures of the lake, trees and mountains for her photography class. She woke up early and left the house at 4:00 am while it was still dark outside. She stopped at a small restaurant for breakfast and ten continued driving. When she arrived at Lake Tahoe, the sky was pink and the sun was rising over the water. She sat on a rock next to the lake and watched the fish swim in water and listened to the beautiful bird chirp.

She thought about how lucky she was to be in such a beautiful, peaceful place. Then she hurried to her car to get her camera so she could take pictures for her class project.
