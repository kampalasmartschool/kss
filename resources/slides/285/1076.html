Study the dialogue and answer questions about it in full sentences.

Lauren: Hello Laura, I have just got a job at a counseling firm.

Laura:   Congratulations! That is great!

Lauren: I am so happy. I don’t have to worry about finding a job and interviews anymore.

Laura:   When do you start?

Lauren: I start next Monday.

Laura:   You are going to have to put in a lot of hours working.

Lauren: I know. I am a little nervous but it’s pretty exciting.

Laura:   I guess you must be nervous.

Lauren: That’s true but I am going to celebrate today. Do you want to hang out?

Laura:   Sure. It’s on your right.

Lauren: Of course. How about Amara? Should we call her?

Laura:   I don’t know. She is a little depressed. She’s having a hard time finding a job right now.

Lauren: I should still call her. In the least, I can refer her to my company. It might help.

Laura:   That sounds like a good idea.
