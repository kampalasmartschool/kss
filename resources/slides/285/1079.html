Study the conversation and answer questions about it in full sentences.

Susan:  Sarah, you look terrible. What happened?

Sarah:   My grandmother just passed away.

Susan:  I am so sorry to hear that. When did this happen?

Sarah:   Five days ago. I have just got back from the funeral.

Susan:  Is there anything I can do?

Sarah:   No, not really. The sad thing is that I wasn’t there when she died. I really wanted to be there one last time.

Susan:  I am sure she knew you loved her. Did she pass away in the hospital?

Sarah:   Yes, she died in her sleep.

Susan:  I am sorry she is gone.

Sarah:   At least she didn’t suffer.

Susan:  I think your grandmother would want you to think of all the good times you spent together.

Sarah:   I know but I miss her.
