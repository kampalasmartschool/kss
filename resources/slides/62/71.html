Nouns

A noun is a naming word e.g.

  1. Names of people e.g. Mary, Jane, John, David, Elaine, Penninah etc
  2. Name of places e.g. Nakasero, Kampala, Nairobi, London, Uganda, Nigeria
  3. Names of objects e.g. spoon, table, chair, desk etc    
  4. Names of lakes, hospitals, animals, birds etc

 

Activity

Circle the nouns from the given sentences

  1. I live at Mengo.
  2. Mary is here.
  3. The cat is running.
  4. Lake Victoria is a big lake.
  5. Kampala is the capital city of Uganda.
