TOURISIM (NB:Teacher should teach tourism i.e. definition importance of tourism)

Problems faced by tourism industry.

  • Insecurity in the country
  • Poor transport
  • Poor accommodation
  • Poor publicity
  • Poor maintenance of tourist centres.
  • poaching

Solutions to problems

  • Providing security
  • Proper maintenance of roads
  • Building and maintaining hotels.
  • Advertise internationally.

Ways of promoting tourism

  • Adequate publicity in the mass media
  • Setting up more tourist sites
  • Improving on hotels
  • Ensuring peace and security
  • Improving social infrastructure
  • Encourage people to invest in tourism.
