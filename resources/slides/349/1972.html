ENERGY RESOURCES IN THE ENVIRONMENT.

Definition of energy resources:

These are resources which provide useful energy to man.

Definition of a resource.

A resource is anything in the environment which man uses to satisfy his needs.

Examples of energy resources.

  • Water.
  • Sun.
  • Wind.
  • Fossils.
  • Animals.
  • Plants.
  • Uranium.

Types of energy resources.

  1. Renewable energy resources.
  2. Non-renewable energy resources.

RENEWABLE ENERGY RESOURCES.

These are energy resources which can be replaced naturally once they are used up.

They include:

  • Sun.
  • Wind.
  • Running water.
  • Plants.
  • Animals.

NON RENEWABLE ENERGY RESOURCES.

These are resources which cannot be replaced by nature once they are used up.

They include:

  • Fossils.
  • Uranium.
  • Fuels.
