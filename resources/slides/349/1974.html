Natural gas.

  • This is mined from areas where petroleum is found. The gas is mainly used for lighting and heating.
  • It can be used for cooking and running power stations.

Coal.

  • This is a hard black material found below the ground.
  • It is a solid fossil fuel formed from the remains of living things.
  • Coal can be burnt to supply heat in power stations.

Dangers of fossil fuel.

  • They lead to pollution.
  • They are non-biodegradable.
  • Bio fuel.

Bio fuels.

Are made from plant wastes and animal wastes.

Examples of bio fuels.

  • Bioethanol.
  • Bio diesel.

Bio ethanol – is a liquid that is made from sugarcane through the process of fermentation.

NB: Maize can also be used to make bio ethanol.

  • It can be used as a fuel in vehicles

Bio –diesel.

Is a fuel made from vegetable oils or animal fat.
