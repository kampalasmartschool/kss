How to keep things / items at home clean.

Beddings and clothings:-

  • By washing them.
  • Ironing.
  • Spreading under the sunshine.
  • Spraying with insecticides.

Diseases that result from poor personal hygiene.

  • Scabies.
  • Ringworms.
  • Impetigo.

Importance of keeping our bodies clean.

  • It controls the spread of germs.
  • It prevents bad body smell.
  • It prevents skin diseases.
  • It prevents teeth diseases.
  • It prevents lice, mites and ticks.
  • To remove dirt.
  • To be smart.
