PERSONAL HYGIENE.

  • Personal hygiene is the keeping of our bodies clean.
  • It is also the general cleanliness of our bodies.

Ways of keeping our bodies clean (How to keep our bodies clean).

  • Bathing regularly.
  • Cutting finger and toe nails short.
  • Brushing teeth every day.
  • Washing hands after visiting the toilet or latrine.
  • Washing hands after a physical task like digging picking rubbish etc.
  • Washing hands before eating or touching food.
  • Washing clothes regularly.
  • Washing beddings regularly
  • Combing hair daily.
  • Ironing clothes and bedding.

Items or things used in keeping our bodies clean.

  • Water (clean)           -Bathing sponge            - Towel
  • Tooth paste             - Soap                            - Nail cutters / razor blade
  • Tooth brush             -Comb                            - Dental floss
  • Tooth picks                                                    - Ear buds
