Twins.

  1. Twins are children born on the same day by the same mother.
  2. The mother of twins is called Nalongo.
  3. The father of twins is called Ssalongo.
  4. A child born before the twins is called Kigongo.
  5. The child born after twins is called Kizza / Kiiza.
