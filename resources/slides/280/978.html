Roles of the family members.

Duties of a father.

  1. A father heads a family.
  2. A father pays school fees.
  3. A father buys food and other things for the family.
  4. A father takes and picks children from school.
  5. A father pays house rent.

Duties of a mother.

  1. A mother cares for the home.
  2. A mother cooks food.
  3. A mother looks after children.
  4. A mother washes clothes.
  5. A mother takes ad picks children from school.
  6. A mother pays school fees.
  7. A mother heads a home when the father is not around.
  8. A mother pays house rent.

Duties of children.

  • Children clean the home.

     

  • Children fetch water.

   

  • Children collect firewood.

   

  • Children help their parents.

   

  • Children wash utensils.

     

  • Children carry babies.
