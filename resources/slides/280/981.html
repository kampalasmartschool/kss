Roles of different people in the community.

  1. Doctors treat sick people.
  2. Nurses cares for the sick people.
  3. Teachers teach children.
  4. Carpenters makes furniture.
  5. Plumber repairs broken pipes.
  6. Farmers grows crops and keeps (rear) animals.
  7. Herds man looks after cattle.
  8. Cobbler mends our shoes.
  9. Shopkeepers sell things in a shop.
  10. Fishmonger sells fish.
  11. Veterinary doctor treats sick animals.
  12. Barber cuts our hair.
  13. Police officers keeps law and order.
  14. Shepherd looks (care) after sheep.
  15. Oculist examines and treats eyes.
  16. Optician sells eye glasses.
  17. Tailor sews clothes.
