Is this a _____________?

No, it is not. It is a ________________ Or Yes, it is ______________________

Are these __________________?

No, they are not. They are __________________ Or Yes, they are ______________

Examples

                              Is this a book?

                                    No, it is not. It is a pencil.

 

          Are these tables?

                                    Yes, they are tables.
