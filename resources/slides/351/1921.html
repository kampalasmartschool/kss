HEAT ENERGY.

What is heat?

Heat is a form of energy that causes matter to become hot or warm. 

OR

Heat is a form of energy that increases the temperature of matter making it hot or warm.

Units for heat is colones or joules.

SOURCES OF HEAT.

A Source of heat is anything that provides heat.

Examples:

  1. Sun – this is the main natural source of heat in the environment.
  2. Fire(fuels) from burning objects e.g firewood, charcoal.
  3. Electricity.
  4. Friction or objects rubbing against each other.
  5. Food.
  6. Decaying matter.

Uses of Heat energy.

Heat energy is useful in many ways.

  1. It enables our bodies to function.
  2. It enables us to cook food.
  3. It helps in rain formation.
  4. Hot objects produce light which enables us to see.
  5. We use heated bodies to iron clothes.
  6. Heat is used to kill germs.
  7. Heat from the sun provides warmth to seeds to germinate.
  8. Heat helps animals to feel warm.
  9. People use heat to roast food.
