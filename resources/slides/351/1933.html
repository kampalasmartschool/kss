BURNING AND RUSTING.

Burning is a chemical reaction in which heat and light are produced.

The gas that is given out during burning is carbondioxide.

The gas that is needed for burning to take place is oxygen.

ZONES OF A BURNING CANDLE.

  1. Blue zone
  2. Yellow zone
  3. Pale blue zone
  4. Central zone of unburnt wax.

An experiment to show that oxygen supports burning.

Covering the burning candle with a glass cuts off the supply of oxygen and the candle lights out.

PUTTING OFF FIRE.

  1. Using fire extinguisher.
  2. Using dust.
  3. Using sand.
  4. Using water.
  5. Wrapping yourself in a thick blanket.
  6. Rolling yourself on the ground.

Reasons why water is not used to put off fire caused by petrol.

Petrol is less dense than water therefore it floats on water and fire continues burning.
