Energy.

Energy is the ability to do work.

Types of energy.

  1. Kinetic energy.
  2. Potential energy.

Kinetic energy is the energy possessed by a moving object (an object in motion) e.g. a stone thrown in air, a leaf falling on the ground, a boy running.

Potential energy is the energy possessed by a body at rest. E.g. a car parked, a stone resting on the ground, a book placed on a table.

Forms of energy.

Forms of energy are produced by objects.

  1. Heat energy.
  2. Sound energy.
  3. Light energy.
  4. Electric energy.
  5. Chemical energy.
  6. Solar energy.

Energy inter-conversion.

Energy can be converted from one form to another.

  • A candle chemical energy stored in the wax to heat energy the light energy.
  • A ball kicked , it hits the goal post. Changes in energy are: potential to kinetic to sound to   heat
