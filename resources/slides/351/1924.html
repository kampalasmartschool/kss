Conclusion.

PREVENTING BAD EFFECTS OF  EXPANSION AND CONTRACTION.

  1. Gaps should be left between railway lines to allow expansion on hot weather.
  2. Electric wires should be loosely fixed on poles to allow contraction on cold weather.
  3. Gaps should be left in soda bottles to allow the expansion of liquids inside when frozen.

Expansion in gases.

Gases have the greatest rate of expansion.
