RUSTING. 

Rusting is a chemical reaction which needs the presence of water and oxygen.

The product of rusting is iron rust or iron oxideA metal gets a brown coating called rust. Oxygen and water are the requirements needed for rusting to take place.

REQUIREMENTS OF RUSTING.

In order iron to rust the following must be available:-

  1. Oxygen
  2. Moisture (water)

EXPERIMENT ON RUSTING.

In the first test tube, the nail rusts because there's supply of bot water and oxygen.

In the second test tube, rusting doesn't occur because the oil on top of the water cuts off the oxygen supply.

In the third test tube, rusting doesn't occur because there's only air and no water. 

Conclusion.

Rusting only takes place in the presence of both water and oxygen.

DISADVANTAGES OF RUSTING.

  1. It results in wearing away iron and steel equipment.
  2. It changes and spoils the colour of things.
  3. Bolts become hard to unscrew after rusting.
  4. Keys fail to fit in padlocks after rusting.
  5. Water from rusty containers becomes poisonous.
  6. Rusting makes cutting materials blunt.

HOW TO PREVENT RUSTING IN METALS.

  1. By oiling and greasing.
  2. By painting.
  3. By using tar.
  4. By galvanizing.
  5. By enameling.
  6. By making iron stainless steel.

COMPARING BURINING AND RUSTING.

Similarities.

  1. Both require oxygen to take place.
  2. Both are chemical reactions.

Differences.

Burning needs only oxygen to take place while rusting needs both oxygen and moisture.
