MATTER.

What is matter?

Matter is anything that has volume and mass.

OR

Matter is anything that has weight and occupies space.

What is Volume?

Volume is the amount of space occupied by an object.

What is Mass?

Mass is the amount of matter contained in an object.

States of Matter.

Matter exists in three states namely:-

  1. Solids.
  2. Liquids.
  3. Gases.
