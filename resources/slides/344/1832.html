Germs and diseases.

A germ is a small / tiny living  organism that cause diseases.

Germs are too small to be seen with naked eyes. They are seen using a microscope.

A microscope.

Types of germs.

These include:-

  • Virus.
  • Bacteria.
  • Protozoa.
  • Fungi.

Where germs are found.

Germs are found in:-

  • Faeces and Urine.
  • Contaminated water.
  • Soil.
  • Air.
  • On dirty clothes.
  • On dirty beddings.
  • Under dirty finger nails.
  • Blood.
  • Inside the body.
  • On our bodies.
  • On dirty food.
  • On dead bodies.
