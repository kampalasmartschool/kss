PHOTOSYNTHESIS.

It is the process by which green plants make their own food.

Photo means light.

Synthesis means to build/make/manufacture.

Conditions for photosynthesis.

  1. Chlorophyll: traps sunlight energy
  2. Sunlight provides energy to the leaf.

NB:   The raw materials for photosynthesis are carbondioxide and water.

         The bi–product of photosynthesis is oxygen.

         The product of photosynthesis is starch.
