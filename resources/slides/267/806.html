STRUCTURE OF A FLOWERING PLANT.

Systems of a flowering plants.

  1. Shoot system.
  2. Root system.

Shoot system.

The system of the plant above the ground level.

It develops from the plumule of the seed.

Parts of a shoot system.

  1. Leaves.
  2. Fruits.
  3. Node.
  4. Terminal bud.
  5. Stem.
  6. Flower.
  7. Internodes.
  8. Auxiliary bud.
  9. Axil.
  10. Branch.

Activity.

1. Apart from the shoot system, name one other system of a flowering plant.

2. Which part of a seed grows into the shoot system?

3. Name any two parts that make up the shoot system.
