Ways plants climb others.

1. Using tendrils: e.g. passion fruits, cucumber, peas, pumpkins etc.

2. Using hooks or thorns: e.g. bougainvillea.

3. By twinning (clasping): e.g. morning glory, some beans, sponge.

Why do some plants climb others?

1. For support.

2. To get enough sunlight.
