Uses of leaves to people.

  1. Some leaves are eaten as food e.g. cabbage.
  2. For sale.
  3. For making shelter for man.
  4. For decoration e.g. palm leaves.
  5. For beverage e.g. tea leaves.
  6. For herbal medicine e.g. mango, guavas etc.
  7. For feeding domestic animals.
  8. For making mats.
  9. Some leaves are used for propagation e.g. bryophyllum.

Uses of leaves to a plant.

  1. For making food (photosynthesis).
  2. For breathing.
  3. For transpiration.
  4. Some store food as the plants e.g. onions.
