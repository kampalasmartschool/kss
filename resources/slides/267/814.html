TYPES OF STEMS.

1. Stem tubers.

What are stem tubers?

Stem tubers are swollen underground stems with stored food. E.g. Irish potatoes, yams.

NB: Sugarcane is not a stem tuber because its food is not found under the ground for example Irish potato structure.
