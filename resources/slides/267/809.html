COMPOUND LEAVES.

These are leaves with more than one leaflet on the stalk.

Characteristics of compound leaves.

  • They have many leaflets.
  • They have many leaf stalks.

Kinds of compound leaves.

  • Compound pinnate leaves e.g. acacia.

  • Compound bi–pinnate leaves e.g. Jacaranda

  • Compound trifoliate e,g. beans, soya.

  • Compound digitate e.g. cassava.
