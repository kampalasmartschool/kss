ONE WORD FOR MANY

Examples

  1. A period of two weeks fortnight.
  2.  A place where items of long ago are kept museum.
  3. A place where animals are slaughtered abattoir.
  4. A place where beer is made brewery.
  5. A set of three children / animals born of the same mother at the same time triplets.
  6. Meat without bones. Fillet
  7. A person who repairs machines. Mechanic.
  8. A place where wild animals are kept. Zoo
  9. A child whose parents are dead. An orphan
  10. A place where films are shown. Cinema
  11. A person who is in charge of a ship. Captain
  12. A place where fruit trees are grown. An orchard
  13. One who deals in flowers. Florist
  14. One who journeys on foot. Pedestrian
  15. A place where bread is made. Bakery
  16. A vessel where flowers are kept. Vase
  17. A doctor who performs operations. Surgeon
