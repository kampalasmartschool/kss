THE FUTURE

Study the dialogue and answer questions that follow in full sentences.

Taj:      Lucy, do you know that Amara is going to have a party?

Lucy:    When will Amara have a party?

Taj:      Tomorrow, Amara will be ten years old. Her parents are inviting her friends.

Lucy:    Are they going to invite us too?

Taj:      Of course, Amara has invited us already.

Lucy:    Oh my! What shall I wear? What will buy for Amara?

Taj:      I shall buy Amara school bag for a gift.

Lucy:    I shall wear my white blouse and jeans. I shall get her a pencil case. I am sure she will like it.

Taj:      We shall go there together. Please, don’t leave me.

Lucy:    of course, I shall not leave you. We always go there together, don’t we?

Taj:      Yes, I shall wait for you.
