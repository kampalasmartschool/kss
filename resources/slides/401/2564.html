Lower work: Review negative statements.

Use of unless

Unless means if not.

Examples

1. If he does not work hard, he will not pass.

Unless he works hard, he will not pass.

He will not pass unless he works hard.

2. If you clean your shoes, the teacher will allow you in class.

Unless you clean your shoes, the teacher will not allow you in class.

The teacher will not allow you in class unless you clean your shoes.
