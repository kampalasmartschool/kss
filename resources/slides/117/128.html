Changing ‘y’ to ‘i’ before adding ‘es’

Examples

Verb                 Present simple tense

 

cry                    cries

fly                     flies

carry                 carries

marry                marries

copy                 copies

try                     tries

Activity

Add ‘ies’ to the words in brackets.

  1. The baby ………….every day. (cry)
  2. A bird……………..(fly)
  3. He………………..a big box on his heads. (carry)
  4. She………………groundnuts every day. (fry)
  5. Peter…………..his friend’s work every day. (copy)
