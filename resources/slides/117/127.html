Present simple tense by adding “es”

Vocabulary

wash    watch   preach  teach    hatch    match   go         box       do         brush   

NB: Verbs that end with sh, x, ch, o, s  we add ‘es’ to form their present simple tense.

Examples

Verb                             Present simple tense

Wash                           washes

Watch                          watches

Preach                          preaches

Brush                            brushes

Box                              boxes

Fetch                           fetches

Activity

Use the word in brackets to fill the gaps correctly.

  1. Joel ________________ his shirt every day. (wash)
  2. Our hen ___________________ many eggs every month. (hatch)
  3. Sandra ___________________ her teeth every night. (brush)
  4. Who __________________ water every morning? (fetch
