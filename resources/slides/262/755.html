MOVEMENT OF WATER THROUGH SOIL.

Observations and deductions.

A. Clay soil allows little water to go through. Why?

It has the smallest air spaces or it has the finest soil particles.

B. Sand soil allows water to pass through fastest. Why?

It has the largest air spaces or it has the biggest soil particles.

C. Loam soil allows water to go through moderately.

Activity.

  1. Pupils should perform experiments about movement of water in the soil using different types of soil.
