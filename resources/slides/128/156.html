CONTENT : Homophones

These are words with similar sound but different meaning.

Examples

hear   -  here                 their      -         there

it        -  eat                    ship      -          sheep

shut   -  shirt                  sun       -           son

meet  -  meat                   sit        -          seat

write  -  right                  knows   -           nose
