CONTENT   : Words with similar meaning (synonyms)

Synonyms are words that mean a similar thing to each other.

Examples

Word                Synonym

Close                shut                             

big                    large

correct              right                             

sick                   ill

begin                start                             

happy               glad

fast                  quick                           

money              cash
