Adjectives

Adjectives are words that talk more about a noun.

Examples of adjectives.

Big, dirty, small, clean, white, long, short, tall

 

Structure.

Our house is big.

House is a noun while big is an adjective.

The _______________ is ________________

The _______________ are _______________

Examples.

  1. The table is dirty.
  2. The desks are clean.
  3. The book is big.
