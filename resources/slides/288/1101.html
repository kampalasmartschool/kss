LAKES.

A lake is a large mass of water in a basin.

The major lakes in our district / Kampala.

  • L.Victoria (the biggest fresh water lake).
  • Kabaka’s lake (man – made) in Rubaga division.

Other lakes in Uganda.

  1. L. Kyoga, the swampiest lake.
  2. L.Albert has oil wells.
  3. L.Edward.
  4. L.Katwe for salt production.
  5. L.Kwania.
  6. L.Wamala.
  7. L.Bisina.
  8. L. George.

a) Lake Victoria.

  • The former name of Lake Victoria was Nyanja Nalubaale.

    

  • It was called Nalubaale because it was a home for local gods.
  • Lake Victoria is the largest lake in Uganda.
  • Some inland ports found on lake Victoria are:-

Port bell, Jinja and Bukakata in Uganda.

b) Lake Kyoga.

  • It is the most swampy lake in Uganda.

      

  • Lake Kyoga is swampy because it is shallow.
  • It is found in central Uganda.

c) Lake Albert.

  • The former name of Lake Albert was Mwitanzigye.

     

  • It is found in Western Uganda.
  • Crude oil (petroleum) has been found in Lake Albert.

d) Lake Edward and George.

  • They are found in western part of Uganda in Queen Elizabeth National park.
  • Lake George is joined to lake Edward by Kazinga Channel

    

e) Lake Katwe.

  • Lake Katwe is found in the Western Rift valley.

  • It is well known for salt production.
