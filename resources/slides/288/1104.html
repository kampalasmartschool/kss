MOUNTAINS / HIGHLANDS.

Mountains are large raised steep pieces of land.

Mountains form the highest physical feature of Uganda.

Examples of major mountains in Uganda.

  • Mt. Rwenzori.
  • Mt. Elgon.
  • Mt. Moroto.
  • Mufumbiro ranges.

Mt. Rwenzori.

  • Mt. Rwenzori is the highest mountain in Uganda.

  • It is found in western part of Uganda.
  • The highest peak of Mt. Rwenzori is called Margherita.
  • The peak of mountain Rwenzori is snowcapped throughout the year.

Question: Why is the peak of Mt. Rwenzori snowcapped?

Answer:    The peak of Mt. Rwenzori crosses the snow line.
