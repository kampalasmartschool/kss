  • By terracing the land.

   Valleys.

A valley is a low land between two close hills or mountains

Diagram of a valley.

Uses of valleys.

  • Valleys are used for crop growing.
  • They are used for grazing animals.
  • They attract tourists.

Dangers of valleys.

  • They flood during heavy rains.
  • They hide disease vectors.
  • They make construction of roads and railway lines difficult.
