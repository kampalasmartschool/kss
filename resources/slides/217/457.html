Peace and Security   

Peace is living in harmony without fighting and quarrelling with one another.

Security is living with protection and freedom.

Factors that promote peace and security at home

  1. Love.
  2. Obedience.
  3. Caring / helping.
  4. Good health.
  5. Good relationships.
  6. Respect.
  7. Good feeding.
  8. Protection.
  9. Reporting wrong doers.
  10. Protection i.e.
  • Keeping dogs at home.
  • Locking doors and windows.
  • Sharing.
  • Working together.

People who promote peace and security at home

  • Parents.

     

  • Guards.

    

  • Elders.

     

  • Relatives.

    

  • Children.

   

  • Maids.
