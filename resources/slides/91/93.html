Forming small words from big words

Examples

schoolgirl         -           school, girl

prepositions       -           positions

doing                 -           do, in

teacher            -           tea, her, he                   

office               -           off, ice, of                     

compound          -           pound, un         

afternoon          -           after, noon, on              

pigeon                -           pig, on 

donkey                -           do, key
