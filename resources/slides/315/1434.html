BLOOD VESSELS.

This is a network of tubes transporting blood in the body.

There are three blood vessels:-

  • Arteries.
  • Veins.
  • Capillaries.

a) Arteries.

  • Arteries are blood vessels that transport blood away from the heart to the body.
  • They have thick walls.
  • They have a small lumen.

b) Veins.

  • These are blood vessels that carry blood  towards the heart.
  • They have thin walls.
  • They have a wider lumen.
  • They have valves to prevent the back flow of blood.

c) Capillaries.

  • These are the smallest blood vessels.
  • They connect arteries and veins.
  • Exchange of body materials take place capillaries.
  • They have thin walls.
