Using When…/…….when…………..

Example

1. He came home. I was in the garden.

He came home when I was in the garden.

When he came from home, I was in the garden.

2. Mukasa laughed. The teacher knocked himself.

Mukasa laughed when the teacher knocked himself.

When the teacher knocked himself, Mukasa laughed.
