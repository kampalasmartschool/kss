Though……../ ………though……

Examples

1. Johnson is not a trained carpenter but he makes nice chairs.

Though Johnson is not a trained carpenter, he makes nice chairs.

Johnson makes nice chairs though he is not a trained carpenter.

2. The carpenter made a nice table. He did not have enough nails.

Though the carpenter did not have enough nails, he made a nice table.

The carpenter made a nice table though he did not have enough nails.
