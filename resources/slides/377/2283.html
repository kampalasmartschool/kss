……….besides……/ ………..beside……

Besides – a part from

Beside – next to

Examples

1. Mr. Malevu has a carpentry workshop. He also has a saw mill.

Mr. Malevu has a carpentry workshop besides a saw mill.

2. Anguzu did not receive any furniture besides the dining table.
