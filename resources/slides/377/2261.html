GRAMMAR: THE USE OF PRESENT TENSE

PRESENT SIMPLE

Examples

She writes

They play

I cook

 

The present simple tense is used to show that something happens regularly, it is a habit or it is done everyday, sometimes, usually, generally, often, twice a week or more than once

We

They                             run

It

Note: the other use of present simple tense is to make statements or facts

e.g. Sugar melts, a river flows, Kampala is the capital city of Uganda.
