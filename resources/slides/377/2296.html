Using………that……

That is used for animals and things and at times used for persons

Examples

  1. The rich man revealed a secret. He is a thief. The rich man revealed a secret that he is a thief.
  2. Our teacher has a car. He leaves it at home. Our teacher has a car that he leaves at home
