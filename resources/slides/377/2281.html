Active and passive in the present perfect

Examples

1. We have drunk cold tea.

Cold tea has been drunk by us.

2. They have drunk cold tea.

Cold tea has been drunk.

3. Sanyu has drunk cold tea.

Cold tea has been drunk by Sanyu.
