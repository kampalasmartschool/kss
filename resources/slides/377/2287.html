ASPECT: RELATIVE CLAUSES

Content: adjective clauses and relatives pronouns

Relative pronouns are; who, whom, which, whose, when, where, that

Use of ……..who……..

Example

1. I am going to see Mr. Mubiru. He is a carpenter

I am going to see Mr. Mubiru who is a carpenter

2. That is Sarah. She gave you a new pen

That is Sarah who gave you a new pen
