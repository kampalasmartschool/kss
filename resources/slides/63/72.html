Using articles ‘a’ and ‘an’

Using article “an” before single nouns starting with vowels a, e, i, o, u  as shown below.

Example

an elephant,      an eagle,           an egg,             an ox    e.t.c

We use “a” before single nouns which begin with consonants.

Activity

Write   ‘a’   or    ‘an’ to fill the gaps

1.       This is _______________ box.

2.       Show me _______________apple.

3.       She is eating ______________egg.

4.       Ibra has ______________pen and ___________ book.

5.       Bring me __________________umbrella and ____________book.
