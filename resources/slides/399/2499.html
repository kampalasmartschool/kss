ACTIVITY

Use the correct form of the words in brackets to complete the sentences.

  1. The ________________ was arrested by the police. (crime)
  2. The local police ended their operation __________________. (peace)
  3. By the time the police arrived, the robbers had __________________. (flee)
  4. They were arrested because they entered the country _________________. (illegal)
  5. All citizens must work hard to promote _________________ in their community. (secure)
  6. None of the __________________ items has been recovered by the police. (steal)
  7. The old man was charged with __________________ by the judge. (defile)
  8. The fire brigade arrived ____________________ the school caught fire. (immediate)
