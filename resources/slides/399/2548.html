Use of: Barely……and No sooner……

Examples

1. The dog had not eaten the bone. The bone was snatched by a hyena.

Barely had the dog eaten the bone when it was snatched by the hyena.

2. The car entered the road. The car got an accident.

No sooner had the car entered the road than it got an accident.
