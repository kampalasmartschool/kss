Use of………looking forward to……

When using …looking forward to…., the verb changes to continuous form.

Examples

1. The P7 pupils will do their PLE in November.

The P7 pupils are looking forward to doing their PLE in November.

2. Ngami will visit his relatives next month.

Ngami is looking forward to visiting his relatives next month.

3. They will be learning French next year.

They are looking forward to learning French next year.
