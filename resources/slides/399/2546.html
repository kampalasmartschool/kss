Use of: Not only………but also/……..not only…………

Examples

1. The goat is sick. It has a broken leg.

Not only is the goat sick but also has a broken leg.

The goat is not only sick but also has a broken leg.

2. He is my best friend. He is my age mate.

Not only is he my age mate but also my best friend.

He is not only my best fried but also my age mate.

3. She respects her teacher. She respects her class monitor.

Not only does she respect her teacher but also her class monitor.

She not only respects her teacher but also her class monitor.
