Use of …….as long as…./ …..so long as…../ ……..provided….

Examples

1. You will catch the earliest bus if you wake up early.

  • As long as you wake up early, you will catch the earliest bus.
  • So long you wake up early you will catch the earliest bus.
  • Provided you wake up early, you will catch the earliest bus

2. If that girl misbehaves, the headteacher will expel her.

  • The headteacher will expel that girl as long as she misbehaves.
  • The headteacher will expel that girl so long as she misbehaves.
  • The headteacher will expel that girl provided she misbehaves.
