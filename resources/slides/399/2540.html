CONJUNCTIONS

As soon as/ immediately / the moment

Examples

1. The dog started barking when it saw us.

  • As soon as the dog saw us, it started barking.
  • The moment the dog saw us, it started barking.
  • Immediately the dog saw us, it started barking.

2. The rebels crossed the border. The army ambushed them.

  • The army ambushed the rebels as soon as they crossed the border.
  • The army ambushed the rebels immediately they crossed the border.
  • The army ambushed the rebels the moment they crossed the border.
