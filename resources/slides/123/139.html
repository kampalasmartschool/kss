THEME            :           Transport and Communication

SUB THEM       :           Types and means of transport

CONTENT        :           Joining sentences using “but”

Examples

1.       Musa went to the airport. He did not see the aeroplane.

          Musa went to the airport but he did not see the aeroplane.

3.       My brother went to the stage. He missed the bus.

          My brother went to the stage but he missed the bus.
