Drawing pictographs

Example:

Five girls were told to pick flowers from the garden and each picked as follows;

Rose picked 6 flowers

Jamila picked 3 flowers

Annet picked 2 flowers

Sarah picked 6 flowers

Questions

a) Make a picture graph and show the information above

b) Which two girls picked the same number of flowers?

c) What is the total number of flowers picked by the five girls?
