GOOD FEEDING.

Balanced diet.

What is a balanced diet?

It is a meal with all food values.

Balanced meals help us to have good health.

Balanced meals help us to grow well.

Examples of balanced diet / meal.

1. Pineapple  +    Meat    +          Rice     +          Nakati

 

2. Bread  + Beans           +          Cabbages        +          Posho

Activity.

Pupils will arrange a balanced diet practically.

Effects of poor feeding.

Some people feed poorly by eating one type of food e.g. posho every day or bananas everyday.

Poor methods of feeding may result into:-

  • Malnutrition
  • Death
  • Blindness
  • Poor body shapes

Signs of malnutrition.

  • Loss of weight
  • Swollen body
  • Change of hair colour and texture
  • Loss of appetite
  • Dullness
