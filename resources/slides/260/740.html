Energy giving foods (Carbohydrates).

This is the food which helps us to be strong.

Examples.

Cassava

Rice

Posho 

Yams

Bread

Potatoes

Eggs   

Maize

 

Other energy giving foods are:-

Bananas                      Millet                                       

Milk                 Sugarcane                   Glucose              Honey

 

Also fats give us energy for example:-

Butter               Ground nuts oil                        Milk fats          Meat fats         Ghee

 

Note:  Lack of carbohydrates in the body causes a disease called marasmus.
