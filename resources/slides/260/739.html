Classes of food.

There are three classes of food.

  1. Body building foods (proteins)
  2. Energy giving foods (carbohydrates)
  3. Health giving foods (Vitamins/ protective food)

Body building foods (Proteins).

These are foods which help us to grow well.

Examples:

Fish, beans, milk, eggs, groundnuts, chicken, meat, peas, cheese
