PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE

Verbs which double the last letter.

get                    getting                                   shut                 shutting

sit                     sitting                                     swim                swimming

dig                    digging                                     mop                 mopping

run                    running                                    win                   winning

cut                    cutting                                    shop                 shopping

stop                  stopping                                   put                    putting

skip                  skipping                                    clap                   clapping

Activity

Add   ing  to the words in brackets and fill in the gaps.

  1. The man is ______________ in the garden. (dig)
  2. The children are ___________________their hands.  (clap)
  3. We are ____________________to school. (run)
  4. She is ______________________on the chair. (sit)
