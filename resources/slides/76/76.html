Use of ‘has’ and ‘have’

Has is used for one thing and pronouns she, it, he

Have is used for more than one thing and pronouns I, you, they, we

Examples

a)       I have a good bag.

b)       The boys and girls have black shoes.

c)       She has a car.

d)       I have two ears.
