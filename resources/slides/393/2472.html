AFRICA'S CHALLENGES.

Africa faces many challenges in the category of social, political and economic.

Economic challenges

  1. Corruption
  2. Unemployment
  3. Low level of technology
  4. Brain Drain
  5. Inflation
  6. Production of similar goods
  7. Poverty
  8. Debt burden

Political problems

  1. Civil wars
  2. Neo-colonialism
  3. Dictatorship
  4. Over dependence on foreign aid

Social challenges.

  1. Famine
  2. Diseases
  3. Illiteracy
  4. Early marriages (high Birth rates)
  5. High infant mortality rate
  6. Disunity
  7. Rapid population growth.
