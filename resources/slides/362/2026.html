IRREGULAR ADJECTIVES

These change the original word.

1.  bad              worse               worst

2.  good             better                best

3.  well              better                best

4.  far                further               furthest

5.  little              less                  least

6.  much            more                 most

7.  many            more                 most

8.  ill                 worse               worst

9.  far                farther               farthest

10.  old             elder                 eldest  

ACTIVITY

Use the correct degree of adjective given in the brackets to complete the sentences

  1. Michael is the _____________________ boy in our class. (good)
  2. Joseph is _______________________ than Richard.  (heavy)
  3. The box is _______________________ than that box.  (light)
  4. My house is ________________________ than yours.  ( near)
  5. Which is the _____________________ building in Nairobi.  ( tall)
  6. Peter is ______________________ than George.  ( weak)
  7. Mary is the _____________________ girl in our class.  ( short)
  8. Please give me _____________________ milk.  ( much)
  9. This flower is ____________________ than that flower. (beautiful)
  10. James is the _______________________ of the two boys. (clever)
