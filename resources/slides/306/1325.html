ABBREVIATIONS AND CONTRACTIONS

Abbreviations are short forms of words, names, titles, organisations or a group of words

Requirements for abbreviations

Full stops on some abbreviations e.g. i.e., st.a.metc

In case of abbreviations which can be pronounced as words, full stops are not needed in between them

Example

UWESO – Uganda Women Effort to Save Orphans

SWICO – State Wide Insurance Company

NIC – National Insurance Corporation

BODMAS – Brackets of division, multiplication, addition and subtraction

If it is one word that has been shorted, the full stop comes at the end e.g.

Rev. – Reverend

Hon. Honourable

Dec. – December

Tue. Tuesday

Dept. – department

Capt. – Captain

Examples of abbreviations

Am – before noon/ ante meridiem

Pm – afternoon/ post meridiem

Etc – and so on

e.g. – for example

ptc – private
