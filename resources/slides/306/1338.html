PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE

This tense is used to express actions which are taki g place now and even those that will happen in the future.

Example

  1. I am not going to school.
  2. They are runningvery fast.
  3. Musa is lying to me.
  4. The president is flying to Canada next Monday.
  5. They are sitting for P.L.E tomorrow.
