NEGATIVE AND INTERROGATIVE STATEMENTS

Affirmative to negative statements

Examples

1. We are dancing now.

We are not dancing now.

2. The shop keepers are selling some sugar.

The shop keepers aren’t selling any sugar.

Examples

Affirmative to Interrogative statements

1. They are learning English.

Are they learning English?

2. James is climbing up the pole.

Is James climbing up the pole?
