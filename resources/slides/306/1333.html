PRESENT SIMPLE TENSE: ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE

In active voice, we begin the sentence with the subject while in passive voice, we begin with the object. The voices change according to the tense used.

Example

ACTIVE TO PASSIVE VOICE

1. Kato plays football.

Football is played by Kato.

2. The timekeeper rings the bell on time.

The bell is rung on time by the time keeper
