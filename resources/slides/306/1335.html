ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE

Examples

Active voice

1. She is reading a novel.

A novel is being read by her.

2. Tom is teaching us.

We are being taught by Tom.

Passive voice

1. The garden is being dug by Christine.

Christine is digging a garden.

2. The car is being washed by us.

We are washing a car.
