CONTRACTIONS

A contraction is used to shorten a word by the use of a punctuation mark called an apostrophe (‘) which means that some letters are missing , omitted or left out

Examples

Can’t – cannot

I’d – I had

Won’t – will not

‘re – are

P’se – please

Ne’er – never

Shan’t – shall not
