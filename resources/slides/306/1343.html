PROPER ADJECTIVES

These are adjectives formed from proper nouns.  e.g.

Uganda                         Ugandan

Kenya                           Kenyan

Tanzania                       Tanzanian

Germany                       German

Africa                            African

Angola                          Angolan

America                        American

Egypt                            Egyptian

England                        English

Britain                           British

Belgium                        Belgian

Cameroon                     Cameroonian

Holland                         Dutch

Ireland                          Irish

Greece                          Greeks

Switzerland                   Swiss

Jamaica                        Jamaican

Thailand                        Thai
