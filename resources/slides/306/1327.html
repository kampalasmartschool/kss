PRESENT SIMPLE TENSE (EVERYDAY TENSE)

Verbs are doing words.

Kinds of verbs

  • Regular verbs
  • Irregular verbs
  • Main verbs (principal verbs )
  • Helping verbs
  • Transitive verbs
  • Intransitive verbs

REGULAR VERBS

These are verbs which take “ed”, “d”, or “t” in their past tense.
