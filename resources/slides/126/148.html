CONTENT        :           Punctuation

                                   Use of a comma (,)

Why do we use a comma?

To separate items on the list and to show a pause.

We do not use a comma after     “and”

Examples

  1. Patrick, Jeremy and Sandra are my friends.
  2. My mother bought sweets, cakes, toys and pencils.

Activity

Put a comma where necessary.

  1. My sister gave me an egg a sweet and bread.
  2. I have books pencils and rubbers.
  3. Kato Bbosa and Brian are friends.
  4. We learn English Maths  News and Literacy.

Use of a question mark (?)

We put a question mark at the end of an asking sentence.

Examples

What is your name?

Is this your pencil?

How old are you?
