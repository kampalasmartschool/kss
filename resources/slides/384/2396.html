Diseases.

Communicable

  • Tuberculosis 
  • Influenza
  • Diphtheria
  • Whooping cough (pertussis)  Pneumonia

Non-communicable

  • Emphysema
  • Lung cancer
  • Asthma
  • Bronchitis

Care for the respiratory system.

  • Eat a balanced diet.
  • Perform regular exercise.
  • Eat meals containing low animals fats.
  • Avoid smoking.

Advantages of regular body exercises.

  • The heart muscles grow stronger and larger.
  • The heat delivers more blood to the body muscles.
  • They reduce the level of fats in the body.
  • The risk of high blood pressure and heart diseases is reduced.
  • Ligaments and tendons become stronger and reduce chances of injury.
  • Joints become flexible.
  • Weight is lost.
