Pottery

Pottery is the making of pots.

A person who makes pots is called a potter.

A pottery is also the place where many pots are kept.

Uses of pots.

  • To keep drinking water.
  • Cooking food.
  • Fetching water.
  • Decoration.
  • For sale (sold to get money).
