OCCUPATIONS.

A conversation.

Taj:         Good morning Lucy.

Lucy:      Good morning Taj. How are you?

Taj:         Is your dad at home?

Lucy:      No, he isn’t. He has already gone to his workshop.

Taj:         You mean your dad is a carpenter? I didn’t know.  Mine is a plumber. He fixes and repairs pipes.

Lucy:      That is so good. I too never knew that your day is a plumber. Rafiki’s dad is a florist. He cuts and sells flowers. Mum usually buys flowers from him.

Taj:         All occupations are important because we need one another.
