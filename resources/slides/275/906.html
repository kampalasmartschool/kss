A poem

A sharp cry in our sub county

AIDS    AIDS   AIDS    AIDS

AIDS the killer

Crying in the north

Crying in the south

Crying in the east

Crying in the west

Oh AIDS the killer!

 

Come out all you people!

Fight the killer AIDS

People from the north

People from the crowd

Stand out and fight

AIDS   AIDSAIDSAIDS

Together we shall kick AIDS

Out of our sub-county
