Dialogue

Tim:      Hey Chris! Hey Jack! Look at this! It’s my new diary!

Chris:    Oh! Tim aren’t you lucky! You seem to get all the good things.

Jack:    Will you write in you diary everyday?

Tim:      Yes, I will write in my diary everyday of the week.

Chris:   Supposing you forget.

Tim:      No, I will not forget. Look, each page has the day, date and month of the year.

Jack:    I can see. Here on Monday, you will note the time you got the new diary.

Chris:   And how you came and told us about it.

Tim:      You told me that you will get the diary, Jack. When will you get it?

Jack:    I will get it on Thursday.

Lucy:    If I tell my uncle, he will buy me a diary too. Let me go and ask him now.
