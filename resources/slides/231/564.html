Money

  • Money is what we use to buy things we need. It is in form of coins and paper notes.
  • Different countries have different currencies and the Uganda currency is called shillings (discuss)
  • Ugandan currency is the following denominations.

Coins                 note / paper money

Shs. 50                          shs. 1000

Shs. 100                        shs, 2000

Shs. 200                        shs. 5000

Shs. 500                        shs. 10,000

Shs. 1000                      shs. 20,000
