Bushenyi.

 

9th March, 2017

 

Dear Jane,

Thank you for your letter which was full of news. It is quite interesting to note that farmers in Kigezi use terrace to control soil erosion.

 

In your letter, you wanted to know how cattle keepers in my home district care for their animals. Well, the cattle keepers in Mbarara keep long-horned cattle for milk and meat production. They take tem to the fields to graze every day. Funny enough, even girls and women look after cattle. This activity keeps them out of home from morning to dusk.

The cattle herders wear sandals to guard their feet against thorns and sharp stones. They also carry roasted cassava and water to eat while in the fields.

Sometimes, the cattle stray and destroy other people’s crops. When this happens, the owner of the cattle pays fines.

It is my hope that I have answered your questions. Once more, thank you for replying to my letter. Please, send my regards to your family. God bless you.

 

Yours sincerely,

Tumushabe Japheth​​​​​
