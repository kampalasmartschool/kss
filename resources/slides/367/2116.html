WRITING FORMAL LETTERS

PARTS OF FORMAL LETTERS

1. Writer’s address

Example

Kinderkare elementary school

P.O Box 11798,

Kampala (U)

2. Date

23rd March, 2017

23/3/2017

3. Receiver’s title ad address

The Headteacher

Hope senior school,

P. O Box 6366,

Kampala (U)

4. Salutation

Dear Sir

Dear Madam

Dear Sir/Madam

If you know the person’s title or name, refer to it as Dear Mr. Kiirya, Dear Ms. Kalembe, etc

5. Reference (Re)

An official letter usually has a title or heading.

Re: APPLICATIPN FOR A VACANCY IN SENIOR ONE.

Re: APPLICATIO FOR A FRIENDLY FOOTBALL MATCH

Re: APPLICATION FOR A TEACHING POST

Re: APOLOGY FOR …………..

6. Body/ message( introduction, message, conclusion)

The body contains the message or he information the writer wishes to send or convey. You may begin your letter in the following ways:

I humbly apply for the vacancy in senior one in your school.

I hereby submit my application for the post of ………………….

With reference to your advertisement on the New Vision Newspaper dated……………

7. Complimentary clause

Yours faithfully, truly, sincerely in service, etc

Sign, name, designation (if necessary)
