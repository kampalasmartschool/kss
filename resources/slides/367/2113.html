ACTIVITY

Complete the following sentences by using the correct form of the word in brackets.

  1. The head teacher’s ____________________ is very tricky. (sign)
  2. Jane always writes her letter___________________. (careful)
  3. The new pupils will get their _________________ letters tomorrow. (admit)
  4. Barbara received her ______________________ letter to the party last week. (invite)
  5. I _____________________ the best pupil in the letter writing competition last term. (to be)
  6. The letter was ______________________ to the bank manager. (address)
  7. There were only two ______________________ who applied for the job. (apply)
  8. It was my own ________________________ to apply for the vacancy. (decide)
