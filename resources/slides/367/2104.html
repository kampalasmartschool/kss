Use of ………..hardly ……

Hardly is used to show quantity or near negative i.e. very little, few, or none, It also means unlikely or almost not

Examples

1. There was no one at the post office.

There was hardly any one at the post office.

2. There isn’t any stamp on the envelope.

There is hardly any stamp on the envelope.

3. There is almost no body who has bothered to apply for the job.

There is hardly anybody who has bothered to apply for the job.
