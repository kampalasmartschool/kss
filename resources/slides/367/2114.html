Language structures

Use of: Barely……………………….

Barely is used in negative sentences. It can be used to replace “not” or “almost”.

Examples

1. Your letter cannot be read.

Your letter is barely read.

2. Your handwriting is not legible.

Your handwriting is barely legible.

3. There is almost no body at the post office.

There is barely anybody at the post office.

We can use barely at the beginning to mean “any” , “very few”, or “very little”

Example

Barely 30% of the people applied for the position advertised
