Language structures

Use of………..is likely to………..

……is likely…….. is used when there’s a possibility that something will happen.

Examples

1. Judith may write to me next week

Judith is likely to write to me next week.

2. I may post these letters tomorrow.

I am likely to post these letters tomorrow.

3. They may visit the post office next term.

They are likely to visit the post office next term.
