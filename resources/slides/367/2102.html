ASPECT 5: LETTER WRITING

Vocabulary practice

Address, first name, personal letter, stamp, envelope, occasion, party, affectionately, sincerely, relatives, friends, classmates, informal, introduction, body, date, reply, purpose, thank, ask, salutation

  • Spell each of the words above correctly.
  • Use each word to construct a meaningful sentence orally.

ACTIVITY

Use the correct form of the word in bracket to complete the sentences.

  1. Lucy is ______________ to write a letter tomorrow. (like)
  2. The letter was _______________ to the head teacher. (address)
  3. I __________________a personal letter now. (write)
  4. Whenever I post a letter, I pay the _______________ fee. (post)
  5. When you address somebody by their name you may end with yours _______________ (sincere)
  6. Jane ended the letter to her aunt with yours ________________ (affectionate)
  7. Taj received a ________________ letter yesterday. (person)
  8. She usually writes to me an ______________ letter. (inform)
