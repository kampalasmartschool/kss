RUSTING.

Rust is reddish brown substance that forms on metal when a metal is exposed to oxygen and water.

Note: Oxygen and water are the conditions needed for rusting.

Examples of metals.

  • Iron , steel , Aluminum , copper

Ways of controlling rusting.

  • By painting.
  • By greasing / oiling.
  • By enameling.
  • By galvanizing.
  • By keeping metals in cool and dry places.

Dangers of rusting.

  • It weakens metals.
  • It makes metals blunt.
  • It makes water in metallic tanks poisonous.
  • It spoils and changes the colour of metal.
