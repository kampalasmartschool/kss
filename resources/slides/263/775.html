SOIL EROSION.

Soil erosion is the removal of top soil by soil erosion agents.

Agents of erosion.

  • Running water 
  • Strong wind
  • Animals

Types of erosion.

  • Rill erosion
  • Gulley erosion

Rill erosion

Gulley erosion

 

  • Splash erosion

Causes of soil erosion.

  • Over stocking
  • Mono cropping
  • Bush burning
  • Deforestation

Ways of controlling soil erosion.

a) In the compound

  • By planting grass in the compound
  • By planting trees (afforestation) in the compound.

(b)In the garden

  • By mulching
  • By crop rotation
  • Afforestation
  • Contour ploughing
  • Planting trees/ grass
  • Planting cover crops
  • Intercropping
  • Agro forestry
  • Bush farrowing

c) In hilly areas

  • By terracing
  • By contour ploughing.
