Ways of preventing accidents at school.

  • Don’t run any how.
  • Avoid bad plays.
  • Don’t push your friends.
  • Avoid climbing trees and walls.

  • Playing in safe places.
  • Don’t run with hot food.
  • Don’t touch or step on open electric wires.

  • Lameness.
  • Blindness.
  • Sickness.
  • Death.
