Using ….although…….

Examples

1. Lucy talked loudly, but few people heard what she said

Few people heard what Lucy said although she talked loudly.

Although Lucy talked loudly, few people heard what she said.

2. The neighbours were invited to our family thanksgiving. They did not attend.

Although the neighbours were invited to our family thanksgiving, they did not attend it.

The neighbours did not attend our family thanksgiving although they were invited.
