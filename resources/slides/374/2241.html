Using ……whereas….

Whereas is used to show that the things or people we are comparing are different.

Examples

1. The chairperson allowed Rukeba. He did not express his opinion.

Rukeba didn’t express his opinion whereas the chairperson allowed him.

2. Jake chaired the family meeting. His sister recorded the minutes.

Jake chaired the meeting whereas his sister recorded the minutes.

3. The patient was early. The doctor was late.

Whereas the patient was early, the doctor was late.
