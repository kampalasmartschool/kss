STRUCTURES

Use of even though……..

Examples

1. The cake was not mine. I ate it.

  • I ate the cake even though it was not mine.
  • Even though the cake was not mine, I ate it.

2. Tom went to the bakery. He did not buy bread.

  • Tom did not buy bread even though he went to the bakery.
  • Even tough Tom went to the bakery, he did not buy bread.
