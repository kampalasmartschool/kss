Use of commas:

  1. A comma is used to separate words written in a list.

Examples:

a. Suzan , Sarah, Carol  and Brenda are all in class.

b. We went to the shop and bought sugar, tea leaves, butter, bread and soap.

c. A comma is also used after No, Yes and Please in a sentence.

Example:

a. No, I did not pick your pencil.

b. Yes, she is my friend.

c. Please, tell me the story.

Activity

Punctuate these sentences using commas.

  1. I went to the market to buy oranges, tomatoes, bananas and beans.
  2. Peter Mark Joel  Annet and Jonah are in the field.
  3. Yes I went to the village last month.
  4. No I don’t like playing football.
  5. At the zoo, we saw lions tigers monkeys zebras and a pea cock.
