QUESTION TAGS

Question tags are short questions that are put at the end of sentences. It is use when you want some one to agree with you.

  • A positive statement gives a negative tag e.g. You are smart, aren’t you?
  • A negative statement gives appositive question tag e.g. You are not smart, are you?

Examples

Positive statements

  1.  She will clean the classroom, won’t she?
  2. We shall collect the rubbish, shan’t we?
  3. The dog will guard our home, won’t it?

Negative statements

  1. She will not clean the classroom, will she?
  2. We shall not collect the rubbish, shall we?
  3. The dog will not guard our home, will it?
