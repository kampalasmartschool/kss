Writing personal letters

Steps to follow (using block method)

Writer’s address

Date

Greeting / salutation e.g. Dear John

Body (message to the addressee)

Ending the letter e.g. your friend,

Signature (name of writer in small letters)

ACTIVITY

Using your school address, write a letter to your best friend inviting him/her to your birthday party. State the date, venue and time of the party.
