POEM

Read the poem and answer questions about it in full sentences.

 

I received a letter,

From my classmates,

In an envelope sealed it was,

For none but me.

 

I received a letter,

A friendly letter it was,

On a bright Sunday afternoon,

Delivered it was,

By none but a postman.

 

I received a letter,

Decorated with stamp it was,

From the general post office it arrived

For none but me

 

I received a letter,

A thank you letter it was,

For the gift of being humble and honest,

To none but my classmates in primary five

                                    Lindsey Fisk
