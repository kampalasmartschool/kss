LETTER WRITING

Vocabulary practice

Invite, letter, envelope, address, card, occasion, party, guest, host, hostess, ceremony, affection, sincerely, yours, dear, sender, receiver, writer, from, reply

  • Spell each word correctly
  • Use each of the words above in a sentence.

ACTIVITY

Use the correct form of the word given in brackets to complete the sentences.

  1. Malcolm is very good at writing ___________________ letter. (friend)
  2. If this letter is lost and found, please return it to the ____________________. (send)
  3. His friend ____________________ writes to me. (occasion)
  4. The little girl ended her letter with yours _________________________. (sincere)
  5. Peter’s letter has no ______________________. (conclude)
  6. Jovan didn’t receive his ____________________ card to the party. (invite)
