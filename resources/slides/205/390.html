Word statements involving addition of mass

a) Aunt bought 3kg of sugar. Uncle bought 5kg of sugar

How many kilograms did they buy altogether?

Aunt ....       3 kg

Uncle ....  +  5 kg

                  8 kg           They bought 8 kilograms altogether.

b) Joan had 7kg of salt. Dan had 9kg of salt. How many kilograms did they have altogether?

c) Add: 12kg plus 10kg.

d) Jane bought 5kgs of rice. Her mother gave her more 3kgs of rice. How many kilograms of rice does Jane have?

e) Paul bought 4 kgs of maize flour. His brother added him more 3kgs. How many kilograms of maize flour does Paul have?
