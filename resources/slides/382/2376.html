USE OF ‘NEVERTHELESS

Nevertheless is used to express contrast in statements.

Nevertheless is preceded by a semi colon (;) and a comma (,) after

Examples

1. The boys were clever but they didn’t get aggregate four.

The boy was clever; nevertheless he didn’t get aggregate four.

2. The beggar is honest although he s poor.

The beggar is poor; nevertheless, he is honest.
