The past form of needn’t

It has two past forms – didn’t need to/ needn’t have

Use of didn’t need to

It means that what was not necessary was not done

Examples

1. I needn’t go with her.

I didn’t need to go with her.

2. I needn’t take poison, I can die.

I didn’t need to take poison, I could die.
