Similes

Similes are words or phrases that compare something to something else.

Examples

  • As white as snow
  • As agile as a monkey/cat
  • As brave as a lion
  • As cold as ice
  • As a like as two peas
  • As handsome as paint
  • As changeable as weather
  • As new as a day
  • As mean as a miser
  • As quarrelsome as a weasel
  • As open as a smile
  • As busy as a bee
  • As wise as king Solomon
  • As clean as a new pin
  • As honest as a mirror
  • As hungry as a wolf
  • As impatient as a lover
  • As heavy as lead
  • As real as the stars
  • As true a the gospel
  • As vulgar as money
  • As ugly as sin
  • As wide as hope
  • As beautiful as a rainbow
  • As loyal as a dove
