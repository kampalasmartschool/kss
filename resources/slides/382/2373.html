Forming opposites using suffixes

A suffix is a letter or group of letters added to the end of the word in order to change its meaning.

Example

Words ending with “ful” form their opposites by replacing “ful” with “less”

Useful                              useless

Careful                             carless

Powerful                          powerless

Helpful                             helpless

Harmful                            harmless

Painful                             painless

Pitiful                               pitiless

Cheerful                          cheerless

Thankful                          thankless

Boastful                           boastless

Hopeful                            hopeless

Shameful                         shameless

Needful                           needless
