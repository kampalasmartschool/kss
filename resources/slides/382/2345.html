Using ……….whenever…………..

When using whenever in the middle, there is no comma.

Example

1. Every time we go to town, we buy a dictionary.

We buy a dictionary whenever we go to town.

2. They always go to the zoo. They see many elephants.

They see many elephants whenever they go to the zoo.

3. The teacher pronounces the words. The pupils repeat after him.

The pupils repeat after the teacher whenever he pronounces the words.
