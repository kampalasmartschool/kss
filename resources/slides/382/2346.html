Using ……as……..as………..

Examples

1. A dictionary is useful. An atlas is also useful.

A dictionary is as useful as an atlas.

2. A novel is small. A dictionary is bigger.

A novel is not as big as a dictionary.

3. That book weighs 50kg. This book weighs 1kg.

This book is not as heavy as that one.
