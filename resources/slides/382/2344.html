Using: Whenever……..

Rewrite the following sentences using: Whenever……..

Examples

1. Every time you find a new word, refer to the dictionary.

Whenever you find a new word, refer to the dictionary.

Refer to the dictionary whenever you find a new word.

2. Sometime you come across a new word should look up its meaning.

Whenever you come across a new word, you should look up its meaning.

You should look up its meaning whenever you come across a new word.
