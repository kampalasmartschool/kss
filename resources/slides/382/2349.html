GUIDED COMPOSITION

Read the poem below and answer questions that follow in full sentences.

A dictionary is a vital reference,

It teaches vocabulary,

It gives meanings and spellings,

It is really a teacher,

Away from school!

 

A dictionary is a vital reference,

It the library, it lies,

With it, I can learn parts of speech,

With it, I can find describing words,

With it, I can learn proverbs.

 

A dictionary is a vital reference,

With it I can learn pronouns,

With it I can master pronunciations,

With it I can write abbreviations in full,

With it I can guess the correct preposition!

                                    By Among Viola
