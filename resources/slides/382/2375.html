Using: Despite

The structure words the same way ‘in spite’ does but it doesn’t take ‘of’

Despite is usually used with abstract nouns like sickness, wisdom, intelligence, etc.

Examples

1. Mukasa married a beautiful lady yet he is not educated.

Mukasa married a beautiful lady despite being uneducated.

Despite the fact that Mukasa married a beautiful lady, he is not educated.
