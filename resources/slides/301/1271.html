HAZARDS THAT CHECK ON POPULATION GROWTH.

a) NATURAL HAZARDS / DISASTERS.

  • Pests and diseases.
  • Floods.
  • Earth quakes.
  • Stronger  winds.
  • Drought.
  • Volcanic  eruption.
  • Lightening.
  • Tsunami /Tornados.
  • Land slides (mud slides)

b) MAN MADE HARZARDS/DISASTERS.

  • Wars.

  • Road accidents.

  • Water and food poisoning.

  • Fire outbreaks.

  • Over Population.
