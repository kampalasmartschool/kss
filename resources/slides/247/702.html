Lesson 2.

Senses and Sense Organs.

We have five senses. these are:-

  1. Seeing
  2. Smelling
  3. Tasting
  4. Feeling
  5. Hearing

We use eyes to see.

We use ears to hear.

We use the nose to smell.

We use the skin to feel.

We use the tongue to taste.
