Common nouns

What are common nouns?

Common nouns are names given to all people, places and things at same kind.

They can either be plural or singular e.g. (countable nouns and uncountable nouns)

boys , girls, birds ,tree, desk,  people,  sugar,  etc.

           

              women                                animals                              stones

What are countable nouns?

These are things which we can count.

Examples

Singular                      plural

Boy                               boys

Girl                               girls

Stone                             stones

Teacher                        teachers

Desk                            desks

Monkey                       monkeys

Donkey                         donkeys

Countable nouns that take ‘es’ in plural

Singular                plural

Church                 churches

Bench                   benches

Watch                  watches

Dish                     dishes

Match                   matches

Note: nouns that end with o, x, ch, s, sh take ‘es’ to form their plurals
