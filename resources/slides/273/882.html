Collective nouns

What are collective nouns?

A collective noun is a name given to a group or things taken as one.

Examples of collective nouns   

A herd of cattle

 

A flock of sheep

 

A bouquet of flowers

 
  1. A gang of thieves
  2. A choir of singers
  3. A congregation of worshippers
  4. A troupe of dancers
  5. A troop of keys
  6. A team of players
