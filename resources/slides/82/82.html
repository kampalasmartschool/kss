Punctuation

Capital letters

We use capital letters when;

a)       Beginning sentences

b)       Starting asking sentences

c)       Starting names of people, places, titles of books, plays, films etc.

d)       Writing days of the week and months of the year.

Examples

1.       Where do you live?

2.       She is a good girl.

3.       Yesterday was a Tuesday.
