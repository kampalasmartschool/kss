ABSTRACT NOUNS

Abstract nouns are names of things which denote an idea of state. They cannot be seen, felt or touched. They cannot also stand on their own. They are formed from other words like adjectives, verbs e.g. beautiful, ugly, poverty, patience, age, death, power, truth

FORMATION OF ABSTRACT NOUNS FROM VERBS

VERB                              ABSTRACT NOUNS

sit                                            seat

speak                                       speech

choose                                     choice

accuse                                     accusation

abolish                                     abolition

punish                                      punishment

pay                                           payment
