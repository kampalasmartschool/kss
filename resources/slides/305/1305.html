Use of so…that…..

Examples

1. The vehicle was very fast. The children could not cross the road.

The vehicles were so fast that the children could not cross the road.

2. My grandmother is very old. He can’t walk without a stick

My grandmother is so old that he can’t walk without a stick.

Use of ………so………

Example

1. Lydia wanted to cross the road. She asked the traffic officer to help her.

Lydia wanted to cross the road, so she asked the traffic officer to help her.

2. It became dark. He turned o the headlights.

It became dark so he turned on the headlights.
