COLLECTIVE NOUNS

These are names given to groups of things collected together.

Examples

  1. A swarm of bees.
  2. Herd of cattle
  3. A crowd of spectators
  4. A suite of furniture
  5. A bench of Bishops
  6. A host of angles.
  7. A gang of thieves.
  8. A flock of sheep
  9. A set of furniture
  10. A forest of trees
  11. A troupe of dancers
  12. A company of actors
  13. A choir of singers
  14. A congregation of worshippers
  15. A bunch of keys
  16. A cluster of bananas
  17. A mouthful of food
  18. A library of books
  19. A pocketful of money
  20. A flight of birds
  21. A fleet of cars
  22. A heap of soil
  23. A bouquet of flowers
  24. A brood of chicken
