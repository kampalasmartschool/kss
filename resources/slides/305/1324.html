REFLECTIVE PRONOUNS

Reflexive pronouns are pronouns tat refer back to the subject of the sentences of clause.

They either end in ‘self’ in singular form or in ‘selves’ in plural.

Examples

  • My self
  • Herself
  • Him self
  • Oneself
  • Itself
  • Ourselves
  • Themselves
  • Yourself
