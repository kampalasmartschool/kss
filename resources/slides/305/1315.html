Formation of plurals by adding “ves” to nouns that end in “f” or “fe”

Singular                                               Plural

knife                                                     knives

wife                                                      wives

calf                                                      calves

loaf                                                      loaves

EXCEPTIONS

roof                                         Roofs

chief                                         Chiefs

hoof                                         hoofs

handkerchief                             handkerchiefs

reef                                         reefs    

dwarf                                        dwarfs / dwarves

scarf                                         scarfs/ scarves

  1. ________________ should be kept away from children. (knife)
