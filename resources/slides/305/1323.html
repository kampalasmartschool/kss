ADJECTIVE AND POSSESSIVE PRONOUNS

Adjective pronouns

Example

  • My
  • Your
  • Her
  • His
  • Its
  • Our
  • Their

Possessive pronoun

A possessive pronoun is used instead of a noun.

There are no apostrophes in possessive nouns.

Example

  • Julie’s car is red. Mine is blue.
  • My car is bigger than her car.
