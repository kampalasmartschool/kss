NOUNS

GENDER: This is the fact of being male, female or none. Gender can be grouped into three;

Gender can be identified in three ways

Gender formed by adding a prefix e.g.

Masculine                     Feminine                      

Cock – sparrow             hen- sparrow                

He – goat                      she- goat                      

Pea-cock                       pea – hen                     

Common gender

  • Baby
  • Child
  • Doctor

Neuter gender

  • Tree
  • Desk
  • Water

Gender formed by adding a suffix e.g.

Masculine                   Feminine

Actor                             actress

Heir                              heiress

Gender formed form a completely different noun

Masculine                     Feminine

Drake                           duck

Bull                               cow

Neuter:

 These are nouns which are neither nether female nor masculine. E.g. tree, desk, window, stone, table, water, book etc
