The use of .............enough to …………………..

Enough to is usually used after an adjectives or adverbs.

Examples

1. He ran fast. He won the race.

He was fast enough to win the race.

2. The wind was very strong. It blew off the roof of the house.

The wind was strong enough to blow off the roof of the house.

3. The shop keeper was very foolish. He did not save any money.

The shop keeper was not wise enough to save any money.
