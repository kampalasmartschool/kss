Game parks and reserves in Africa.

  • Game Park is a large area of land gazzeted by government to preserve wildlife for public and future generations to see and admire.

  • Game Reserves are areas of land gazzeted by government or put aside by government for future development e.g Expansion of a National Park or a human settlement scheme.

 

Importance of Game Parks in Africa.

  • They preserve wild life for future generation.

  • They attract tourists who pay foreign exchange to the country.

  • They are sources of Educational information. [For study purposes]

  • They provide employment to people.

  • It facilities the development of transport system. e.g Air Transport.

Problems facing Game Parks in Africa.

  • Poaching.
  • Encroachment.
  • Insecurity.
  • Wild bush fires.
  • Animal diseases vectors / diseases.
  • Poor government policies.
  • Drought.
  • Deforestation – (land encroachment)
  • Poor management.
