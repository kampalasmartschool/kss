Word statements involving addition of metres.

       a)      Joy moves 3 metres. Sarah moved 4 metres.

                 3 metres + 4 metres = 7 metres.

                 They both moved ____________metres

b)      Bursar had 12 metres of a black cloth and 4 metres of a yellow cloth. How many metres of          cloth had the bursar?

 

c)      Tom walked 10 metres and ran 5 metres. How many metres did he move altogether?

 

d)      The tailor had only 14 metres of green material. Then a friend gave her 5 metres more.               How many metres of the green material does the tailor have now?

 

e)      Juma travelled eighteen metres to visit a friend. Then walked 2 metres more. What is the          total distance covered by Juma?
