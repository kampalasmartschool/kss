Money

Subtraction of money (word problems)

Example

Mary had shs 300. He gave away shs 100. How much money did he remain with?

            shs 300

         -  shs 100

            shs 200

Activity

1) Baba had sh. 1000. He bought a book at sh. 700. How much money was he left with?

2) Lucy was  given sh. 500. She spent sh. 250. How much money remained?

3) Taj had shs. 1200. He bought a toy car at sh. 800. How much did he remain with?

4) Mummy had sh.3300. She bought salt at shs 350. How much money did she remain with?
