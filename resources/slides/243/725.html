Measures

Money

Word problems involving division of money

Example

1. Mr. Kasule had shs 800. He shared it equally between his two children. How much did each child get?

 

Acitivity

1. Taj had shs. 2000. He shared it equally between his two friends. How much did each friend get?

 

2. Rudy received 3000 for his birthday. He wants to split the money into groups to save for {three things}: movie tickets; art supplies; music downloads. If he wants the same amount for each activity, how much money will he put into each group?
