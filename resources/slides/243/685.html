Addition of money (word problems)

Examples

I had 200 shillings. My father gave me 50 shillings more. How much money do I have altogether?

I had                        200 shillings

Father gave me     +   50  shillings

I have                      250 shillings

 

Activity 

1) Taj had sh. 250. She was given a nother sh. 300. How much money had he                      altogether?

 

2) Lucy got sh. 500. Her friend gave her sh. 500 more. How much money  does she           have now?

 

3) Uncle had sh.1000. Mummy gave him sh. 200 more. How much money did he have?

 

4) I sold a mango at sh.350 and an avocado at sh.400. How much money did I get?
