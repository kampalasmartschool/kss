2. Minimum and maximum thermometer.

  • It is sometimes called the six’s thermometer because it was first made by James Six.
  • The minimum and maximum thermometer is used to measure the lowest and highest temperature of the day.
  • It uses both alcohol and mercury.

Note:

  1. In a weather station, we find the Stevenson screen where delicate weather instrument are kept.
  2. A Stevenson screen is painted white to reflect heat.
