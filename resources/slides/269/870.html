Types of thermometers.

  • Clinical thermometer
  • Minimum and maximum thermometer.
  • Scientific / clinical thermometer
  • Wall thermometer

1.    Clinical thermometer.

- It is used in hospitals by doctors, nurses, etc. to measure the human body temperature.

- The temperature of a human body must remain constant at 370C or 98.40F except when the person is sick or has fever.

The clinical thermometer is placed in the following places:-

  • In the mouth under the tongue to prevent biting and breaking the bulb.
  • In the arm pit.
  • In the anus
  • In the vagina.

NB: These parts maintain the temperature.
