Forming plurals of nouns which end with ‘y’ by adding – ‘s’

Examples:

bay                   bays                            

boy                   boys

donkey              donkeys

key                   keys                            

tray                   trays

turkey               turkeys                        

Practice

  1. Identify nouns in the group.
  2. Spell the plurals of the nouns.

Nouns that end with ‘f’ that change to ‘ves’

Knife – knives

Wife – wives

Life – lives

Wolf – wolves

Thief – thieves

Loaf – loaves
