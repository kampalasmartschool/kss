Forming plurals of nouns by dropping ‘y’ and adding – ‘ies’

Examples:

baby                 babies

story     -           stories

fly                     flies

ferry                  ferries

puppy               puppies

lady                  ladies

family               families

library               libraries

city                   cities

party                 parties

fairy                  fairies

lorry                  lorris

Practice

  1. Read the words above and their plurals.
  2. Spelling the plurals of the words.

Activities

Complete the sentences giving the plural of the words in the brackets.

  1. The nurse treated many _______ in the hospital. (baby)
  2. Many _______ came from the rubbish pit. (fly)
  3. Those __________ were very smart for the party. (lady)
  4. _________ feed on milk. (puppy)
  5. Kampala and Nairobi are _________( city)
