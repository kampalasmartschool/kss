If the singular ends in ’y’ preceded by a consonant change ‘y’ into ‘i’ before adding ‘es’

Examples

Singular         Plural           Singular                 Plural

City               cities             company            companies

Lady              ladies           country               countries

Duty              duties           industry              industries

Fly                flies               society               societies

Factory         factories       puppy                 puppies

Party             parties          penny                 pennies

Spy               spy                family                families

Vacancy       vacancies

If the singular ends in ‘y’ preceded by a vowel, simply add ‘s’

Example 

Singular                plural

monkey                  monkeys

donkey                   donkeys

chimney                 chimneys

trolley                     trolleys

turkey                    turkeys

day                        days

jockey                    jockeys

key                        keys

A few nouns form their plural by changing their inside vowels.

Examples 

Singular                        plural ​​​​​​

man                              men

woman                         women

tooth                             teeth

louse                             lice

mouse                           mice

goose                           geese

There are two nouns that form their plural by adding ‘en’ to their singular i.e.

Singular                plural

ox                          oxen

Child                     children
