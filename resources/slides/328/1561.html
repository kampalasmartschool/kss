ACTIVITY

Use the correct form of the words in brackets to complete the sentences below.

  1. This chair is more _______________________ than that. (comfortable)
  2. He doesn’t have the ______________________ to admit when he is wrong. (humble)
  3. Many people in Africa are living in ___________________. (poor)
  4. Joan is a very _____________________ friend. (reliable)
  5. What is the ____________________ between your bag and mine? (different)
  6. People voted for John because of his ________________________. (generous)
  7. She won the Miss Uganda contest because of her _________________. (beautiful)
  8. ______________________ leads to nothing but poverty. (poverty)
  9. Jack and Jill’s ________________________ grew over time. (friend)
