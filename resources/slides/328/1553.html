NOUNS

A noun is a naming word or a name given to an object, an action, quality, an idea

Types of nouns

  1. Common nouns
  2. Proper nouns
  3. Abstract nouns
  4. Collective nouns

PROPER NOUNS

A proper noun is a particular name of a person , a thing or a place

       Example

Name of a;

Person      -       Edward

Country     -      Uganda

River        -       River Nile

Lake        -       Lake Victoria

Mountain -       Mt. Elgon

City         -        Nairobi

Day        -        Sunday

All proper nouns must be written beginning with capital letters
