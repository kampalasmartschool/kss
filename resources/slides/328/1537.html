Speeches

Direct and indirect (reported) speech

Examples

1. Direct: “Where are you going to spend your holidays, Lucy?” The teacher asked.

  1.     The teacher wanted to know where Jane was going to spend her holidays.

2. Direct: “Will you help your parents, Tom?” asked his friend.

  1.     Tom’s friend asked him if he would help his parents.

3. Direct:“I am going for holidays now”, said Joyce.

  1.     Joyce said that she was going for holidays then.
