ACTIVITY

Use the correct form of the word given in brackets to complete the sentences.

  1. Lucy __________________ her parents every morning. (greet)
  2. The farmers in our village _________________ plant their crops during the rainy season. (usual)
  3. It is very _________________________ to travel by train. (interest)
  4. Helping our parents during the holidays is _______________________. (volunteer)
  5. It was such an ________________________ journey that everybody enjoyed it. (interest)
  6. He was a _____________________ pupil because of his hard work. (success)
  7. School children usually go for holidays ____________________ a year. (three)
