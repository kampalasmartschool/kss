Language Structures

………….going to…………

Near future

Examples

1. I will visit my grandparents during the holidays.

I am going to visit my grandparents during holidays.

2. Tom will travel up country when holidays commence.

Tom is going to travel to up country when holidays commence.

3. Lucy will enjoy her brother’s birthday party.

Lucy is going to enjoy her brother’s birthday party.
