Valleys in our district.

A valley is a low land between two close hills/ mountains.

Valley                                             Important feature

Bat valley                                  Bat Valley Theatre/ Primary School

Nakivubo valley                         St. Balikuddembe Market, Nakivubo stadium

Kitante valley                            Golf Club, Garden City, Kitante Schools

Katonga valley                           Hostels

Importance of valleys.

We get clay for pottery.

Brick making is done in valleys.

Fishing in springs, streams, rivers is done in valleys.

Papyrus for crafts and building are got from valleys.

Forests in valleys are homes of some wild animals.

Hunting is done in valleys.

Valleys are sources of some rivers and streams.

Farming is carried out in valleys.

Swamps in Kampala district.

A swamp is a wetland with vegetation.

Importance of swamps.

Water for domestic use can be got from swamps.

Fish can be got from swamps.

People can grow crops like rice, sugarcane, yams in swamps.

Swamps help in the formation of rainfall.

Papyrus reeds for making crafts can be got from swamps.

Swamps are homes of some wild animals.

Problems faced by people living in swampy areas.

Easy spread of diseases.

The area can easily flood.

People experience coldness especially at night.

People can easily be attacked by wild animals.

Water in swamps weakens buildings.
