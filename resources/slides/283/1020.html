Lakes and rivers.

a) LAKES

Lakes are larger mass of water in a basin. A lake is bigger than a well.

Examples of lakes in Uganda

Lake Victoria (biggest lake in Uganda)

Kabaka’s lake (man made lake in Rubaga division)

Lake Kyoga.

Lake Albert.

Lake Wamala.

Lake Edward.

Lake George.

Lake Mburo.

 

b) RIVERS.

A river is a mass of flowing water.

Rivers are small but longer than most lakes.

Examples of rivers.

River Nile, Mpongo, Kwania, Mayanja, Unyama, Acwa,

NOTE: River Nile is the longest river in Uganda.
