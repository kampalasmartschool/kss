Question mark:

Questions are used at the end of sentences which are in question form.

Examples

  1. Why are you late?
  2. Will you be here tomorrow?
  3. Is she going to school?
  4. What is your name?

Practice

  • Mention some of the words used to ask questions.
  • Forming questions orally.
