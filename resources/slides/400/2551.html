AT THE BANK

Mrs. Baba:        Good morning, sir.

Cashier:            Good morning, madam. Can I help you, please?

Mrs. Baba:        Yes, sir. I would like to learn about banking.

Cashier:            That’s right, madam. Have a seat, please.

Mrs. Baba:        Excuse me sir, what dos a cashier do?

Cashier:            A cashier receives money from customers who are depositing into their accounts and gives out money to those who come to withdraw.

Mrs. Baba:        Is any body allowed to get money from the bank?

Cashier:            Of course no. the cashier gives out money to only those who have accounts with this bank.

Mrs. Baba:        What do I need to open up an account with your bank, sir?

Cashier:            For you to open up an account with our bank, you need to fill an application form, two passport size photographs and a letter from L.C                          1 or your employer.

Mrs. Baba:        Suppose I don’t know how to read and write, how do I fill the form?

Cashier:            If you do not know how to read and write, a member of your staff can help you to fill the form.

Mrs. Baba:        Is it necessary for the customer or applicant to sign the form after filling it?

Cashier:            Oh yes, a customer or an applicant to sign after filling the form.

Mrs. Baba:        Imagine the applicant or a customer doesn’t have a signature, what does she do?

Cashier:            A customer or an applicant who doesn’t have the signature uses a thumb print or finger print.

Mrs. Baba:        Thank you very much fro helping me, Sir. Have a nice time.

Cashier:            You are welcome. Have a nice time, too.
