LEGENDS

  1. The spear and the bead.
  2. Kintu and Nambi.
  3. Mundu and Seera.

The spear and the bead:

  • Gipir and Labongo were sons of Olum.
  • The elephant ran away with the spear.
  • Rubanga (the spirit) helped Gipir to look for Labong’s spear.
  • The descendants of Labong are the Acholi and Gipir are the Alur respectively.

Lesson learnt from the legend of the spear and the bead

  • To be kind.
  • To ask for permission.
  • Revenge is bad and can lead to death.
