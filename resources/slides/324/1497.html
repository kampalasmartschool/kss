Word problems involving multiplication by one digit.

Example. 1.        

Juma  is paid shs. 6960 a day.  How much will he get if he works for 7 days.

            Solution:

                        1  day he gets shs. 6960

                        7 days he gets     6 9 6 0

                                                   x        7

                                        Shs. 4 8 7 2  0

He  gets  48, 720  in 7  days.

Multiplication as repeated addition.

Example:

(a)        4  x  2    =    2  +  2  +  2  +  2

                          =          8

(b)        3  +  3  +  3  +  3        =    4  x  3 

                                             =      12
