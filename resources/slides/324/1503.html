Number sequence by Adding.

Example.

(a)        (1,  3,  5,  7,  9,  ___,  ___)

            [Keep adding 2]

1 + 2    =    3

3 + 2    =    5

5 + 2    =    7

7 + 2    =    9

9+ 2     =   11

11 + 2  =   13

The missing numbers are 11 and 13

 

(b)        (1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8, ____)

            [Add 1 then add 2]

Begin with 

            1  +  1  =  2

            2  +  2  =  4

            4  +  1  =  5

            5  +  2  =  7

            7  +  1  =  8

            8  +  2  =  10

The missing number is 10

NOTE:   Every  sequence has its own pattern
