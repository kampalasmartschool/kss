Grammar

The past perfect tense

Here, it is the use of auxiliary verb “had” and the perfect of the main verb. It doesn’t matter whether it is a singular or plural subject.

Examples

  1. I had taken the chance already.
  2. By the time Doris got to the party, everyone had already gone home.
  3. Harold had known about it for a while.
