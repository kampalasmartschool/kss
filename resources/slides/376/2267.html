Using:……….too…..to

Examples

1. Nalugwa’s aunt is very old. She cannot remember anything.

Nalugwa’s aunt is too old to remember anything.

2. Kato is weak. He cannot do good work.

Kato is too weak to do good work.

3. The food is very hot. The baby can’t eat it now.

The food is too hot for the baby to eat now.
