ASPECT: ADVERBS

Adverbs modify a verb by giving the following information

  1. How the action occurs. (manner)
  2. Where the action occurs. (place)
  3. How many times it occurs (frequency)
  4. At what time ( time)
  5. Intensity of the actions. (degree

ACTIVITY

Fill in the gaps with a correct form of the word in brackets.

  1. He _____________________ beats his wife. (regular)
  2. “Get out of my office,” the headteacher told me _______________________. (angry)
  3. Linda ____________________ visits us at least once a week when she is staying in town. (usual)
  4. On receiving the call, the police went to the scene ______________________. (immediate)
  5. Students should always dress _____________________________. (decent)
  6. ________________________ had the chairman finished his speech when disorder broke out among the audience. (hard)
  7. We have packed the goods very ______________________. (careful)
  8. He ______________________ escaped the fire through the window. (narrow)
  9. Baba milks his cows ____________________. (everyday)
  10. The tourists travelled ____________________. (safe)
