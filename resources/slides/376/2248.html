Affirmative and negative statements

An affirmative sentence is a sentence that is positive instead of negative while negative sentence is a sentence that states that something is false.

Examples

1. Affirmative:            I was made to lie down yesterday.

Negative:                I wasn’t made to lie down yesterday.

2. Affirmative:            He got a first grade.

Negative:                He did not get a first grade.
