ASPECT: VERBS AND TENSES

CONTENT: Future Perfect Tense

Here we use either “shall have” or “will have” plus the perfect of the main verb. e.g.

  1. I shall have understood tenses by the end of is topic.
  2. He will have taken a bus to Malindi.

ACTIVITY

Use the given verbs in the future perfect (resent)

  1. The Primary One pupils ____________________ how to read and write by the end of Primary Two. (learn)
  2. By the beginning of the lesson, the teacher _________________________ the classroom. (enter)
  3. Those children _____________________________ for six hours by 2:00 p.m. (swim)
  4. This bee _________________________ one hundred people by the time it does. (sting)
  5. By tomorrow, she __________________________ five baskets. (weave)
  6. By the end of the year, I __________________________ a house of my own. (build)
  7. The congregation _____________________________ prayed hard by morning. (pray)
  8. I ________________________ the lorry p to Mbale by the time he leaves Kampala. (drive)
  9. We __________________________ for first grade by the end of the year. (strive)
