CONTENT:  QUESTION TAGS (FUTURE PERFECT)

Question tags are short questions that at the end of sentences

Examples

  1. I shall have cleaned the desk by next week, shan’t I?
  2. The manager will have arrived by mid-day, won’t he?
  3. We shall have done the test by tomorrow, shan’t we?
