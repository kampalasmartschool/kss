Use of ……..just….

Examples

1. The children reached school as the bell was rung.

The children have just reached school.

2. My aunt gave birth a while ago.

My aunt has just given birth.

3. Your niece got married to an Ethiopian a while ago

Your niece has just got married to an Ethiopian.
