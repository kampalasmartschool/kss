A verb is a doing word. Verbs are subdivided into two groups; regular and irregular

Regular verbs

These form their past and participle by adding a ‘d’ or ‘ed’

Examples

Regular verbs which take ‘d’

Present Simple              Past Simple                  Past Perfect

believe                                believed                        believed

deceive                               deceived                       deceived

receive                                received                        received

escape                                escaped                        escaped

arrange                               arranged                        arranged

arrive                                  arrived                          arrived

move                                  moved                          moved

complete                           completed                     completed

compare                           compared                      compared

change                               changed                        changed

use                                    used                             used

live                                    lived                             lived

Regular verbs which take ‘ed’

Present Simple                 Past Simple                   Past Perfect

walk                                   walked                          walked

wait                                    waited                           waited

work                                   worked                          worked

add                                     added                           added

allow                                   allowed                         allowed

hang                                   hanged                          hanged

laugh                                  laughed                         laughed

talk                                      talked                           talked

roll                                      rolled                            rolled

cook                                   cooked                          cooked

dance                                 danced                          danced

pack                                   packed                          packed

 

Sometimes the last consonant must be doubled as in plan – planned.

Examples

Present Simple                  Past Simple                   Past Perfect

plan                                    planned                         planned

slap                                    slapped                         slapped

stop                                    stopped                         stopped

slip                                      slipped                          slipped

 

Regular verbs that end with ‘t’

Present Simple                 Past Simple                   Past Perfect

mean                                  meant                           meant  

spoil                                   spoilt                            spoilt

dream                                 dreamt                          dreamt

learn                                   learnt                            learnt

burn                                    burnt                             burnt

leap                                    leapt                             leapt

deal                                    dealt                             dealt

sweep                                 swept                           swept

Use the correct form of the verb given in bracket to complete the sentences.

  1. They were _____________________ by the Arabs in Mombasa. (slave)
  2. He has just ______________________ his second house. (build)
  3. They ________________________ the hare to the fox. (comparison)
  4. He ______________________ before going to the chapel to pray. (bathe)
  5. The girls’ action _____________________ madness. (depiction)
  6. He _____________________ his career until his death. (pursue)
  7. The terrorists will be _______________________ with according to the law. (deal)
  8. Johnny _______________________ all his life as a hunter. (live)
  9. Jimmy has finally _____________________ down the isle with his bride. (walk)
  10. My entire plantation was _______________________ by wild fire. (clear)
