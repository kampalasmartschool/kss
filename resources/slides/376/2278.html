CONTENT: If 2 sentences

In the if II we use a past simple or past continuous tense in the if clause and a future simple past in the main clause (would clause)

Example

1. John’s seeing a snake under my bed. My running away.

If John saw a snake under my bed, I would run away.

2. Mugaga’s coming from a rich family, his passing so much.

If Mugaga was coming from a rich family, he would pass so much.
