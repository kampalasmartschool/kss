Use of ……is….but….is……….

Examples

1. Hidaya is my niece. Her brother is my nephew.

Hidaya is my niece but her brother is my nephew.

2. Mundu is old. Seera is young.

Mundu is old but Seera is young.

3. Tommy is very tall. His father is a short man.

Tommy is very tall but his father is a short man.
