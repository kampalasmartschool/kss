CONTENT: INTERROGATIVE STATEMENTS

Change to interrogative form.

Examples

1. You will have gone by the time the office opens.

Will you have gone by the time the office opens?

2. We shall have listened to the teacher for half an hour by the end of the lesson.

Shall we have listened to the teacher for half an hour by the end of the lesson?
