CONTENT: Active and Passive voice

Active voice describes a sentence where the subject performs the action stated by the verb. In passive voice sentences, the subject is acted upon by the verb.

Examples

1. The dogs chased a goat.

A goat was chased by the dogs.

2. It ate a rat last week.

A rat was eaten last week.

3. Tom dug a deep pit last week.

A deep pit was dug by Tom.

Change the following sentences into active voice e.g.

a) Football was played yesterday by Sam.

Sam played football yesterday.

b) Food was cooked by Norah last night.

Norah cooked food last night.

c) She collected our books yesterday.

Our books were collected yesterday.
