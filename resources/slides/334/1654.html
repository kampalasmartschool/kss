Types of accidents.

Burns,      Nose bleeding,      Cuts,    Breaking bones,     Falls,     Electric shock,     Drowning,     Choking,   Poisoning,   Bites,    Stings

Burns.

Burns are caused by:-

  • Playing with fire.

  • Careless house keepers.

  • Careless handling of hot objects/things.

  • Limited space in the home/work place.

  • Keeping dangerous fuels in the house.

Falls.

Falls are caused by:-

  • Running carelessly.

  • Rushing with no reason.

  • Rudeness.

  • Playing rough games.

  • Climbing.
