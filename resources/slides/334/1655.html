Cuts.

Cuts are commonly caused by:

  • Playing with sharp objects e.g. nails, razorblades, pins, knives etc.

  • Playing in unsecure places.

  • Playing/walking on rough places.

Poisoning.

Poisoning is commonly caused by:

  • Greediness.

  • Keeping poison near open places.

  • Carelessness.

  • Neglect over some substances.

Bites.

Bites are caused by;

  • Carelessness.

  • Children trying to discover more.

  • Rough playing.

  • Stray animals.

  • Playing in bush/long grass.

  • Bad handling of animals.

Activity:

  1. Name any two causes of poisoning.

  2. List down any three sharp objects.

  3. Give any three animals that cause bites.
