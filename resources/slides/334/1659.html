Preventing accidents in our homes/school.

  • Stop running unnecessarily.
  • Handle sharp objects carefully.
  • Keep away poison/medicine.
  • Wear shoes/gumboots when walking in bad places.
  • Not playing rough games.
  • Not playing near/on water bodies.
  • Not playing with unfamiliar animals.
  • Use medicines as instructed by health worker.
  • Avoid climbing.
  • Follow traffic rules when using roads.
  • Avoid playing in bushes.
  • Not playing with electric wires.
  • Not speaking on phones while driving.
  • Not to drive when drunk.

Activity

Pupils discuss the ways of preventing accidents with the help of the teacher.
