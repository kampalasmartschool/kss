First aid box (kit)

What is a first aid kit?

            A first aid kit is a box where things used to give first aid are kept.

Examples of first aid materials (things).

(i)         Soap                -           Washing hands and around cuts.

(ii)        cotton wool      -           for cleaning the cuts.

(iii)       bandage          -           tying wounds/broken limbs.

(iv)       razorblade       -           cutting plaster, strings etc

(v)        Safety pins      -           removing objects from the body.

(vi)       Iodine/spirit      -         Cleaning cuts to kill germs.

(vii)      Aspirin/panadol-           pain killers.

(viii)     gloves

(ix)       a pair of scissors – for cutting plaster / bandage.

Where to find first aid kits.

-           Hospitals.

-           Schools.

-           Hotels.

-           Vehicles.

-           Homes.

-           Aircrafts etc
