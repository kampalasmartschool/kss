COMPASS DIRECTION.

A compass direction is an element that shows direction of places.

It can also be called a compass rose.

The four main points of a compass are called cardinal points  OR primary points.

N=North

S=South

E=East

W=West 

Points that lie in between the cardinal points are called Secondary points.

N.B:  A compass needle points to the North whenever at rest.

People who use a compass in their daily work.

  • Pilots.

  • Sailors.

  • Mountain climbers.

  • Tourists.

  • Navigators.

  • Soldiers.

  • Land Surveyors.

Other ways of finding direction of places.

  • By using the position of the sun.
  • By using the shadows.
  • By using landmarks.
