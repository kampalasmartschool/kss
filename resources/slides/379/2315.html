POEM

Read the poem below and answer the questions that follow in full sentences.

Wake up fellow youths!

Prepare for tomorrow

By acquiring skills in various occupations

First by joining training schools

 

Wake up fellow youths!

Prepare for our future

By learning carpentry, tailoring or baking

Bearing in mind the scarcity of white collar jobs

 

Wake up fellow youths!

Avoid bad peer groups

That may block our plans

To achieve success in life

 

Wake up fellow youths

Remember to seek career guidance

From our parents or teachers

Because they are our mentors
