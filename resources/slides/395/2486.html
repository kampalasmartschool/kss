FACTORS THAT LED TO THE INTRODUCTION OF SOCIAL SERVICES.

  • Disease treatment.
  • Promotion of trade.
  • Need to prevent environment degradation.
  • Civilization of society.
  • Interdependence.
  • Need for specialized skills.

Problems faced by government when providing services.

  • Shortage of funds.
  • Corrupt government officials.
  • Poor infrastructure e.g. roads, communication network.
  • Shortage of skilled labour.
  • Low levels of education illiteracy.
  • Political instability.
  • Rapid population  growth.

What can the government do to improve services.

  • Fighting against corruption.
  • Ensuring security in the country.
  • Constructing good roads and maintaining them regularly.
  • Getting loans.
  • Promoting education/training man power on new techniques.
  • Sensitizing people about the importance of small families

CONTROLLING POPULATION GROWTH

  • Family planning.
  • Tax benefits to small families
  • Education benefits to small families
  • Legalization of abortion.
  • Discouraging early marriages
  • Discouraging early marriages
  • Discouraging  polygamy
  • Teach people the disadvantages of a big family.
