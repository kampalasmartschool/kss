PERSONAL HYGIENE.

What is personal hygiene?

Personal hygiene is the way of keeping our body clean.

Ways of keeping clean.

  • Brushing
  • Washing regularly
  • Cutting finger nails short
  • Drinking boiled water
  • Combing hair
  • Cutting hair short.

Things we use to clean the body

  • Tooth brush
  • Sponge
  • Water
  • Comb
  • Razor blade
  • Tooth paste
  • Towel
  • Pair of scissors
  • Safety pin
  • Toilet paper
  • Handkerchief
  • Basin
  • Nail cutter
