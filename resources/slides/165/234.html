Pronouns:

Pronouns are words used instead of nouns.

Examples:

            She                                          they

            He                                            him

            I                                               you

            It                                              we

Possessive pronouns:

These are words that show ownership.

Examples:

his, hers, ours, yours, mine, its, theirs

This is his bag. It is his.

That garden belongs to us. It is ours.
