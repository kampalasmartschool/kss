People who move different means of transport

Pilots      –    aeroplane, helicopter.

Captain   –    ship.

Driver     –   car, lorries, buses.

      

Cyclist    –   bicycles, motor cycles.

     

Riders    –   camels, donkeys, horses.

Note

1. The commonest type of transport is road transport.

2. The quickest type of transport is air transport.

3. An aeroplane is the quickest mean of transport.
