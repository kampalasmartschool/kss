ENERGY CHANGES IN A CIRCUIT.

  • When the circuit is complete, chemical energy in a dry cell is changed to electricity.
  • In a bulb, electricity is changed to heat and then heat to light energy.

Types of a circuit.

  1. Parallel circuit: Is one in which all positive terminals are connected by one conductor and all negative terminals are connected by one another.
  2. Series circuits:  Is one in which the positive terminal of one cell is connected to the negative terminal of another cell to form a battery.
