AN ELECTRIC BULB.

Is an electric appliance that changes electricity to heat and light energy.

Parts of an electric bulb.

Brass cap: Enables the bulb to be fixed in the lamp holder.

Sealing tube: Enables air to be removed from the bulb and this prevents the filament from combining with oxygen.

Coiled filament:

  • The filament changes electrical energy to heat and then light energy.
  • The filament is coiled to increase resistance to electric pressure.
  • The filament is made up tungsten which has a high melting point.
  • Tungsten is got from a mineral called wolfram.

Glass bulb.

  • Holds a mixture of two gases. Argon and Nitrogen.
  • These gases prevent the evaporating of tungsten.
