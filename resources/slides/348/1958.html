CAUSES OF SHORT CIRCUITS.

  • Use of naked wires when installing a circuit.
  • Pouring water in electric appliances.
  • Poor wiring.
  • Use of faulty electrical appliances.

DANGERS OF SHORT CIRCUITS.

  • Short circuits may cause fire that may destroy property.
  • Short circuits lead to destruction of electrical appliances.

HOW TO PREVENT SHORT CIRCUITS.

  • Installation or wiring in a house should only be done by experts.
