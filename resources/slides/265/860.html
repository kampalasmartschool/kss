FOOD PATHS.

Food path are different stages in food production.

Type of food paths.

  • Village food path.
  • Town food path.
  • Earning  food path.

Village food path.

This is the food path where farmers grow crops for home consumption.

Stages of village food path.

  • Land preparation (clearing the land).
  • Planting.
  • Caring for crops.
  • Harvesting.

Town food path.

This is the food path where farmers good or produce food for sale.

Stages in town food path.

  • Clearing the land.
  • Planting.
  • Caring for crops.
  • Harvesting.
  • Drying seeds.
  • Marketing.
  • Buying and cooking food.
  • Eating.

Earning food path.

This is the food path where people who work and get salary use it to buy food in markets.

Stages of earning food path.

  • Getting salary.
  • Budgeting.
  • Buying food.

Blocks of food path.

Blocks of food path are problems faced in food production and may lead to little yield when harvested.

Examples of blocks of food path.

  • Crop pests.
  • Crops diseases.
  • Poor farming methods.
  • Poor health (elnino, drought).
  • Earth quake.
