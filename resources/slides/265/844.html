Care for garden tools.

  • Washing after use and drying them.
  • Keep the tools in dry place.
  • Painting some of them.
  • By oiling them.

Crop growing practices.

  1. Land preparation.

It is done during dry season to:-

  • Prevent the weeds from germinating again after digging and ploughing.
  • Avoid the soil from sticking on to the hoe or plough.

Ways of preparing land.

  • Digging.
  • Ploughing.
  • Slashing/clearing.
  • Cutting big trees.
  • Harrowing.
  • De– trashing. (Removing tree stumps)
