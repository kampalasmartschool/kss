Annual crops.

These crops grow, produce and die within a year.

Examples Of Annual Crops.

  • Beans
  • Maize
  • Soya beans
  • Millet
  • Sorghum
  • Rice etc

Perennial crops.

These crops grow, produce and die in more than a year.

Examples Of Perennial Crops:-

  • Tea
  • Coffee
  • Cocoa
  • Mango
  • Banana
  • Cotton etc.
