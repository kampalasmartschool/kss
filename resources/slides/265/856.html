Watering.

Watering is the supply of water to crops.

Uses of water in soil.

  • It makes the soil soft for roots to grow easily.
  • It is used for seed germination.
  • Plants use water to make food.
  • It softens the ground for easy weeding.
  • Cools the plants during transpiration.

Harvesting.

This is collecting of ready (mature) crops from the garden.

  • It is done during the dry season to dry harvests.

Some garden tools for harvesting.

Tool

Purpose

Sickle

Harvesting cereal crops

Hoe

Harvesting root crops.

Panga

Harvesting sugarcane, banana.

 

Methods of harvesting.

  1. Hand picking. (e.g. coffee, oranges etc.)
  2. Cutting stems. (e.g. sugarcane, banana)
  3. Uprooting. (e.g. groundnuts, cassava)
  4. Digging. (e.g. potatoes).
