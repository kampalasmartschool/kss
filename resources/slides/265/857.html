Storing of food.

Keeping of food safely for future use.

Reasons why farmers store food.

  1. To be eaten in dry season.
  2. For planting in next season.
  3. To be sold when market prices are better.

Places where food can be stored.

  1. In granaries.
  2. Silos.
  3. In refrigerators / freezers.

Types of stores.

  • Traditional stores e.g. granaries
  • Modern stores e.g. silos

Places where food can be stored.

  • It should be well ventilated.
  • The roof should be leak proof.
  • It should have rat guards.
  • It should be clean and dry.
