CROP ROTATION.

It is the growing of different types of crops on the same piece of land seasonally.

Advantages of crop rotation.

  • Keeps the soil fertile.
  • Controls soil erosion.
  • Controls crop pests.
  • Controls crop diseases.

NB: 

1. Legumes are alternated with non – leguminous plants.

Why: They make soil more fertile since legumes add nutrients to the soil.

2. Shallow rooters are alternated with deep rooters.

Why: This balances the use of nutrients from soil at different levels.
