FOOD PRESERVATION.

Food preservation is the preventing food from going bad.

Methods of preserving food (modern / local).

Sun drying

Cassava, sweet potatoes, maize, Irish potatoes, Onions, millet, rice, sun flower, wheat, beans, soya beans, peas.

Freezing

Oranges, mangoes, avocados, sweet banana, Irish potatoes, cucumber, cabbage, water melon.

Tinning / canning

Beans, Tomatoes 

Salting

Meat / fish

Smoking

Meat + fish

Refrigeration

All fruits / vegetation / meat / fish

Roasting

Meat / fish
