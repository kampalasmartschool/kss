Adjectives that take “more” in comparative and “most” in superlative

Examples

Positive             Comparative                  Superlative

beautiful                   more beautiful                       most beautiful

careful                          ………………….                        …………………

comfortable                  ………………….                        …………………

ignorant                        ………………….                        …………………

handsome                    ………………….                           …………………

delicate                        ………………….                        …………………

interesting                     ………………….                        …………………

important                      ………………….                        …………………

dangerous                    ………………….                        …………………

NB: These have more that two syllables.

 

Adjectives that are irregular change completely the original word.

Examples

Positive          Comparative                Superlative

bad                              worse                           worst

good                            better                            best

far                               farther                       farthest

little                             less                              least

much                            more                             most

many                            more                             most
