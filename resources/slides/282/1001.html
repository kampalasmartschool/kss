NOUNS

Definition:A noun is a name of anything.

Common nouns

A common noun is a name given to things of the same kind (type)

e.g. pens tables.

SINGULARS AND PLURALS

1. Common nouns that take “s”

E.g. Singular                         Plural

       bag                                  bags

       girl                                  girls

       lake                                 lakes

2. Common nouns that take “es”

These nouns follow a number of rules.

i) Common nouns that end with a hissing sound. ( x,s,ch,sh)

Examples

 Singular                      plural

box                              boxes

fox                              ____________

gas                              ____________

bus                             ____________

watch                         ____________

torch                         _____________

brush                        _____________

dish                          _____________

 

​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​NB: Exception of ox – oxen.

ii) Common nouns that end with y change y in to I and adds es in plural. However this rule works where the y is preceded by a consonant letter.

     Example

               Singular                                                                      Plural

               country                                                                     countries

               lady                                                                           ladies

               lorry                                                                          lorries

iii) But

Common nouns that end with y preceded by a vowel letter simply takes ‘s’ in their plural.

Example

          Singular                      Plural

key                              keys

chimney                      chimneys

ray                             rays

toy                             toys

iv) Common nouns that end with “o” take es in their plurals.

Example

          Singular                    Plural

mosquito                   mosquitoes

         echo                         echoes

         motto                       mottoes
