COLLECTIVE NOUNS

Definition: Collective nouns are names given to a group of things collected together.

Examples

  1. A group of sheep                                                                          flock
  2. A group of bees.                                                                           swarm
  3. A group of cattle                                                                           herd
  4. A group of people praying.                                                             congregation
  5. A group of thieves                                                                         gang
  6. A group of singers.                                                                        choir
  7. A group of soldiers.                                                                       troop
  8. A group of people watching a game.                                               spectators
  9. A group of people walking on foot.                                                 pedestrians
  10. A collection of sticks.                                                                    bundle
  11. A group of people listening to a speech.                                          audience
  12. A group of bishops.                                                                       bench
  13. A group of people at a funeral.                                                       mourners
  14. A collection of books, pens, pencils, files, and rulers.                       stationery
