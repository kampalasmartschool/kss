SYNONYMS:

Synonyms are words with different sound but similar meaning.

Examples:

begin -        start                    

close  -        shut

difficult -    hard                     

finish -        complete

simple -        easy                     

give   -        offer

sick    -        ill                         

nice    -        fine

quiet  -        calm

join    -        unite

afraid -        scared
