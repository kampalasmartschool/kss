Articles

Articles ‘the’ , ‘a’ and ‘an’

Article ‘a’ is used before nouns that begin with consonants and these nouns must be in singular.

Examples

a baby, a monkey, a girl, etc except for the word uniform

Article ‘an’ is used before nouns that begin with vowels e.g. an egg, an insect, an owl, an eagle, an orange etc

  2. Joel eats…………..egg everyday.
  3. The supervisors waited for less than………….hour
  4. Sam met them…………year ago.
  5. …………..old man was seated next to the door.

Article ‘The’

Article the is used when on both countable and uncountable nouns.

It points out nouns that you are particularly sure of e.g.

  • Here is the boy who stole my book.
  • Has she done all the numbers?
  • Tom took the ruler which was in the desk.
