Arranging words in alphabetical order.

(When the first letter is different)

Vocabulary:    class,tree,leaf,stem,root,eye,nose,hand,leg,foot,john,Betty,Dorothy,Annet.

                

   nose                                eyes

Example

Arrange the following words in alphabetical order.

       3           1       4        5         2

1. horse, camel, lion,   ox,  donkey

    camel, donkey, horse, lion, ox

         3         4        5       1        2 ​​​​

2. desk, stool, table, bench, chair

bench,  chair,  desk,  stool,  table
