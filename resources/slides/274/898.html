Structures

Who am I?

Example

  1. I am in-charge of a library. Who am I?  You are a librarian.
  2. I write   articles   for newspapers or magazines. Who am I?  You are an editor.
  3. I catch fish from a lake or river. Who am I? You are a fisherman.
