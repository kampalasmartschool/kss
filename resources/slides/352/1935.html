GROWING CROPS.

Importance of a School garden to the school.

  • It enables the children to learn: how to dig and how to care for crops.
  • Children get the food to eat for lunch.
  • The school gets money after selling excess food.

Factors to consider when planning a School garden.

  1. Availability of capital.
  2. Availability of enough modern tools.
  3. Enough well drained fertile piece of land.
  4. Availability of disease free seeds or seedlings.
  5. You should consider the land make up.

Ways of acquiring capital.

  1. Through acquiring loans.
  2. Through fundraising.
  3. Given in kind.
  4. Through mortgaging.

Stages of preparing of land.

Primary tillage.

This is the clearing (removal) of the natural vegetation cover.

Secondary tillage

This involves loosening of soil in preparation of planting.
