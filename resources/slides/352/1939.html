WEEDS.

Weeds are unwanted plants in the garden.

Examples of weeds.

  1. Elephant grass.
  2. Star grass.
  3. Coach Grass.
  4. Finger millet weed.
  5. Black jack.
  6. Wondering Jew.
  7. Sword grass.
  8. Spear grass.

Blackjack

Advantages of weeds.

  • Some weeds are used as vegetables to man.
  • Some weeds are eaten by wild animals.
  • Weeds help to control soil erosion since they cover the soil.
  • Weeds rot and turn into manure.

Disadvantages of weeds.

  1. Weeds compete with crops for plant nutrients.
  2. Some weeds are poisonous to man and livestock.
  3. Weeds lower the crop yields.
  4. Weeds are alternative food for some pests.

Ways of controlling weeds.

  • By mulching.
  • By crop rotation.
  • By slashing.
  • By spraying using herbicides.
  • Biological weed control method.
  • By uprooting and burning.
