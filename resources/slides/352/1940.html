PESTS.

A Pest is a bird, insect or animal which destroys plants.

Effects of pests to plants.

  1. They reduce the yield of the crop.
  2. They make plants unhealthy.
  3. They lower the quality of the crops.

Methods of controlling Crop pests.

  • Practice crop rotation - to starve life cycle of pests.
  • Spray or dust with Agro-Vet chemicals.
  • Practice early planting.
  • Planting clean varieties and disease free and resistant varieties.
