Methods of controlling diseases.

  • Practice crop rotation.
  • Dust with chemicals.
  • Plant disease free and resistant varieties.

Ways of controlling weeds in a garden.

  1. Uprooting and burning then.
  2. Regular weeding to prevent them from flowering.
  3. Cut and burry the weeds.
  4. Spray with herbicides.
  5. Mulching and shading the crops.
