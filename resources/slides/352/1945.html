Preservation of food.

Some crops can be preserved by:

i. Sundrying like root crops, cereal, legumes.

ii. Smoking

iii. Refrigeration etc

Storage of crops.

i. In granaries

ii. In silos

​​​​​​​

Conditions for proper storage.

  1. The grains should be stored when they are dry.
  2. Stores should have good ventilation.
  3. Presence of rat guards and shelter on the store.
  4. Regular dusting of crops with pesticides.

Qualities of a good food store.

  • It should be well ventilated.
  • It should be water proof.
  • It should be kept dry and clean at all times.
  • It should be raised from the group to prevent dampness.
