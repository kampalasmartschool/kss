Root crops.

These are crops that store food in their roots. They are also called root tubers.

Examples of Root crops.

  • Cassava.
  • Sweet potatoes.
  • Carrots.

Mode of propagation.

  1. Stem cutting eg in cassava.
  2. Sweet potato veins eg in sweet potatoes.
  3. Seeds eg in carrots.

Harvesting.

Harvesting is the removal of ready crops from the garden usually done in a dry season

Why?

To allow easy drying of crops since there is enough sun heat.

Methods of harvesting root crops.

  • Uprooting of the crop.
  • Doffing with hoes, sticks and any hard tool.
