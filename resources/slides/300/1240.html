PHYSICAL FEATURES IN EAST AFRICA.

Physical features are land forms of an area.

Examples of Physical Features In East Africa.

  • High lands /mountains.

  • Rift valley.

  • Plateau.

  • Coastal plains.

  • Lakes and Rivers.

  • Plains and inselbergs.
