FISH.

Fish belongs to the group of vertebrates called Pisces.

Characteristics of fish.

  • They live in water.
  • They breathe through gills.
  • They lay eggs which hatch into fish.
  • Their eggs are fertilized by the male frog outside the female’s body in water (external fertilization).
  • Most have scales on their body except cattle fish.
  • They have fins for swimming and protection.
  • They are cold blooded vertebrates.
  • They feed on plants and animals in water.
