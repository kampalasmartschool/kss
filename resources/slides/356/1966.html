MAGNETISM.

Magnetism is a force in a magnet that has the ability to push or pull magnetic substances.

Magnet.

A magnet is a piece of metal with the ability to attract magnetic substances

A magnet is made up of two poles named the North Pole and South Pole.

Magnetic substances.

Magnetic substances are materials which are attracted by a magnet.

Examples of magnetic substances.

  • Iron.
  • Steel.
  • Lead.
  • Cobalt.

Non magnetic materials are materials which are not attracted by magnets.

  • Rubber.
  • Plastic.
  • Paper.
  • Cloth.
  • Wood.
