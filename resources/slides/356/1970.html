USE OF MAGNETS (ELECTRICITY &  MAGNETISM IN MODERN WORLD).

  1. They are used in compasses to show direction.
  2. They are used in telegraph and telephone receivers to amplify sound.
  3. They are used in generators to produce electricity.
  4. They are used in industries to pick heavy metallic magnetic objects.
  5. They are used in electric bells.
  6. They are used in hospitals to remove magnetic materials from the eye.

Electrical method.

Is a method where current is used to make a magnet. The magnet made is called an electro magnet.

Ways of increasing the strength of an electro magnet.

  • Increasing the voltage.
  • Increasing the turns in the solenoid.

Coiling the wire around the iron bar makes a solenoid.

Advantage of using an electro magnet.

  • Its strength can be increased.

NB: An electro magnet can be demagnetized by passing it through alternating current.

Examples of equipment that use electro magnets.

  • Electric bell.
  • Sound amplifiers.
  • Generators.
