BALANCED DIET.

Balanced diet is a meal containing all food values in their right amounts.

What makes up a balanced diet?

A balanced diet is made up of (3) three main classes of food.

Food values.

  • Proteins.
  • Carbohydrates.
  • Fats and oils.
  • Water and mineral salts.
  • Vitamins.
  • Roughages.
