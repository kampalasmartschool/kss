MINERAL SALTS.

They are health giving foods so keep us healthy.

Types of Mineral salts

  • Iron.
  • Calcium.
  • Phosphorus.
  • Iodine.
  • Sodium.
  • Potassium.
  • Magnesium.
  • Fluoride.

IRON.

  1. For making red blood cells.
  2. Formation of haemoglobin

Sources of iron.

  • Meat.
  • Liver.
  • Calcium.
  • Milk.
  • millet.
  • Green vegetables  e.g. spinach
  • Beans.

NB:  Phosphorus, magnesium also strengthen bones and teeth.
