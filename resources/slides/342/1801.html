Vulnerable groups of people.

These are groups of people that are easily affected by lack of proper feeding.

These are groups of people that need special care in terms of feeding.

Examples of vulnerable groups of people.

  • Pregnant mothers.
  • Sick people.
  • Weaning babies.
  • Elderly people.
  • Breastfeeding mothers.

Breast feeding

It is the act of feeding  baby on breast milk.

Importance of breast milk to a baby

  • It is easy to digest.
  • It contains all food values needed by the baby.
  • Breast milk contains antibodies which protects the baby against diseases.
  • It is at the right body temperature.
  • It is clean.

Importance of breast feeding to a mother.

  • It saves her time.
  • It is cheap.
  • It create love bond between the mother and the baby.
  • It delays the next pregnancy.

Malnutrition

  • It is a condition when the body does not receive enough food nutrients.
  • Signs / symptoms of malnutrition   -   tireless , loss of body weight , dullness.
