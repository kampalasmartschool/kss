FATS AND OILS.

They are energy giving foods so provide energy and heat.

Sources of fats and oils

  • Milk.
  • Butter.
  • Cheese.
  • Egg yolk.
  • Ground nuts.
  • Margarine.
  • Meat.

Dangers of having little fats in the body.

  • Lack of energy.
  • Thinners.
  • Rough and dry skin.
  • Feeling cold all the time.

Note: Fats are solids while oils are liquids at room temperature.
