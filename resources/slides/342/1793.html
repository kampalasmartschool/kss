PROTEINS.

These are body building foods.

Uses Of Proteins.

  • Proteins helps in making new body cells.
  • Proteins help in replacement of worn out tissues / body cells.

Sources of proteins.

(a)  Animal proteins

  • Beef.
  • Chicken.
  • Fish.
  • Eggs.
  • Grasshoppers.
  • White ants.
  • Pork.

(b)  Plant proteins

  • Beans.
  • Soya beans.
  • Ground nuts.
  • Peas.
