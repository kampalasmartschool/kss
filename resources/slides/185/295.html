Conjuctions

Use of ……………and………………

Example

1. Zuma has a ball. Zuma has a rope.

Zuma has a ball and a rope.

2. Sarah is smart. Josephine is smart.

Sarah and Josephine are smart.

Adjectives

Examples

  • busy                 -  honest
  • thin                  -  active
  • ugly                  -  kind
  • weak                 -  early

Opposites of adjectives

Fat – thin

Ugly – beautiful / handsome

Weak – strong

Poor – rich

Heavy          light

Tall              short

Wet            dry

Old              new

Sharp          blunt
