THE PAST PERFECT

The passive form of the past perfect tense is used in the order below; had been + a past participle verb

Examples

1. I had eaten food. (active)

Food had been eaten by me. (passive)

2. Cars had carried people (active)

People had been carried by cars. (passive)

3. The doctor had warned me against smoking cigarettes. (active)

I had been warned against smoking cigarettes by the doctor (passive)
