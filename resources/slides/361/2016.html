THE FUTURE TENSES

THE FUTURE SIMPLE TENSE

The passive form of the future simple tense is got from the order below ; will be + a past participle verb

Examples

1. I shall eat food. (active)

Food will be eaten by me. (passive)

2. Cars will carry people (active)

People will be carried by cars. (passive)

3. The doctor will warn me against smoking cigarettes. (active)

I shall be warned against smoking cigarettes by the doctor. (passive
