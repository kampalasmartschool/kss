COMMUNICATION

The post office

Vocabulary

Aerogramme, box rental, directory, letter box, letter slot, mail, money order, package, parcel, private box, post, post office, registered letter, stamp, telegram

  • Spell each word correctly
  • Use each of the words above in a sentence.

ACTIVITY

Uses the correct form of the words in brackets to complete the sentences.

  1. The postmaster was ___________________ by a bee yesterday. (sting)
  2. Having ____________________ a nice letter, I went to the post office. (write)
  3. The secretary ____________________ two letters last week. (post)
  4. Opio wrote his name ___________________ on the envelope. (clear)
  5. It is not good to open someone’s package ____________________. (illegal)
  6. His ______________________ was misplaced in the post office. (apply)
  7. I _______________________ an aerogram to my father last December. (send)
