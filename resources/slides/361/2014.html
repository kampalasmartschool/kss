THE PAST CONTINUOUS

The passive form of the past continuous tense is used in the given order; was being, were being + a past perfect verb

Examples

1. I was eating food. (active)

Food was being eaten by me. (passive)

2. Cars were carrying people (active)

People were being carried by cars. (passive)

3. The doctor was warning me against smoking cigarettes (active)

I was being warned against smoking cigarettes by the doctor. (passive)
