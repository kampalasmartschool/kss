Problems experienced by explorers.

  • Poor transport facilities.
  • Attacks from dangerous animals.
  • Tropical diseases – e.g. malaria
  • Language barrier.
  • Hostile tribes.
  • Tribal wars.
  • Shortage of supplies (medical/ food).
