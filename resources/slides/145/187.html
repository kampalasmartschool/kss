Sub themes: Roles of people in the community.

Vocabulary:

doctor, teacher, nurse, carpenter, policeman, policewoman, shopkeeper, cobbler, farmer, barber, butcher, fisherman, fishmonger (include people, places of work, tools)

 

Structure:

  • Show me a / the ……………………..

This / that is a (nurse, teacher)

  • Where is the ………………….?

The (doctor /driver) is ( in / under) the (hospital / house / car)

  • What does a …………………. Do?

A …………………….. treats / builds.

Activities:
