PAST PERFECT TENSE

When used;

  • It’s used to show that two actions took place in the past, one took place before the other one began.

Example

  • When Robert came, the teacher had started the lesson.
  • By the time dad reached home, we had (already) had our supper. We had already had our supper by the time dad reached home.

Characteristics

Use ………………..had it past participle of main verb
