NEGATIVE AND INTERROGATIVE SENTENCES IN PAST CONTINUOUS TENSE

Examples

1. They were splitting firewood (affirm)

They were not splitting firewood (neg)

Were they splitting firewood? (inter)

2. He was tending the sheep when the lion attacked the flock.

He wasn’t attending the sheep when the lion attacked.

Was he attending the sheep when the lion attacked?
