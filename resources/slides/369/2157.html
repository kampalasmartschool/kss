ACTIVE AND PASSIVE OF THE PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS TENSE

Structural pattern to use;

Example

1. Active:   Namukose has been singing the National Anthem

Passive: The National Anthem has been being sung by Namukose

2. Active:  The president has been inspecting the guards.

    Passive: The guards have been being inspected by the president.
