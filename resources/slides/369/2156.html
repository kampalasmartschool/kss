QUESTION TAGS IN PRESENT PERFECT TENSE

Examples

  1. He has come very early today, hasn’t he?
  2. She has treated her brother badly, hasn’t she?
  3. They have not eaten anything, have they?
  4. We have finished the work, haven’t we?
