Language structures

Using: Whereas……

Whereas is used to compare and contrast two facts

When ‘whereas’ begins a sentence, a comma is used.

Examples

1. Many candidates passed the examinations. Okello failed the examinations.

Whereas many candidates passed the examinations, Okello failed.

2. Babirye came to school early. Nakato came to school late.

Whereas Babirye came to school early, Nakato came to school late.

3. The English examination paper was very difficult. Mathematics examination paper was very easy.

Whereas the English examination paper was easy, Mathematics examination paper was difficult.
