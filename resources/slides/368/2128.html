Use of……..so…….that………..

So…that…to combine clauses that show a cause and effect relationship between sentences

Examples

1. The box is very heavy. I cannot lift it.

The box is so heavy that I cannot lift it.

2. William played cleverly. John could not beat him.

William played so cleverly that John could not beat him.

3. The milk was very good. We could not help drinking it.

The milk was so good that we could not help drinking it.
