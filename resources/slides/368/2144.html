Formation of verbs

There are different ways in which verbs are formed and so they have different names;

Infinite verbs

These are the kind of verbs that are formed by adding “to” before them.

Sleep                to sleep

Buy                   to buy

Write                 to write

Sit                    to sit

Eat                   to eat

Regular verbs.

These verbs are formed by adding “ed” or “d”

Move                     moved

Cook                     cooked

Work                     worked

Dance                   danced

Allow                     allowed

Pack                     packed

Use                       used

Live                       lived

Talk                      talked

Walk                     walked

Verbs can be formed from nouns and adjectives

From nouns

Noun                                  Verb formed

Cloth                                  clothe

Solid                                   solidify

Brass                                  braze

Friend                                 befriend

Food                                   feed

Knee                                   kneel

Glass                                  glaze

Liquid                                  liquidity

Circle                                  encircle

Tale                                    tell

From adjectives

Adjective                     Verb formed

Glory                                 glorify

Angry                                anger

Beauty                              beautify

Company                           accompany

Deep                                 deepen

Safe                                  save

Clear                                 clarify

Strong                               strengthen

Low                                   lower

Pure                                  purify

New                                  renew

ACTIVITY

Fill in the blanks with a correct form of the verb formed from the words in the brackets.

  1. They were _________________ in Mombasa by the Arabs. (slave)
  2. A river starts narrowly from the source but _________________ in the middle. (broad)
  3. He _________________ before coming to the chapel to pray. (bathe)
  4. The work was ____________________ when they brought two more teachers. (less)
  5. He ___________________ many crates of soda to his friends. (sale)
  6. The story was ____________________ to us by the teacher. (dictation)
  7. This place has just been ______________________, it was empty. (occupy)
  8. The road was _______________________ by the road constructors. (wide)
  9. Many Christians were _______________________ during the Roman Empire. (head)
  10. He ___________________ his uncle in power. (success)
