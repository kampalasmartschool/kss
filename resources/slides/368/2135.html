Using: Despite …….

Despite is a preposition of contrast.

Despite can be used with abstract nouns such as sickness, wisdom, intelligence, smartness, laziness, cleverness, etc e.g. followed by …..being: Despite being beautiful, she never married a serous man

a possessive determiner e.g. : Despite her beauty, she never married a serous man

Examples

1. Jacky was intelligent. She did not score 100%.

Despite her intelligence, Ruth did not score 100%.

Despite the fact that Ruth was intelligent, she did not score 100%.

2. Wilson was late. He finished his examination in time.

Despite his being late, Wilson finished his examination on time.

Despite the fact that Wilson was late, he finished his examination on time.
