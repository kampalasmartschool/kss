HOMOPHONES

Homophones are words that are pronounced alike but having different spelling and meaning.

Examples 

knew                            new

all                                 whole

no                                 know

meat                             meet

aloud                            allowed

check                           cheque

council                          counsel

flew                              flue

lesson                           lessen

lose                              loose

week                            weak

ring                               wring
