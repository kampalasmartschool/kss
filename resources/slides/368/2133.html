Using………could…........

Past form of ‘can’

Used to express the general possibility in the past

Used to ask for permission in a very polite way

Used to suggest something

Examples

  1. Cheating could lead to closure of the examination centre.
  2. Malpractice could lead to disqualification from the examinations.
  3. Examination leakage could lead to cancellation of the results.
