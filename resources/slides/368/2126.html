Use of ……..look forward………

…….look forward to…….. means being optimistic or thinking with pleasure about something that is going to happen. It is followed by a verb ending with “ing” e.g. sitting, meeting, writing, doing, etc.

Examples

1. We expect to sit our final examinations.

We look forward to sitting our final examinations.

2. I am eager to score aggregate four in PLE.

I look forward to scoring aggregate four in PLE.

3. He is optimistic to join Dr. Obote College in senior one.

He is looking forward to joining Dr. Obote College in senior one.
