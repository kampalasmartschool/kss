ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE

In active voice, the sentence starts with a subject (doer) and ends with an object (receiver)

In passive voice, the sentence starts with an object.

NB: Vague subjects should not be repeated in passive voice e.g someone, somebody, people, some girls, no one, we, they

Someone ate my food.

My food was eaten.

People speak English all over the world.

English is spoken all over the world.
