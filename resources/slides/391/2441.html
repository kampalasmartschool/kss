THE PEOPLE OF AFRICA.

The people Africa are grouped under ethnic groups.

These include the following:

  • Bantu.
  • Semites (Arabs)
  • Berbers.
  • Nilotes e.g River –Lake Nilotes, Highland Nilotes and Plain Nilotes
  • Hamites / Cushites.

Original inhabitants of:-

  • North Africa - Berbers.
  • Central Africa - Pygmies.
  • East Africa - Bushmen.
  • South Africa – Khoi-Sans.
