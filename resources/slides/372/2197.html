ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE (PAST SIMPLE TENSE)

Examples

1. Mary cooked a lot of food.

     A lot of food was cooked by Mary.

2. The teacher taught him English.

     He was English by the teacher.

3. He killed the rat.

     The rat was killed by him.
