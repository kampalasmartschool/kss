AFFIRMATIVE, NEGATIVE AND INTERROGATIVE (PAST SIMPLE TENSE)

Examples

1. A – He came to school late yesterday.

N - He did not come to school yesterday.

I – Did he come to school late yesterday?

2. Sarah took her friend to school.

N - Sarah did not take her friend to school.

I -Did Sarah take her friend to school?
