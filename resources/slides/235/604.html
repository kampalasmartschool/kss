Finding place values

e.g.    TH     H       T        O

          1        2       3         4

4 is ones,   3 is tens,   2 is hundreds,   1 is thousands

 

Find the place value of 8 in the number: 4789

Solution: 4789

                     tens

The place value of 8 is tens.
