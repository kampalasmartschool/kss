Using “was” and “were”

We use “was” for one item or person.

We use “were” for more than one item or person.

Examples (was)

  1. One egg was cracked.
  2. The apple was in the basket.
  3. Was it a good pen.

Examples  “were”

  1. The eggs     “were” cracked.
  2. The apples were on the table.
  3. The oranges were sweet.
