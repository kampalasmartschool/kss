ACTIVITY

Complete the sentences with a correct form of the word in bracket.

  1. The lions were heard ____________ in the forest. (roar)
  2. Grunting is to pig as ____________ is to donkey. (neigh)
  3. The pigeons ____________ all through the night. (coo)
  4. ____________ dogs seldom bite. (bark)
  5. Cats ________ whenever they are hungry. (purr)
