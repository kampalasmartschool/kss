AFFIRMATIVE NEGATIVE AND INTERROGATIVE (present continuous tense)

Examples

Negative

1. I am going home now.

You are not going home now.

2. The president is flying to U.S.A.

The president is not flying to U.S.A.

Interrogative

1. We are going to buy a car.

Are we going to buy a car?

2. She is bringing it now.

Is she bringing it now?
