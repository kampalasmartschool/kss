QUESTION TAGS (PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE)

Examples

  1. Mukasa is sleeping on his bed, isn’t he?
  2. I am feeding the puppies, aren’t I?
  3. I am not feeding the puppies, am I?
  4. Children aren’t eating supper, are they?
