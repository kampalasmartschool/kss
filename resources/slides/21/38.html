Uses of school needs

a) Classrooms        – to protect us against bad weather and to study from them.

b) Pencils              – we need pencils for writing with

c) Teachers          – use chalkboard to give instructions

d) Waste bins      - water papers , basket are used for putting in rubbish

e) We need chairs for sitting on

f) We need food for growth
