Uses of people in our school

Headmaster                -           he heads the school.

Deputy headteacher   -           he assists the headteacher.

Teachers                     -           they teach the children.

Secretary                    -           types our home work and exams.

Bursar                         -           keeps the school money.

Cooks                          -           cook the food.

Cleaners                      -           clean the school.

Nurse                          -           helps the sick children at  school.

Security                       -           guard   keeps the school safe.

Matrons                       -           look after children in the boarding                                                 section.
