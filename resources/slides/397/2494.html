TELEPHONES

    

It is the fastest means of communication used in East Africa.

Telephone companies in Uganda are;

MTN- Mobile Telephone Network.

AirTel- Uganda Telecom Limited (UTL),Vodafone , Africel, Smile etc.

 

Advantages of Telephone;

  • It is very fast.
  • Gives immediate feedback.
  • It saves time.

 

TELEVISION

       

Examples of Television Stations in Uganda are;

UBC, TV, WBS, LTV, Record TV, NTV,TV West, Top TV, NBS, Bukedde, E.A TV, DSTV.

Advantages

  • It provides both sound and pictures.
  • Provides current news.
  • Gives entertainment.
  • Announcements.

Disadvantages;

  • Showing pornography.
  • They are expensive.
