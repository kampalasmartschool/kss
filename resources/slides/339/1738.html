FISH  PRESERVATION.

Preservation   is  the  way  of  keeping  food  for a long   time  without   going  bad.

Modern methods of preserving fish.

  • Canning  / tinning

  • Refrigeration

Local methods.

  • By smoking.

  • By salting.

  • By sun drying.

Qn: Why do we preserve fish?

  • For future use.

  • To prevent wastage.
