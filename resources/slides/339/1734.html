AN AQUARIUM.

An aquarium is a  glass  tank  where   fish and  other  water animals  are  kept  in  our  homes.

Places  where  an  aquarium can  be  found.

  • Hotels.

  • School  compounds.

  • Homes.  etc.

CLASSIFYING ANIMALS.

Animals without legs.

  • Fish.

  • Snakes.

  • Slugs.

  • Earth worm.

Animals which lay eggs.

  • Lizards.

  • Tortoise.

  • Chameleon.
