ENVIRONMENT.

Environment are things / around us. Surroundings is another word to mean environment.

Components of the environment.

Environment is made up of two components namely:

  • Living components.
  • Non – living components.

Living components

Non-living components
  • Plants
  • Animals
  • Air
  • Water
  • Soil
