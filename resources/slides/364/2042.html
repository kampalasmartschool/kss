NATIONALITIES

I wish I were a Nigerian,

I would act Nigerian movies,

For my audience to watch and smile

Like Popo and Chinedu

 

I wish I were a Congolese,

I would live in Ituri forest,

And sing Lingala songs

Like Pepe Kale and Kanda Bongoman

 

I wish I were a Japanese,

I would make Toyota cars

And sell to African countries

And become a billionaire

Like Bill Gates

 

I wish I were a Chinese,

I would import long lasting

Pairs of shoes for Ugandans

To purchase at reduced prices.

And realize value for money

 

Oh yes! I should thank,

The Lord my God

For I am a Ugandan by nationality!
