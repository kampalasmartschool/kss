Words that end with ‘y’ we remove ‘y’ and add ‘ier’ or ‘iest’

lazy                  lazier                laziest

ugly                  uglier                ugliest

crazy                crazier              craziest

greedy              greedier             greediest

Exercise

Fill in the gaps with the correct form of adjective in bracket.

  1. Kauma is the ________________ girl in our class. (lazy)
  2. Who is ___________________ than you? (happy)
  3. John is ____________________ than Peter. (hungry)
  4. I am the ____________________ today. (early)
  5. English is _____________________ Music. (easy)
