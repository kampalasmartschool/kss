QUESTION TAGS

There are two types of question tags i.e positive and negative question tags.  Negative question tags are used in affirmative sentences while positive question tags are used in negative sentences and commands.

Examples

1.  Taj collects books every day.

     Taj does collect books every day, doesn’t he?

     Taj doesn’t collect books every day, does he?

2. Opio and Okia like playing in class, don’t they?

3. It doesn’t rain everyday, does it?
