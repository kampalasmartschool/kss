ABSTRACT NOUNS

These are the nouns which cannot be seen or counted.

They are nouns of qualities of things, creatures or people.  These qualities only exist in something.  They don’t exist on their own.  Abstract nouns can be formed from adjectives or verbs.

Examples

width                            height

length                          anger

breadth                         behaviour

truth                             love

sadness                       heat

happiness                     presence

bitterness                     choice
