Use of capital letters

  • At the beginning of the sentence; e.g.  My father is a good man in our village.
  • At the beginning of proper nouns; e.g.  names of people, cities, lakes, mountains, countries, rivers, days, months , streets  etc. e.g.   Musoke, Kampala, Victoria,  Elgon, Uganda,  Nile, Sunday, February,  William street  respectively.
  • Writing abbreviations    e.g.   P.L.E.  ,  U.P.E  ,  D. E. O   etc  
  • Pronouns I  e.g   You  and  I are friends.
  • To begin in direct speech.  E.g.  The teacher said,  “ Go  out!”
  • To begin word  He, Him, His  -  If they are relating to God.
