QUESTION TAGS

Examples

Complete the sentences using suitable question tags.

  1. That man has taken my handkerchief, hasn’t he?
  2. Mr. Baba hasn’t come today, has he?
  3. You have not carried your set with you, have you?
  4. He has not lost his way, has he?
