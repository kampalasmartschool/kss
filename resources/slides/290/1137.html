COLLECTIVE NOUNS

These nouns stand for a lot of things /individuals.  We call them collective nouns because they refer to collections of things, people, items  etc.

Examples

A gang of thieves                                              

A packet of cigarettes

A flock of sheep                                    

A fleet of cars

A team of oxen                                     

A heap of soil

A troop of lions                                     

A heap of soil

A swarm of bees                                               

A bouquet of flowers

A set of furniture                                               

A brood of chicken

A forest of trees

A leap of leopards

A troop of monkeys

A crowd of people

A troupe of dancers

A company of actors

A choir of singers

A gang of prisoners

A mob of disorderly people

A congregation of worshipers

A bundle / bunch of keys

A cluster of bananas

A mouthful of food

A pack of cards

A herd of cattle

A pocketful of money

A library of books

A queue of people

A flight of birds
