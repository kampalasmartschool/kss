Exclamation mark ( !)

Used to show surprise, admiration or fear e.g.  My God!   What a dirty plate that is!

It’s used on exclamatory words e.g. Hurrah!, Oh!, Alas!, Ah!, Ooops!, Hullo!

Examples

  • Oh Tim, why did you kill that dog!
  • “Shut up!” He ordered.
  • Hurrah, the holiday is at hand!
