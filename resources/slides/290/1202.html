QUESTION TAGS (PAST SIMPLE TENSE)

Examples

Supply suitable questions tags to these sentences.

  1. The dog did eat your hen, didn’t it?
  2. The bursar didn’t pay me, did she?
  3. They contributed greatly to our party, didn’t they?
  4. You saw them in the church, didn’t you?
