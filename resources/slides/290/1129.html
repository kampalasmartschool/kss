COMMA ( ,  )

Used when listing things e.g. he bought a ball, pencil, pen and hut.

Used in address or date e.g.  Kinderkare Elementary School,

                                                P.O. Box 4096,

                                                Kampala.

Salutation e.g.

Dear Dad,

  • Used after “yes” or “No” at the beginning of the sentence.  E.g. No, that you.
  • Yes, I shall go
  • Used in expressions like   On the other hand, however, additionally, on top of that, for instance, by the way       further more etc.
  • Used to set off the name of the person addressed e.g. Why do you come with us, Peter?
  • Used in speeches e.g., Mary said, “I will help you”.
  • Used in question tags e.g. She is beautiful, isn’t she?
