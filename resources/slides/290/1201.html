Interrogative sentences

Turn the given affirmative sentences to interrogative.

Examples

1. She took the food to the hospital yesterday.

Did she take food to the hospital yesterday?

2. The stranger spoke French.

Did the stranger speak French?

3. Those boys quarreled over food.

Did those boys quarrel for food?

4. The customer cheated the seller of meat.

Did the customer cheat the seller of meat?
