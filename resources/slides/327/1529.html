Lesson Focus :  ‘cl’ words

class      clean        close    click     clay

clan    clever       clock          climb         clap

Sentences

Mummy bought a nice cloth.

I belong to the kob clan.

They always clean the compound.

He is in class one.

There is a clock on the table.

Activity

Read the sentences many times

Rearrange the sentences to make a good story

Write the story.

Jumbled story

When it is ready. I pour it in my cup.

I put the kettle on a charcoal stove.

Then I take my tea.

I light the charcoal stove.

I put the water in the kettle.

Good story
