Personal hygiene

Personal hygiene is the cleanliness of the body. We clean the body by bathing, brushing the teeth, cutting the nails and hair short. Combing the hair and cleaning the nose. We use things like soap, sponge, water, comb, towel, toothbrush, and toothpaste to clean the body. We should clean our bodies to avoid smelling, to look smart and to avoid diseases.
