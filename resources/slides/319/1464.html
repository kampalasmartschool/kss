Our community

Comprehension about people in our community.

A community is a group of people living and working together. Different people do different activities.

Mr. Kasule is a farmer. He grows crops and keeps animals. Mr. Okello is a doctor. He treats sick people. Mr. Obara cuts people’s hair and Mr. Mugarura mends shoes. They will enjoy their work.
