PREPOSITION

in,      on,          under,   over,     near,     next to.

Example

a)                    The pencils are in the tin.

 

 

b)                                 The bird is flying over the church.

 

 

c)                                 The ball is near the box.

 

 

e)                                 The fish is in the basket.

c)                                 The cat is under the table.

Activity

Fill in the correct preposition.

  1. Mary is sitting _____________ the mat. (on, with)
  2. The boy is pointing _____________the sun. ( at, near)
  3. The bird is flying  ______________the tree. (over, on)
  4. The fish is _________________water. (above, in)
