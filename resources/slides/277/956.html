VACCINES.

Vaccines are special drugs introduced to the body to make it resistant against specific diseases.

Storage of vaccines.

They are stored in cool places like refrigerators , ice cubes , in vacuum flasks (villages).

Types of Vaccines.

There are three types of vaccines namely:- 

  1. Toxoids: (Poisonous substances)
  2. Attenuated: (Weak disease causing germs),
  3. Dead organism vaccines: (Dead disease causing germs)
