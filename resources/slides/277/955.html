Artificial immunity / acquired immunity.

A person gets artificial immunity when the vaccines are introduced into one’s body.

This is mainly done by injection or by oral method. Artificial immunity is got through immunization.

Importance of immunity.

  • It keeps the body healthy and productive.
  • It reduces death rates.
  • It strengthens the immune system of the body.
