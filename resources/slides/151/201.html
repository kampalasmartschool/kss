Conjuctions ‘and’

Changes when using ‘and’

  • Is becomes are
  • Was becomes were
  • Has becomes have

Examples

1.Mark is a good boy. Richard is a good boy.

Mark and Richard are good boys.

2. Joan is smart. Joan is beautiful.

Joan is smart and beautiful.
