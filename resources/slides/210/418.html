Word problems involving division of numbers by 3.


a)      Mummy had   6 bananas. She shared them equally among 3   children. How many bananas did               each get?

6     ÷     3      =    2

       

       Each child got 2 bananas.

b) Nine divide by three equals  3

 

c)    Share12 pencils equally among 3boys.

        12    ÷     3      =   4 pencils 

             Each child gets 4 pencils.

d)    What do we get when we share 3 apples equally among 3 girls?

             3    ÷     3      = 1 apple
