Roman numerals got by repeating 1 or X.

2 = I + I = II  _______________20 = 10 + 10 = XX

3 = I + I + I = III ____________30 = 10 + 10 + 10 = XXX

Roman numerals got by adding to 5 (V).

6 = 5 + I                        7   =  5 + 2                                8 = 5 + 3

   = VI                                = VII                                      = VIII

Roman numerals got by subtracting from 5 or from 50.

a) 4 = 1 subtracted from 5

    4 = IV

b) 40 = 10 subtracted from 50

    40 = XL

The roman numerals got by subtracting from 10 and 100. 

a) 9 = 1 subtracted from 10.

    9 = IX

b) 90 = 10 subtracted from 100   

    90= XC
