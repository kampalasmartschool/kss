Word statements about multiplication

Example 1.

a) One hen has two legs. How many legs have four hens?

 4 x2 =    8   legs

Four hens have  8 legs

b) If a stool has three stands. How many stands do three stools have?

  3 x 3 = 9 stands

Three stools have  9 stands.
