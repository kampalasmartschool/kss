Measures

Word problems involving finding perimeter of a shape

Example

A  rectangle measures 4cm length  and 2cm width. Find its perimeter.

         

P= s+s+s+s

 = 4m+2m+4m+2m

 =  6m +6m

 =  12m

P= 12m

Activity

1. Find the perimeter of a square garden whose length is 4m.

2. Our school hall is 20 metre long and 10 meter wide. Find its perimeter.

3. Calculate the perimeter of a plot of land whose length is 50m and width 25 m.
