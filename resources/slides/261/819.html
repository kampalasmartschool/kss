Measures

Word problem involving subtraction of metres and centimeters

Example

Musa had a string of 8m 47cm. He cut off 2m 16cm. What length of the string was left?

  m     cm

  8      47

- 2     16

  6      31

Activity

1) Taj had a cloth measuring 3m 12 cm. He sold the part of the cloth measuring 2m  6cm .

What was he left with?

2) Lucy had a sugar cane measuring 2m 49 cm. She ate the length of 1m  23cm.

What was the length of the part left?

3) Sheila's garden is 20m  24 cm long. She cultivated only 12m  22cm of it. How much was left?
