Finding perimeter

Perimeter

Definition: Perimeter is the total distance around any given figure.

  • It is measured as a length and can be in a range of units such as cm, inches, feet, miles, etc.

Example

Find the perimeter of the figure below.

                

      P  = s+s+s+s

          = 4cm +3cm+4cm+3cm

          = 7cm +7cm

        P = 14cm
