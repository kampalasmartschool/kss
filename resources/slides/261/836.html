How To Calculate Area

What is area?

Area tells us the size of a shape or figure.

It tells us the size of squares, rectangles, circles, triangles, other polygons, or any enclosed figure.

Area is measured in squares (or square units).

How many squares are in this rectangle?

We can count the squares or we can take the length and width and use multiplication.
