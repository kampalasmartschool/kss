Present continuous tense.

Adding – ing to the verbs.

Example:

throw – throwing

brush – brushing

touch – touching

burn – burning

cover – covering

collect – collecting

sweep – sweeping

Activities

a. Changing verbs to present continuous tense.           

b. constructing sentences using verbs in present continuous tense.

Change these verbs to present continuous tense.

slash    slashing            throw    throwing            look      looking

cook     cooking             point     pointing             read      reading

boil       boiling               walk      walking             pray      praying

Verbs that end with ‘e’

we remove ‘e’ and add – ing

Example

bathe – bathing

drive – driving

live – living

move – moving

Activity

 Use the words in the brackets correctly.

  1. The children are _________ their books. (read)
  2. She is __________ to town. (drive)
  3. The boys are _____________ the compound now (slash)
  4. The cat is ______________ milk now. (drink)
  5. We will ________________ tomorrow. (leave)
