Verbs that end with ‘y’

Remove ‘y’ and add ‘ies’

Examples

Marry               marries

Carry                carries

Dry                   dries

Try                   tries

Fly                    flies

Practice

  • Identify more verbs which change to present simple tense by assign – es
  • Spell the verbs changed to present simple tense.

Activity

Fill in the gaps with a correct form of the word in bracket.

  1. Our baby _____________________ every morning. (cry)
  2. She ___________________ to Dubai every month. (fly)
  3. Our teacher ___________________ to every question asked. (reply)
  4. Johan _______________________ a lot when she is late. (hurry)
  5. Too much sunshine __________________ farmers’ crops. (dry)
