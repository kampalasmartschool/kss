Use of apostrophe in short forms:

We use the apostrophe to write short forms of words.

Examples:        

  • Is not          -           isn’t
  • Was not      -           wasn’t
  • they are      -           they’re
  • it is             -           it’s
  • he is           -           he’s
  • has not       -           hasn’t
  • we have      -           we’ve
  • they have    -           they’ve
  • do not         -           don’t
  • cannot        -           can’t
  • does not      -           doesn’t
  • could not     -           couldn’t
  • would not    -           wouldn’t
  • let us          -           let’s

Activity

1. Join these words using an apostrophe.

  1. has not ________         
  2. we are ____________   
  3. did not ____________               

2. Fill in the gaps with the short forms of the words in the brackets.

  1. Roge rs ____________ coming to school today. (is not)
  2. The dog ____________ inside the kennel. (was not)
  3. We ___________ work when there is noise in the classroom. (cannot)
