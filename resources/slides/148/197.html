Past simple tense.

Verbs which change to past simple tense by adding  “ed”

Examples:

brush                brushed                         need                 needed

clean                cleaned                         box                   boxed

cook                 cooked                          help                  helped

cover                covered                         wash                washed

touch                touched                         kick                  kicked

Activity

Complete the sentences below with a correct form of the word in bracket.

  1. I ________________ my parents to cook last weekend. (help)
  2. We _______________________ the classroom by ourselves. (clean)
  3. My father ____________________ his teeth thrice yesterday. (brush)
  4. Have you _____________________ your uniform yet? (wash)
  5. The boxer _____________________ Johnny into a box full of matchboxes. (box)
