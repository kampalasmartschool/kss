Verbs that add letter ‘s’ to form past simple tense

Example

Play                  plays                            help                  helps

add                   adds                             push                 pushes

plant                 plants                           clean                cleans

collect               collects                         cook                 cooks

touch                touches                         work                 works

cover                covers                          wait                  waits

Activity

Complete the sentences using the correct form of the word in the brackets.

  1. The cat ____________ the tree yesterday. (climb)
  2. We ___________ for the bus as the park last week. (wait)
  3. Rhoda ___________ her uniform yesterday. (wash)
  4. It __________ heavily last Monday. (rain)
  5. Deborah _________ the window last evening. (open)
