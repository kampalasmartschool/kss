Present continuous tense of verbs which double their last letter.

Examples:

stop      -           stopping

clap      -           clapping

slap      -           slapping

skip      -           skipping

tap        -           tapping

swim    -           swimming

cut        -           cutting

get        -           getting

knit       -           knitting

rot        -           rotting

shut      -           shutting

put        -           putting

Activity

  1. Fill in the gaps using the present continuous tense of the verb in the brackets.
    1. Mummy is ________________ meat now. (cut)
    2. I am going  for ______________ now. (swim)
    3. The horse is ________________ now. (trot)
    4. We are __________ to go to Kabale. Now. (plan)
    5. The boys are __________ in Nambole now. (run)
