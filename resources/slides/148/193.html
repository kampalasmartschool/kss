Present simple tense:

Changing verbs to present simple tense by adding ’s’

Examples:

walk           –         walks                            read      –          reads   

pray            –         prays                            sing      –         sings

play            _          plays                            run        _          runs

learn           _          learns                           put        _          puts

sweep         _          sweeps                         take      _          takes

dance         _          sing                              write     _          writes

Practice

  1. Give examples of verbs which are in the category learnt.
  2. Change these verbs to present simple.

Activity

Use the correct form of the words in the brackets.

  1. The doctor _______________ sick people everyday. (treat)
  2. Sarah _______________ well in her book every week. (write)
  3. Mary _______________ her prayers every morning. (say)
  4. A policeman ___________________ law and order every night. (keep)
  5. The cobbler _________________ shoes everyday. (mend)
