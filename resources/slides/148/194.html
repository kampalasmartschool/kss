Changing verbs to present simple tense by adding – ‘es’.

Examples:

brush    -           brushes            

wash    -           washes

touch    -           touches

go         -           goes

do         -           does

watch   -           watches           

match   -           matches

teach    -           teaches

Practice

  • Identify more verbs which change to present simple tense by assign – es
  • Spell the verbs changed to present simple tense.
