Enough….to

Examples

1. He ran fast. He won the race

He ran fast enough to win the race

2. The wind was very strong. It blew off the roof of his house

The wide was strong enough to blow off the roof of his house

3. The shopkeeper was very foolish. He did not save any money.

The shopkeeper was not wise enough to save any money

4. Tom is very young. He can’t go to school.

Tom is not old enough to go to school.
