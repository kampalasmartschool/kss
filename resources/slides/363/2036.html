Using relative pronouns as conjunctions.

Examples

1. The man was taken to the hospital. The man was seriously injured.

The man who was seriously injured was taken to the hospital.

2. The rabbit was later trapped. The rabbit had run very fast.

The rabbit which had run very fast was later trapped.

3. The soldier was badly hurt. The children saw him.

The soldier whom the children saw was badly hurt.

4. The cow died. Its tail was cut off.

The cow which tail was cut off died
