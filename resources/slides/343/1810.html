The Brain.

This is the most important organ of the body.

-     The brain is found in the head.

-     It is protected by the skull.

Diagram shows the position of the brain

Uses of the brain.

  1. For thinking.
  2. For recall / remembering.
  3. For body balance.
  4. For storing information.
  5. For learning / reasoning.

Diseases of the brain.

Epilepsy, cerebral malaria, meningitis etc.

Care for the brain

  1. Having enough rest.
  2. Avoid drugs like alcohol, marijuana, tobacco.
  3. Eat a balanced diet.
  4. Having physical exercises to refresh the brain daily.
  5. Early treatment of malaria.
