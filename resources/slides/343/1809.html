The Nose.

            -   It is located at the front of the face.

            -    It has two nostrils used for taking air into and out of the body (lungs).

            -    The nose is the sense organ for smelling.

Front view of the nose

The nose has hairs (cilia) and that traps any foreign bodies like dust, dirt. 

OR

Cilia filters air before it goes to the lungs.

Diseases of the nose.

  • Influenza (flue).
  • Disorders – having a foreign object in the nose.
  •  Nose bleeding.

Care of the nose.

  • Regular cleaning.
  • Covering the nose in dusty.
